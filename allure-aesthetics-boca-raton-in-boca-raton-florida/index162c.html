It looks like you'd benefit from additional treatments of hyaluronidase to further even out your lips. It is sometimes necessary to dissolve asymmetrical filler more than once to get desired results. Assuming your after pictures were taken after swelling had resolved, your left side seems to have more volume and may have developed some post-injection nodules. I recommend following up with your injector and treating with hyaluronidase before adding more filler.
