Is it okay to use baby oil during Laser Hair Removal treatment?
Laser Hair Removal
Jan 12, 2022
Most laser hair removal devices require some kind of “glide” product to be applied to the skin for the laser to safely fire through. There is a common product used for this called Lux Lotion – it’s a skin protectant and feels exactly like baby oil.
Is it true that tear trough filler can lead to under eye bags?
Tear trough filler
Jan 12, 2022
With any hyaluronic acid filler, there is a risk of product migration. If tear trough filler migrates away from the location where it is injected, then there’s a risk of it giving the appearance of puffiness below the skin in areas where it is undesirable.
