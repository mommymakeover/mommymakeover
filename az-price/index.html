Mommy Package Plastic Surgery Arizona Price

“Mommy makeovers” in Arizona or post-maternity breast and body reshaping are individualized for each patient, but procedures performed include tummy tuck, liposuction, breast augmentation, breast lift and breast reduction.

No two patients are the same, so the mommy makeover combines the best of these procedures to restore a youthful, pre-pregnancy body.

The length of mommy tuck surgery depends specifically on the procedures performed, but typically lasts from 4-6 hours.

In most cases, all of the needed procedures can be performed in one stage. Recovery varies from patient to patient, but in general, you should anticipate missing 1-2 weeks of work and waiting 6-8 weeks before resuming a normal exercise regimen.

Mommy makeover and time of surgery

No two mommy makeover procedures are identical, so the simple answer is it depends on how much needs to be done. A sensible upper limit in my opinion is 6 hours.

The procedures involved in a mommy makeover in Arizona typically include a tummy tuck and breast implants +/- a lift, and maybe some lipo.

If you need more than that then staging it might make sense, but this is not often the case.

However, there is good data to support the safety of the combination surgeries if the length of the surgery isn’t too long.

What You can Do to Prepare for a Mommy Makeover Surgery

Dr John J. Corey, MD Mommy Plastic Surgery Package Woman

The best thing you can do going into a mommy package plastic surgery is to be as healthy as possible. So, first and foremost, eat well, continue to exercise, minimize stress, stay hydrated and get plenty rest.

Avoid anything that will tax your body, this includes excessive dieting. Some medications, such as blood thinners, should be avoided prior to surgery.

If you are taking medication and/or supplements, tell your surgeon so he or she can provide directions. Be sure to stock up on food, prescriptions, and other supplies before you go into surgery so that you can make the most of your recovery.

Mommy makeover preparation

Your surgeon obviously feels you are a candidate for this procedure if you have already set it up. You should maintain a healthy balanced diet, avoid nicotine, avoid aspirin type medications and other herbal supplements.

You should also make sure you have the appropriate amount of help at home to allow you to recover without needing to over-exert yourself.

One surgery better than 3 for mommy makeover
There are different schools of though on this, but for a healthy patient I see no reason whay the 3 procedures shouldn’t be done together. The benefits are obvious: lower cost and one recovery time. It would typically take no longer than 5 hours of mom plastic surgery package to do this, which is reasonable.

The main disadvantage is that when a breast lift and implants are done together, there are a lot of things that have to heal harmoniously for a good long-term result, and so revisions are not uncommon. However, most of these are minor and can often be done under local anesthesia, which is better than planning 2 breast operations in the first place which is sort of like a 100% revision rate.

Recovery after a Mommy Makeover

The recovery time from a Mommy Makeover is limited by the Tummy Tuck. You would be fine to be back at your job two weeks after the breast augmentation (although you may be a bit sore with a lot of scrubbing activities until 3 weeks have passed).

From the Tummy Tuck, I do not allow any cleaning activities for 2 weeks, and then light household work for short periods of time would be OK.

I restrict my patients from heavy lifting (15lbs) until 6 weeks have passed. A professional cleaning job would likely be too strenuous to even consider until 4 weeks have passed due to the continuous amount of heavy activities. Plan for a month off work.

Recovery time are estimates we give to patients. For your surgeries I usually recommend 2 to 3 weeks before returning to that type of work.

Weight Gain after Tummy Tuck and Breast Augmentation

Dr. Josh Olson Liposuction And Tummy Tuck AZ Results

Your weight gain is most likely due to swelling that occurs after surgery. During surgery you are also given extra fluids through your IV which can remain weeks after surgery. We tell our patients not to weight themselves until at least 1 month post-op.

Even then only 66% of the swelling is gone. At 3 months you should see the true long-lasting results of post baby plastic surgery. We also recommend all of our patients wear a compressive post-surgical garment to help reduce the swelling. Don’t get discouraged!

You are a free woman one month after a mommy makeover in Arizona

I generally advise my patients to aim for 100% activity levels one month after this type of mommy makeup. So, for work or exercise, cut back by the Rule of 4s. During the first 2 weeks after mother makeover, increase your activity level by 2/4 or 50%, at 3 weeks by 3/4 or 75%. By 4 weeks, you are a free woman. Always use common sense, if it hurts, dont do it.
