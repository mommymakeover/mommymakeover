Smoking and Mommy Makeover Surgery

I normally suggest that patients be nicotine free for 4-6 weeks prior to tummy tuck surgery. The longer the better. Ideally, you will stop smoking to have surgery and go through the recovery process and then hopefully NOT go back to smoking afterwards.

Restoring Your Pre-pregnancy Body

Smoking is a definite problem for a mommy makeover

The breast surgery is not a big concern, however smoking and tummy tuck, a common part of a mommy make over is asking for trouble. We would suggest three weeks off, but the longer the better.

Mommy Makeover before or after pregnancy
