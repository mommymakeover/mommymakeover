What is Vivace? Vivace is an FDA-cleared treatment that combines the latest generation of microneedling technology with radiofrequency (RF), a proven non-invasive skin tightening method, and LED light therapy! Designed with patient comfort and safety in mind, Vivace is virtually painless, it is safe for any skin type, and there’s little to no downtime! Like all microneedling treatments, Vivace triggers your skin’s natural healing process by creating microscopic openings in the skin with tiny needles. Your skin builds new collagen and elastin and increases cell turnover in response to these “microinjuries,” resulting in a smoother, more even complexion within weeks. The Vivace treatment is the first to enhance skin tightening with Radio Frequency during your treatment, and it incorporates LED technology to calm redness and gives collagen production an extra boost!
