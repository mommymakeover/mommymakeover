Mommy Makeover in Colorado Springs and a breast lift without implants

A mommy makeover is a generic term to describe surgical procedures that can positively address changes that occurred as the result of pregnancy? Typically it includes some sort of abdominal procedure (full abdominoplasty or mini tummy tuck) and a breast procedure (breast augmentation, breast lift with or without breast implants or even a breast reduction).

Liposuction and other procedures can be part of this as well. In order to determine what is best for you (photos would be more helpful here), you should consult with one or more board certified plastic surgeons.

There is no specific combination of operations that makes up a mommy makeover.

I think the colloquial term mommy makeover is silly. Nevertheless, it’s commonly used in refers to a combination of operations used to remedy some of the aesthetic problems created by pregnancy. Almost always it includes an abdominoplasty and breast surgery.

Colorado Springs Mommy Makeover – Breast Lift or Augmentation

A good rule of thumb as to whether you’ll best benefit from a breast lift or a breast augmentation is to look in the mirror with your arms by your sides. If the nipple is at or above the crease below your breast, then an augmentation will likely give you a good result. If the nipple is below the breast crease, then a lift is usually needed.
