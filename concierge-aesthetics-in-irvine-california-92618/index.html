Stacy Vencill, PA-C, MPH

Name: Stacy Vencill, PA-C, MPH
Position: Physician Assistant
Years experience: 14
Biography: Stacy is a graduate from the George Washington University Physician Assistant Program, in Washington DC. After working for 6 years in multiple medical spas and plastic surgeon offices, Stacy refined her injection skills, techniques and discovered she really enjoyed the art of aesthetics and getting to know her patients, ensuring that they’re always completely satisfied with their treatments. Unfortunately, she realized that her priority and focus on patient care was not shared with her previous employers.In 2009, she decided to start her own practice and she founded Concierge Aesthetics and expanded her treatment offerings to also include Medical Aestheticians and Laser Treatments. Stacy has developed a passion for injectables over the last few years which has driven her to complete advanced training in BOTOX and dermal. In addition, she is a National trainer for Allergen, Bellafill and Suneva, assisting and teaching other practitioners.
