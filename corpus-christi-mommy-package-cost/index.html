Liposuction and Abdominoplasty Can Be Done Concurrently

It is often very important to perform liposuction in conjunction with your abdominoplasty, particularly on the love handles, or flanks, in order to achieve adequte circumferential contouring. I probably perform this combination on 80-90% of my abdominoplasty patients to achieve a better look.

Liposuction on other areas at the same time can also be done safely at the same time as a tummy tuck, but there can be some potential increased risks with adding additional surgery, including potential blood clots.

Liposuction on the abdominal skin at the same time as a tummy tuck is generally not recommended, as this can cause problems with the blood supply to the skin, and lead to wound healing problems. (Samuel Sohn, MD, Las Vegas Plastic Surgeon)

Liposuction + Tummy Tuck is Routine

How to optimize the body shape with Tummy Tuck surgery:

Step 1 – Tummy Tuck Tummy Tuck surgery removes excess skin and fat from the lower abdomen. And it tightens the muscle wall of the abdomen and powerfully re-supports any looseness of the supporting tissues below the skin.

Step 1b – Cut out the deep fat layer – safer than liposuction of the abdomen Fat in the tummy area is divided into superficial (below the skin) and deep layers by a thin layer of tissue. By cutting out all of the deep fat from the area that becomes the fat layer of the lower part of the abdomen, I am able to more safely and thoroughly improve the contour here than I can achieve with liposuction.

Step 1c – Quilt down the tissues in the areas that have been lifted up from the abdominal wall I use several sutures underneath the “flap” of tissue that is raised and moved in a tummy tuck. This helps speed the healing process and it shares the forces of the deep layer closure over an area much larger than just the incision alone.

Step 2 – Liposuction of the hips and lower back The only time I do not perform liposuction when I perform a Tummy Tuck is in very thin patients that also happen to be blessed with a thin waistline. Otherwise, I routinely perform liposuction of the hips and lower back to optimally rejuvenate and improve the overall figure of the lower trunk.

Step 3 – Liposuction and rejuvenation of the pubic area Often I perform liposuction of the pubic area in both men and women and as needed raise or lift this area up. For women, liposuction can reduce prominent fullness in the labia area as well This is my most common approach to achieving optimal re-contouring with a Tummy Tuck. (Nick Slenkovich, MD, Denver Plastic Surgeon)
