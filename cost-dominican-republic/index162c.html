Fluid Retention After Mommy Makeover

After a Mommy Makeover with tummy tuck, correction of rectus diastasis, and breast augmentation, you have had so much disruption of tissue that your body is retaining fluid.

Weight Gain after Tummy Tuck and Breast Augmentation?

The weight gain that you have experienced after “mommy makeover” surgery is to be expected.

This wight gain is related to fluid retention that has occurred around the time of the procedure.
