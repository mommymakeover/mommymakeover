How much is a mommy makeover surgery

As with any procedure in plastic surgery, the price for a mommy makeover will vary widely from surgeon to surgeon. There are many factors that go into the price estimate you will receive for a mommy makeover in Arlington, TX, including the:

  • Procedures Involved
  • Surgeon’s Fee
  • Anesthesia/facility Fees
  • Follow Up / Consultation Costs
  1. A mommy makeover in Arlington, TX can include a range of procedures, dependent upon what parts of your body are your concern. For most women, these include the breast and tummy. Common procedures can include a tummy tuck, liposuction, breast augmentation, breast lift, or other adjunct procedures such as fat transfer. Each of these procedures have individualized needs and costs that you will discuss with your surgeon.

  2. Your surgeon’s fee will vary based on your location and which surgeon you see. However, this is often related to the time spent on your procedure. This fee will change based on the procedures you decide suite your needs.
  3. The facility fees will differ based on your location and the type of facility where your surgeon operates, as each facility charges differently. It is best to ask your surgeon where he/she operates in your consultation.
  4. Verify before your procedure whether or not your surgeon charges for follow-up appointments. You will most likely see your surgeon for multiple follow-ups after your procedure. Lipoplasty and Tummy Tuck

I routinely perform some limited liposuction at the time of abdominoplasty, but I feel that aggressive suctioning of the upper abdomen is safer to perform later as a secondary procedure.

A tummy tuck involves division of several of the main blood vessels to the abdomen.

The blood supply must then come from other vessels, including those of the upper abdomen and chest which can be injured with aggressive liposuction. (John Whitt, MD (retired), Houston Plastic Surgeon)
