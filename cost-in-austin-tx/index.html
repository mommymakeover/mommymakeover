I cannot fault your surgeon for being cautious. He has examined you and knows your full medical history and we do not. But – in many cases we commonly do this set of procedures in one sitting without major complications.

Mommy Makeover Preparation

To prepare your body for mommy lift plastic surgery, make sure you are eating healthy nutritous meals and have a good workout routine (do the heavy cardio/weights now, as you will have to wait several weeks after surgery before resuming).

Since you will be unable to pick up your young children for a few weeks, I advise my patients to have childcare set up in advance. Furthermore, for the first few days, you may also need help for yourself at home.

To help speed your recovery, take it easy those first few weeks, as you must remember that you just had major surgery. Wishing you the best of luck with your new body!

Mommy makeovers is often a single stage procedure

The concept of the ‘Mommy Makeover In Austin TX ‘ is usually based on breast and abdominal changes.

Breast Lift In Austin, TX Before And After Images

This involves some form of a tummy tuck and breast reshaping. Most commonly, there are done together no matter what variations of the operations are needed.

The time required to do all of them is not excessive for most patients who are healthy. The only compelling reason to separate the breast and abdominal procedures would be for an immediate cost issue where one may not be able to economically do both at the same time right now.

Drains after a tummy tuck

Drains stay in until they are draining minimally so the risk of seroma is as low as possible. I ask that the daily output be under 25 cc and this usually takes 7-10 days to get there.

Dr Rocco C Piazza, MD Mommy Makeover Plastic Surgery

We take the drains out when they stop making large amounts of fluid.

Typically, it takes 7-10 days for this to happen, and the drainage to decrease to 25-30 cc each per 24 hour time period. People who have lipo at the same time tend to make more fluid for longer.

I do not use drains for breast lifts or breast augmentation. I use drains for the tummy tucks. They usually stay for about 3-7 days.

When the drains can be removed varies greatly. It is based on how much fluid is produced by the individual during the healing process.

It can be as variable as between 3-14 days. Overdoing it after mummy makeover in Austin can cause more fluid to form and then having to leave the drains in longer.

Interval between abdominoplasty and mastopexy augmentation

Dr Jennifer L Walden, MD Mommy Makeover Recovery Time Photos

Many plastic surgeons perform abdominal and breast surgery concomitantly. That being said, staging can improve safety if the time on the operating table is going to be significant.

Retrospective studies in many different surgical specialties have clearly demonstrated a direct correlation between time and complication rates.

Whether to do these procedures in stages or together depends on your age, health history, duration of procedure, etc.

If you and your surgeon decide to stage things, I would consider waiting 3-6 months to allow your body to fully heal particularly after abdominal surgery.

This will likely depend on how you recover/heal from your TT. Assuming all goes well from the first procedure, performing the second procedure 6 weeks later shouldn’t be a problem.

With that said, performed them together would put you under anesthesia only once instead of twice and you would only have one recovery period to heal from. something to think about
