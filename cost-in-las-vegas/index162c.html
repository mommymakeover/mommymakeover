The term “mommy makeover” in Las Vegas can really refer to many different procedures usually encompassing something to be done on the abdomen and the breasts. The time in surgery really depends upon the type of procedures being performed. As plastic surgeons we used to simply list procedures.

In response to so many questions from patients, we realized that women often seek a comprehensive approach to correction of post-pregnancy changes in their bodies. Someone thought up the cute term “mommy makeover.”

It doesn’t have a specific meaning, but usually includes doing something for your breasts and torso (tummy & flanks or hips). It may also include thighs, chin, or other areas. Most commonly patients have breast augmentation (with or without breast lift / mastopexy) and tummy tuck or tummy liposuction. During our consultation, we provide each patient a customized evaluation and detailed written information about each and every procedure.

We discuss the recovery carefully well before the procedure(s). Doing it in stages is not the most common way, but could apply depending on individual circumstances or additional procedures. We want you to have the best result safely.
