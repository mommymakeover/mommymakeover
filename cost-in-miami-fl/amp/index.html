A Mommy Makeover in Miami FL is any procedure or combination of procedures that will help get your post-baby body back in pre-baby shape. In our practice, we encourage a woman to wait approximately 6 months before performing any sugeries post-baby.

This help your body heal from pregnancy and delivery. It is also encouraged to begin daily exercise to increase the level of oxygen in your blood stream which helps with wound healing.

It will also help with your mental capacity in the postoperative period as you experience discomfort and swelling.

Exercise may also obviate the need for liposuction making your combination procedure more affordable and safer.

If you are a nursing mother, it is best to wait at least 3 months after nursing to consider augmentation.

If you plan to have additional children, it is always best to wait until after delivery of the last child to have a tummy tuck as you will receive maximal benefit from the procedure. Aside from the above suggestions, a great time for mommy makeover surgery is when you are able to “baby yourself” a little.

Take a day or two after mommy makeover to relax without the high stress and demands put on a mother.

It’s best to see if a spouse or other relative is able to help with the daily chores with children so that you can rest as you need to at first and not worry so much about anyone but yourself.

An investment in yourself in the days right after mommy makeover surgery may go a long in your longterm recovery and results. (Eric A. Marchant, MD, FACS, Philadelphia Plastic Surgeon)

Plastic Surgery in One Session or Multiple?
It is quite common to combine multiple procedures, but not all procedures so well together or are safe so rely on your surgeon to inform you regarding safety. It will depend on your state of health, what the specific procedures are, how long they will take, the medications you are on and to some extent your weight.
