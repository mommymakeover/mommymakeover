Cost Of Mommy Lift In Michigan

Mommy makeover surgery in Michigan address the breast and abdominal area. The recommendation by the American Society of Plastic Surgeons is to limit out-patient anesthesia time for elective surgery to six hours or less.

In most cases, a mommy makeover surgery can be performed in this time frame.

If your surgeon feels he/she cannot complete the surgery in six hours, then it is best to stage the surgeries for safety reasons.

The length of mommy plastic surgery package depends on several factors such as: Amount of liposuction Mini or full tummy tuck, Short or extended Muscle repair Breast implants Type of breast lift etc.

Commonly these take a minimum of 3 hours and up to 8 hours in more complex cases.

Mommy Makeover preparation

The best way to prepare for mommy make over in Michigan is to make sure that you are healthy, and have realistic expectation of your outcome.

Additionally, make sure that you have the support you need for the procedure.

Besides the financial support, you need the social or psychological support at home. When all the above has been satisfied, go and visit with a board certified plastic surgeon. Do not look for a free consult. You get what you pay for.

Most experienced plastic surgeons charge a fee for a consultation.

This is a lot of surgery, so be sure that you are in good enough health to get through it safely. Next, you should always rely on your doctors guidance. Finally, as a rule, you should have no restrictions after 6 weeks, but your healing may be slower, and you MUST be cleared by the doctor first.

Weight gain after mommy lift

Dr. Gowda Mommy Lift Photo

The weight gain you are experiencing is intravenous fluid given to you during and after your mommy package plastic surgery. You will notice this is temporary and your weight will be less than when you started. It takes days so be patient.

Without a physical examination it is difficult to provide you with a definitive answer for your weight gain. However, your weight gain is more than likely due to excess fluid in your body that can occur after mom plastic surgery package.

During mommy tuck you are given extra fluids through your IV which can remain weeks after mom plastic surgery. I require my patients to wear compression garments after having a tummy tuck. The garment is designed to help resolve swelling in the abdomen. Be sure that you are carefully moving around and elevating your legs when you are sitting. Do not over exert yourself and only do the activities your surgeon has released you to do.


Too much physical activity too soon could increase your risk of post operative complications. You should also be drinking plenty of water and avoiding salty foods to help flush the extra fluid from your body. There’s no way to tell how soon your body will lose the extra fluid and subsequent weight but if you feel like your swelling is getting worse or becomes painful contact your surgeon immediately.
