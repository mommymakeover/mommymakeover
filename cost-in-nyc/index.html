Return to work after Mommy Makeover

Douglas S. Steinbrech, MD, FACS mommy makeover – Tummy Tuck And Breast Augmentation

Tummy tucks and breast augmentation surgeries usually require at least 4 to 6 weeks recovery before the patient resumes vigorous exercise or intense activity, such as housekeeping. This amount of time may vary slightly but is necessary to ensure there are no complications after surgery. Liposuction alone usually allows return to full activity 2 weeks after the procedure. (Olivia Hutchinson, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)

Mommies should never do housework

Believe it or not this is one of her most common questions. After a mommy makeover in New York, the patient should be careful in lifting heavy objects if a tummy tuck has been performed. We typically tell patients that they may return to exercise such as walking one and a half to two weeks after their surgery. However patients should refrain from returning to the gym or lifting anything more than 10 to 15 pounds for at least 4 to 6 weeks. This will ensure that your tummy will heal as well as possible (Pat Pazmino, MD, FACS, Miami Plastic Surgeon)

Weight Gain after surgery

Dr. Michael A Fiorillo, MD Mommy makeover

Weight gain two weeks after TT or breast surgery is only retained water. You likely received several liters of IV fluid during your surgery, and the swelling after surgery is additional retained water. So the 10 lbs extra that you’re seeing is merely fluid retention. Without one week of surgery, a good portion of that fluid retention should have resolved itself. The remainder should slowly resolve over the next 3-6 months.
