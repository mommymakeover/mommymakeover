Mommy Tuck In Seattle WA

Mommy Makeover, for the “pre”pregnancy body

The “Mommy Makeover” in Seattle WA is a combination of procedures.

The intent is to get a woman’s body back to her “pre” pregnancy shape and many times make even further improvements.

The combination is usually where the breasts are lifted with or without an implant, although in some cases a lift might not be necessary, just an augmentation.

Also, a full tummy tuck with a possible waist enhancement.

Many women choose to have some liposuction done at this time around their back and possibly inner and outer thighs.

When you have a consultation with your plastic surgeon you will be discussing what areas you want corrected and you will find out what your options are.

The length of time in surgery will depend on the combination of procedures. It is not unusual for the surgery to take up to 8 or 9 hours.

Most surgeons refer to a “mommy makeover” as a series of cosmetic surgery procedures intended to restore a woman’s figure after pregnancy.

I perform these procedures as part of a “mommy makeover” for patients who want an improved figure after having children.

Pregnancy, childbirth, and breastfeeding can take a serious toll on a woman’s body, and many moms find that no amount of diet and exercise can restore their pre-baby figures.

Mommy Make Over In Washington Is Performed For Women Post-partum

Procedures commonly performed during mommy makeovers include:
  • Abominoplasty (tummy tuck)
  • Breast Augmentation +/- Lift, or
  • Breast Lift only without implants, liposuction

These procedures can be performed in one stage or multiple stages depending on the patient’s goals, health status and other screening criteria.

After pregnancy, breasts can lose their youthful position and shape, the tummy can become stretched and lose its tone, and extra fat may get deposited in areas that are not so flattering.

The plastic surgery procedures above are intended to correct those conditions and help restore your pre-pregnancy figure. (C. Bob Basu, MD, FACS, Houston Plastic Surgeon)
