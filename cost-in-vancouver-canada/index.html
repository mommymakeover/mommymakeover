Mommy Body Makeover In Vancouver, Canada

A mommy makeover in Vancouver can refer to a variety of things but certainly is not confined to a woman who has had children

Simply, a mommy makeover usually refers to changing the shape or size of the breasts and tummy in coordination. This could be a breast reduction, augmentation, lift or lift with augmentation combined with a tummy tuck, liposuction or tummy tuck with liposuction

It does not refer to any one specific grouping other than doing surgery on the breasts and tummy at the same time. It could also be combined with other procedures

Age is less important than general health. If you are otherwise healthy, there is no reason why you cannot undergo the procedures commonly associated with a “Mommie Makeover”, including tummy tuck, breast lift and/or augmentation, liposuction, etc

Your health status is much more important than your calendar age but age does play a role in recovery. See a very experienced and board certified surgeon for an evaluation to determine what it will take to get you to your goal

It may be that doing the mommy makeover in Vancouver in two stages rather than all at once would be a better choice for you.

There is no age limit on any elective cosmetic surgery. I have done face lifts and other cosmetic surgery on healthy people in their 80s. The issues are your health -If you are a normal weight with no risk factors, a mommy make-over is a great idea

Mommy Makeover and Smoking

This is something to discuss with your surgeon but smoking can definitely affect how you heal. You will find different surgeons have different opinions about this. I require my patients to quit smoking for 2 weeks prior surgery and 2 weeks after mommy tuck

How long should I stop smoking before tummy tuck.

Smoking is a risk factor for all surgery, though with tummy tuck the risk is a very real one. Smoking will affect your abilty to heal and might cause the incision to break down and can even cause skin loss

Stop as early as you can, several weeks is best, and of course no smoking after until you are well on your way to a safe recovery.
