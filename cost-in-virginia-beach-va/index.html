Cosmetic Surgery After Pregnancy In Virginia Beach, VA

The term “mommy makeover” in Virginia Beach, VA was coined to address the trauma to the body brought on by pregnancy and the surgery to correct this trauma.

Pregnancy may result in loss of volume in the breasts, sagging of the breasts, loose skin and stretch marks on the abdomen, and increased fat in the hips and thighs.

Surgery to correct these issues include breast lift and/or breast augmentation, tummy tuck, and liposuction.

These are all procedures that are frequently done individually. When done collectively, however, we usually refer to them as a mommy makeover.

Hospital stay after long plastic surgery

There are a number of factors that decide whether a patient should stay in the hospital after mommy surgery. the length of the operation is an important one. In surgeries lasting more than 6 hours the risk of complications increases significantly, these risks include leg clots and pulmonary embolus.

We recommed a one to 2 days hospital stay after long surgeries to be able to place the patient in compression boots to prevent those clots.
