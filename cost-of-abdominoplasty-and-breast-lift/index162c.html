Abdominoplasty And Breast Lift Cost

The cost will vary but I would expect for a abdominoplasty and a breast lift that you should expect to pay between $10-12K give or take. Costs include surgeon, anaesthetic, and facility fees.

Ask to see if followups are included and any garments that are needed. I would strongly suggest that you choose the surgeon not so much on the price but based on the trust you have that they will give you an amazing result, the rapport you build with them, and the confidence that they will be just as happy to see and support you after surgery as they are before. (Rodger Shortt, FRCSC, Toronto Plastic Surgeon)

When a breast lift and a abdominoplasty are combined together we typically refer to this is as a Mommy make over.

The term Mommy make over refers to a surgical procedure in which both the breast and the abdomen are operated on to correct changes that happened as a result of being a mom.

In essence correcting changes from the collarbone down through the knees. To correct the changes in the breast to give them a more aesthetic appearance, surgeries would be designed to lift and/or enlarge.

The breast procedures could include; a breast reduction, breast augmentation, ,a breast lift , or a breast lift with implants. The surgeries to improve the abdominal contour could include liposuction alone, a abdominoplasty with or without liposuction, a lipo-abdominoplasty, or a mini-tummy.

The ultimate cost of a Mommy make over will vary depending on which of these surgical procedures are combined together. The total cost not only is dependent on which procedures you choose, but will include charges for the facility, anesthesia and the surgeon’s fee.
