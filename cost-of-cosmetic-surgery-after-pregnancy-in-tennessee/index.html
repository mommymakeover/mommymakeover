Cost Of Cosmetic Surgery After Pregnancy In Tennessee

A Mommy Makeover in Tennessee is a chance to improve breasts and belly at the same time. If you are healthy and therefore a low risk for anesthesia, combining surgery on the breasts and tummy is a great way to improve everything with only one surgery and one recovery period.

The best surgical plan is one designed in person with your surgeon, just make sure he or she is appropriately trained and certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery

Mommy Makeover – Is it for you?

It sounds like you would be a good candidate for a mommy makeover procedure. This usually involves lifting the breasts and adding volume with an implant, and a simultaneous tummy tuck giving you a tighter, flatter abdomen.

You are able to correct all of the post-pregnancy changes in one procedure. However, being at your consistent and “ideal” weight is very important for a successful outcome. You can only get a definitive answer after a consultation with a board certified surgeon in your area.

Am I a candidate for a mini tummy tuck

I think any reputable plastic surgeon wants to do the safest surgery they can on their patients. They also want to do no more surgery than is necessary. That being said, the word “mini” in plastic surgery is most likely the most misrepresented and most misunderstood description we have in plastic surgery.

I have met patients who desire “mini” recovery and “mini” cost, but I have yet to meet one who desired a “mini” result! It is understandable and human nature for someone to want a great result with little to no scar or downtime but this is in reality rarely possible.

Dr J. Jason Wendel Endoscopic Tummy Tuck

I believe this term “mini” is even misused sometimes by surgeons for marketing purposes so be careful. So “mini” is ill defined and there is not standard definition. I assume you want a small scar, but I assume you also want the laxity and excess skin removed from your lower abdomen.

Your photos show that when you sit down you have laxity that spreads across your entire abdomen and all the way up to your belly button.

To remove this excess skin would not be possible under any definition of mini in my opinion. Furthermore, the term min also can mean that you do limited to no muscle tightening. In your case with 5 children, your muscles also appear to be lax by photographs.

If you tightened your muscle only in the lower abdomen that causes bulging in the upper abdomen. You should to no tightening and live with abdominal bulge or tighten the entire abdomen.

Dr Shayne Webb Mommy Lift

If you let your surgeon do the proper mommy surgery of a full abdominoplasty and breast augmentation you are truly an exceptional candidate. You are to be congratulated on keeping a great figure despite 5 pregnancies. You have kept excess weight off and truly should be thankful that your breast shape has remained in this good of shape. You should expect an outstanding result.

Tummy tuck and Breast augmentation

It is very common to get Tummy tuck and breast surgery (augmentation or augmentation/lift) at the same time. It seems like you are healthy; so 5-6 hour mommy lift should not be physically stressful to your body.

As long as your plastic surgeon believes you to be a good candidate (physically and cosmetically), you should be good to go.

There are definite advantages to having combo surgery: one recovery time and cheaper overall OR and anesthesia fee. In terms of exercising, you will need to wait about 6 weeks prior to resuming to your full exercise routine.

Dr. Stephen M. Becker Plastic Surgery In Breast

Combinations for mommie makeovers

The combinations that can be done are vast for a mommie makeover. This combination is typical. You should wait about 6 weeks after these surgeries prior to progressively resuming your exercise regime.

Low tummy tuck scar

Tummy tuck scars can be placed quite low in patients with enough stretch to their skin. If there is a particular style of swimsuit you like, wear it to your consultation with your surgeon.

Fees vary considerably from surgeon to surgeon and based upon your location. Best to schedule several consultations to compare fees and people – choose a surgeon who makes you comfortable and has the same vision for your result.

Mommy Makeover in a different location.

In general, it is difficult to undergo mommy tuck in one location and have follow up for your recovery somewhere else. This would be especially true if you are traveling to a different country.

Many Mothers Aren’t Satisfied With The Changes In Their Body After Childbirth

Plastic Surgeons and doctors in general would be reluctant to take care of another doctor’s patients, particularly if there were problems or complications.

In addition, there would be extra fees incurred if you were having a different doctor manage your post op care, such as dressing changes, suture removal or drain management.

This may obviate the benefit of cost savings to travel to do mommy tuck in a different country.

As others have pointed out, traveling to South America to save money may sound like a great idea…

Until you have a major complication without a physician to care for you. Keep in mind that you may have problems well after 4 weeks and you may require a revision in the future.

Mommy Lift Cosmetic Surgery By Doctor David B. Reath

I think you will find it very difficult to find someone to care for you in the postop period here in the States. Best to save your money and find a board certified surgeon in your area.

This is the major issue with abroad surgery. Difficulty in followup afterwards. Most patients go for mommy makeover abroad due to cheaper costs. I would highly recommend saving money over time and postponing surgery until you can afford it in the states and have good followup.

Would I be a good candidate for a mini tummy tuck and breast augmentation with no lift?

You are not a good candidate for a mini-tummy tuck. You will achieve the much better results if you undergo a full abdominoplasty or tummy tuck. On the other hand, you should be able to undergo a breast augmentation without a breast lift.
