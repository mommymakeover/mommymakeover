I would discuss this with your primary care physician and your plastic surgeon in detail to determine if the procedures need to be staged to different days or perhaps done in a hospital setting with an overnight stay for observation. Mommy Makeover is safer when you have stopped Smoking

Ideally, smoking should be stopped 3-4 weeks prior to Mommy Makeover surgery in Baltimore MD. The risk of healing problems makes it absolutely necessary to not smoke during the weeks after your surgery as well.
