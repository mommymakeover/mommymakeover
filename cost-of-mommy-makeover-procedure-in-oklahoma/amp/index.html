Cost Of Mommy Makeover Procedure In Oklahoma

Mommy makeover in Oklahoma includes a breast procedure and a tummy procedure

Usually a mommy makeover in OK consists of a breast procedure (implants and/or breast lift) combined with an abdominal procedure (tummy tuck and/or liposuction). Which procedure(s) varies from patient to patient depending on their specific anatomy and desires.

The best time to undergo these types of surgeries is after you have completed all your pregnancies. Consult with a board certified plastic surgeon to see which type of mommy lift is best suited for you.

Tummy tuck with Breast Implants

You do seem like a good candidate for a tummy tuck with breast implants. A special kind of tummy tuck I perform is called a Lateral Tension Abdominoplasty which will remove lose skin and reattach the muscles that can be separated during pregnancy.

You might be a candidate for a TABA (transabdominal breast augmentation) procedure which is where we place the implants through the tummy tuck incision and can avoid scars on your breasts. You will need to have an in person consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon for precise recommendations.

Breast lift and tummy tuck. Can they be done at the same time?

Yes the combination of TT with Breast Lift with or without implants acceptable for office surgery. Best to address ALL your questions to the doctors in Oklahoma you are evaluating.

Combining procedures

A Mommy Makeover Include Breast Augmentation, Breast Lift, Tummy Tuck, Butt Lift, Liposuction And Occasionally Breast Reduction

The procedures that you are interested in are commonly done in one mommy surgery. The choice of whether or not to have an implant has more to do with what look you desire for your breasts. You appear to have adequate tissue to do a lift with no implant.

If you want more volume, an implant can be added. Implants don’t have a certain longevity. If there are no problems with the implants, there is no specified time in which they need to be removed/exchanged.

Staging a mommy makeover, breast first or tummy tuck

There is no right or wrong answer so do the procedure that you want to do the most first. If either one would be fine for you I would do the tummy tuck first since there is a small amount of skin from the fold of the breasts that is pulled down with the tummy tuck and this will allow more accurate incision placement when you get your breasts done.

How A Mommy Makeover Works

I usually recommend tummy first as it is easier to use bras to enhance your breast shape (eg. push up bras). Of course your preference of what bothers you the most is of primary importance. Do what you want first.

Tummy tuck or breast augmentation order

Do the one first that bothers you most. That being said:

  1. If you can do both procedures simultaneously you will save yourself a significant amount of recovery time. It will most likely also save you on the overall cost for both procedures.
  2. Most patients find that they need more time to recover from the tummy tuck than from the breast augmentation.

Multiple procedures and extensive liposuction

Restore Your Body To Its Original Pre-pregnancy State

Safety should be your main concern as well as that of your surgeon. Safety involves many aspects and surgeries can have a variety of complications. My main caution is the total surface area that you are planning to liposuction.

While you appear to have a lot of areas to remove fat, the question is where are you and your surgeon going to stop. Suctioning the entire body is not safe. Also the volume removed needs to be limited and the question is whether that limited volume will sufficiently impact your shape without undue risk. Be cautious and get a few opinions from experienced board-certified plastic surgeons.

Breast Lift and Tummy Tuck

You seem to be a good candidate for a tummy tuck with a breast lift. If you choose to only use an implant, you most likely will not be happy with the results as they do not provide any kind of lift.

All of these procedures can be done at the same time to optimize recovery. You will need to have a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon who has expertise in these areas.

Oklahoma Mommy makeover: combining breast surgery and tummy tuck

Once you are at your ideal weight a mommy makeover sounds like a good procedure for you. Provided you are in good health these procedures (breast lift +/- implants) and a tummy tuck can be done on the same day as long as they are completed in a reasonable amount of time (less than 5 hours).

You should obtain medical clearance from your primary care physician prior to mommy tuck and can consider staying in an after care facility the night of mommy lift for observation. Consult with a board certified plastic surgeon to determine if you are a good candidate for this type of mommy lift.

Recovery after mommy makeover

After a mommy makeover in OK (usually combines breast and body contouring there is a considerable amount of recovery and I typically tell my patients that they will need 2 weeks off work and will still have activity restrictions for a full month after OK mommy makeover and sometimes longer depending upon the procedures performed.

You should expect to be asked to walk multiple times per day to keep your blood circulating but avoid deliberate exercise to elevate heart rate for about 4 weeks. Your surgeon will give you specific guidelines and it is best to follow them as best you can.
