Mommy Makeover: Customized and Individualized

A “mommy makeover in Philadelphia, PA” is essentially an umbrella term used for patients who want any type of combination of cosmetic surgery performed in a single sitting.

The details of each mommy makeover are personalized on a patient-by-patient basis and are determined with your surgeon.

What you choose to operate on essentially depends on what areas of your body you would like to rejuvenate.

Still, the most common procedures that moms tend to benefit from are tummy tucks, liposuction around the body, and breast lifts and/or augmentations.

Board Certification By The ABPS Makes A Difference

The most rigid and thorough certification process (in my opinion) is through boards recognized by The American Board of Medical Specialties (ABMS). Doctors with board certification in these specialties include your family doctors, pediatricians, orthopedists and psychiatrists.

Plastic surgeons who have American Board of Plastic Surgery (ABPS) certification are the ONLY plastic/cosmetic surgeons recognized by the ABMS to perform breast and body cosmetic surgery.
