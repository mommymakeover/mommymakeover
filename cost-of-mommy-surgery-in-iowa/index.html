Cost Of Mommy Surgery In Iowa

A mommy makeover in Iowa is a popular term for any combination of breast and body surgeries that restores a woman back to what I call her pre-baby body

This may include breast augmentation breast lift breast reduction, abdominoplasty, fat grafting in many different combinations

The concept is that pregnancy changes a woman’s body forever. It is not simply a matter of losing weight going to the gym and working out

There are issues with lost breast tissue, too much breast tissue, too much skin stretching and sagging, stretch marks, Abdominal muscle separation.

A mommy makeover is a personalized surgical solution for each individual woman’s problem

Very few women can have multiple pregnancies and still have their bodies look great. This is not the norm. Most women, after one or more pregnancies, have a stretched out abdomen with a separation of the abdominal musculature.

They have excess fat on their abdomen which doesn’t appear to respond well to diet and exercise. Fat also appears on their back, thighs, and inner knees which were not present prior to the pregnancies.

Breasts can go any which way – breasts can get larger after a pregnancy from either fat and/or glands, or breasts can get smaller and just look deflated, or they can droop due to being stretched out.

One other component of the Mommy Makeover in Iowa is a surgery to reduce excess labia minora tissue which often grows from either pregnancy and/or labor. All of the above areas are included in the Mommy Makeover depending on the patient’s needs and/or preferences.
