Sleeping after mommy makeover

Following a mommy makeover that includes a tummy tuck, it is important to keep your hips flexed so that you decrease tension on your lower abdominal incision. Most often this means sitting up in bed at a 45 degree angle and putting a pillow under your knees

Usually is also ok to rest on your side with your hips flexed. It is best to change positions from time to time rather than staying in only one position. You can straighten out completely after a few weeks
