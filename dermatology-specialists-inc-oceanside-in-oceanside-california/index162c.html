Jens Thiele, MD, PhD

Name: Jens Thiele, MD, PhD
Position: Business Owner
Years experience: 27
Biography: Originally from Frankfurt, Germany, Dr. Thiele graduated summa cum laude from JW Goethe University. He completed his internship at the University of Chicago Hospital and received his Dermatology residency training at Boston University and Tufts University, where he also served as Chief Resident. He then completed an additional one-year fellowship in Mohs micrographic surgery.
Fellowship-trained also in Procedural Cosmetic Dermatology, Dr. Thiele offers cosmetic anti-aging procedures such as Botox, soft tissue filler injections (e.g. Restylane, Juvederm, Sculptra), sclerotherapy for leg veins, chemical peels, and Laser treatments (such as VBEAM).
In addition to his expertise in Mohs surgery, Dr. Thiele is an internationally renowned expert on skin aging and antioxidants, the subject of his PhD thesis.

Joseph A Samady,MD

Name: Joseph A Samady,MD
Position: Business Owner
Years experience: 22
Biography: Dr. Samady is a dermatologist serving patients from San Diego and Riverside counties. For more information, or to request your consultation, please contact us.

Dr. Samady enjoys spending time and traveling with his wife, son and daughter.
