Is this the best way for me to lose weight? Is there a plan for monthly payment?
Weight Loss
Jun 5, 2018
SculpSure is great way to non-surgically get rid of areas of fat. But, it is not a “weight loss” device. The ideal candidate is someone who is at or near their ideal weight, but still has regions of excess fat that will not go away with diet and exercise.
For the initial weight loss, there are several dietary strategies. It would be best if you worked with a local clinician who could give you more specific advice on the best plan for you.
41 y/o, 5’2 and 150lbs, done having kids, no diet and exercise- Sculpsure with a 28 BMI?
SculpSure can help
May 22, 2018
You should always be working on a healthful diet and maintaining regular exercise. However, SculpSure can definitely help reduce some of the fat. The ideal candidate is someone who has a BMI less than 30. There are several technologies available to help reduce fat, from surgical approaches such as liposuction, to the nonsurgical treatments including CoolSculpting, Vanquish and SculpSure. In my experience SculpSure is the best option for nonsurgical treatment.
