time to bounce back
Mar 3, 2020
If you are having an abdominoplasty (Tummy tuck) I usually recommend two weeks off of work, if it is just breast surgery I usually recommend 1 week off and 5-6 weeks from strenuous physical activity for both surgeries. Some patients might be able to make it back a little sooner but that is the exception more than the rule. You always want to wait until you have enough time down to recover and bounce back after surgery.

Preop Testing

Many young healthy patients do not need additional preop labs unless they have a significant medical issues or a pertinent family history of issues with anesthesia etc. In the old days they ordered labs on everyone, but the yield of valuable information ended up being so low that they no longer do that for many minor surgical cases.

Are you able to get a lower body lift and bra line back lift at the same time?
can you combine a Lower body lift and bra line lift
Oct 23, 2019
Theoretically, you could do both procedures at one time but it would significantly increase the risk of complications, because of competing areas of tension and decreased blood supply. Therefore, I would never recommend doing both at the same time. This falls under the category of just because it can be done does not mean it is a good idea.

Is it safe to have a breast lift if I have hidradenitis suppurativa?
Get infections treated before surgery for safety.
Oct 23, 2019
HS is not a contraindication to surgery but if you are having an active flare up you should get that treated adequately before starting any new surgeries. Having an active infection elsewhere increases your risks for any other surgical procedure. I would make sure to talk about it with your surgeons and they may be able to help with your armpit lesions before surgery. Best of luck on your journey.
