Considering a TT but need to rule out the option of DIEP first. Tummy Tuck vs DIEP?
DIEP FLAPS
Jan 9, 2020
It sounds like you would be a good candidate for DIEP flaps especially if you are considering having a tummy tuck.  Please see a DIEP flap surgeon who can examine you and go over your options.  Good luck!
I had DIEP flap reconstruction, now with bulge called eventration? Is this a fancy word for bulge? Wi
bulge after DIEP
Jan 9, 2020
This can occur after DIEP flaps and can be repaired.  Insurance will usually cover this procedure so you should discuss with your doctor.
Muscle repair and hernia repair both added charges with DIEP flap surgery?
Hernia repair
Jan 9, 2020
Usually muscle/small hernia repair can be done at the same time of DIEP surgery, but should be discussed with your surgeon beforehand.  I do muscle repair work as part of the DIEP procedure.  Best wishes!
Flap procedure if you are a small person with no extra skin?
DIEP FLAPS
Jan 9, 2020
It would be best to go in for a consultation so the doctor could examine you and go over all your options.  You are correct about the radiation as it can compromise your implants and the cosmetic outcome.  Best wishes!
I had a TT with lipo done 4 weeks ago. I would like to know why it looks uneven?
Swelling
May 13, 2019
You will need to be patient and wait for the swelling to go down which may take several months.  If the area is still of concern then touch up lipo can be done.  Please continue to follow up with your PS.
How safe is it to do fat grafting to the breasts after explant?
fat grafting
Mar 5, 2019
Fat can be injected at the same time as you have your implants removed.  However there will be some fat loss over time as not all the fat that is injected survives.  Make sure to see a board certified PS for your options.
If you have very strong abdominal muscles, will your tummy tuck recovery time be any less?
abs
Mar 5, 2019
Being healthy and strong will definitely help with your recovery and your overall results.  But to actually see a 6 pack will require dropping your body fat percentage through diet.  Good luck!
Prophylactic mastectomy and reconstruction. Would it be necessary to use expanders instead of just pl
Breast Reconstruction
Mar 5, 2019
The quick answer is yes but many factors come into play during the actual surgery to determine if a direct implant can be done.  You may also want to consider DIEP flaps which uses the fat from your belly to reconstruct the breasts and no implants are required.  Best wishes!
Is it possible to have all breast tissue removed due to severe fibricystic breasts, then have implant
breast disease
Mar 5, 2019
Yes this is a possibility for you but make sure you see a PS who can offer you all your options in regards to your reconstruction: implants vs. using your own tissues (DIEP flaps).  Best wishes!
Can my implants still settle and change after breast reconstruction ? It’s been 5 months
implants
Mar 5, 2019
Yes your implants can change with more time and I would also recommend waiting at least 6 months before any revisions can be done.  Best wishes!
Too much compression ruin my TT?
swelling
Mar 5, 2019
Swelling is normal during the post op period.  Your binder may have been positioned incorrectly so that more swelling went to the pubic area so you may want to try adjusting or just wearing the spanx for now.
I am now 3 weeks post op and seeing great results but will they last?
lipo
Mar 5, 2019
Your results will continue to improve and if you add diet and exercise you will be able to maintain your results.  Remember you cannot cheat the "fat" as it will come back if you are not careful.
When doing a mommy makeover can I get new boobs , snatched waist and juicy booty in one surgery ?
combination procedures
Mar 5, 2019
It would be best to go in for a personal consultation as a variety comes into play to see if you are a candidate for combination surgeries: your health status, the time it will take for your PS to perform the procedure, and of course safety.  Please see a few board certified PS for consults.
Is a tummy tuck my only option?
tummy tuck
Mar 5, 2019
Your best results will come with a tummy tuck/lipo procedure.  If you try just lipo or laser tightening, you will be disappointed with the results.  Make sure you see a board certified PS.
