Would I be able to have a subglandular augmentation?
Subglandular augmentation
Apr 20, 2020
Yes, it appears from the photos that you would be an excellent candidate for a subglandular breast augmentation. The advantages of the subglandular approach are a better shaped of the breasts, a faster recovery from surgery and no risk of breast animation deformity that can occur from placement under the pectoralis major muscle. We utilize the no touch technique breast augmentation and have not seen any issues with capsular contractures.

Breast augmentation after breastfeeding: can I consider augmentation if one breast is still exp

Apr 20, 2020
It is best to avoid any breast surgery after breast feeding for a minimum of 6 months. Otherwise, you run the risk of developing an infection. I took care of a patient who had a breast reduction too soon after breast feeding done by another surgeon and one of the breasts developed a bad infection with some tissue loss. Can breast augmentation increase breastmilk production?
Increase in breast milk production with breast implants?
Apr 20, 2020
Breast implants do not increase breast milk production. However, if a breast implant is placed that is too large for the breast pocket, atrophy of breast tissue can occur and this would indirectly have an adverse effect of breast milk production. That is why it is so important to place an appropriately sized implant for the patient’s tissue characteristics, while at the same respecting the patient’s wishes to the greatest extent possible.
