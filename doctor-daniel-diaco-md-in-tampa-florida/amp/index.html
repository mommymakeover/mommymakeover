Are dog ears typical for tummy tuck?
Dog ears
Aug 31, 2017
Dog ears are pretty typical with tummy tuck/lipoabdominoplasty surgery. Dog ears are also very easy to revise and should be part of the original cost of the surgery. Usually the dog ears can be revised under local anesthesia after about 6mths to a year of recovery. I wait at least 6 months to a year because they can flatten out on there own. Hope this answers your question.
