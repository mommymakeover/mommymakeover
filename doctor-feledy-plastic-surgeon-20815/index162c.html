I had a full tummy tuck. I am not happy with what I am seeing. Am I judging too soon?
Too Early
Jan 28, 2016
2 weeks post operative results are too early to be concerned. In the weeks to come, you will see changes that take place. Tummy Tuck is a very big surgery and takes many months to see the final result. Continue to see your plastic surgeon for your concerns and follow up visits. Best of luck to you and your new figure!
Am I a good candidate for Lipo or Tummy Tuck?
Weight Loss Surgery before Body Contouring
Jan 19, 2016
Hi,We would worry that your BMI at 36 is too high for a safe and effective surgery. You would want to get your BMI reduced first, and then proceed with a consultation for possible Circumferencial Body Lift or Tummy Tuck with Liposuction if required. After loosing your maxial weight, you should be evaluated to see what would work best for you. We have had many patients be very successful with weight loss surgery then to proceed with body contouring, Tummy Tuck afyer your goal is...
What actually happened? Is it possible that I'm completely healed there?
Liposuction - Post operative question
Jan 18, 2016
It is very common that most patients have some type of swelling and discomfort with Liposuction. Maximal swelling time is usually 7-10 days. It is true that some patients do not feel pain like others, so it doesn't mean that Liposuction was not performed in that area. You should review your procedure with your surgeon during your post - operative visit.
