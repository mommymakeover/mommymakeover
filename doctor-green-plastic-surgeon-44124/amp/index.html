Am I a good candidate for forehead reduction?
The final decision will need to be made in surgery.
Aug 23, 2020
I would aim to remove about 1.5 to 2cm of the forehead skin. The actual amount will depend on how mobile you scalp is after lifting it up. It sometimes takes a lot of work to stretch the scalp and move it forward.
I think this procedure will help you a lot. You seem to be an ideal candidate.
Can saline implants be drained weeks prior to explant surgery to help determine if a breast lift is
I think that is a great idea.
Aug 23, 2020
The implants can certainly be drained with a needle with local anesthesia in advance of the surgery, and I have done this several times. This should not affect the capsule removal, and should make complete capsule removal easier.
Drainless, progressive tension closure tummy tuck: besides convenience, does it speedup attachment
There are many advantages to a Drainless Tummy tuck.
Aug 23, 2020
I have been using a progressive tension closure with no drains for several years now. I only wish I had done this earlier. The healing is better with less tension on the suture line, the dead-space under the skin flap is drastically reduced, the incidence of seromas is less, even than when I used drains.
In addition the discomfort and inconvenience of the drains is eliminated.
It does take a bit longer, but the benefits make it worth while.
I'm scheduled to get a BBL on Dec 15th. My height is 5'3", weight 170 lbs. Is this a good weight fo
I think that is fine.
Aug 23, 2020
As long as you are comfortable with your weight and not planning any drastic change I think it is fine to do a BBL.
Thinner patients have a better response with less fat volume. (I.e a little makes a big difference)
I do not restrict my BBL procedures to any particular BMI. But iff someone is very overweight and planning to lose weight I would delay.
Good luck with your procedure.

Would I still need to wear a Brava prior to a fat transfer if my skin is already stretched from bre

Mar 22, 2020
If you have skin laxity and you have sufficient basic breast volume as well as donor fat available you may not need any preparation. Sometimes you may need a second session of fat injection to accomplish your goal but even that may be preferable to wearing the Brava device for a month before surgery.
Can fat transfer breast augmentation help increase fullness in the lower pole of the breast?
Yes you can.
Mar 22, 2020
The great thing about fat transfer is that you can put the fat wherever you want and you are not limited to the shape and size of a synthetic implant. You can add extra volume for the upper pole, the lower pole, the cleavage area, and the sides of the breasts. You can also lower the inframammary fold and even correct tubular breast deformity. Where you put the fat will depend on the deficiency that you have and your plastic surgeon should be able to guide you on this.
Do I need a breast reduction or a breast lift with fat transfer to breast?

Mar 22, 2020
Fat transfer is a very useful technique to add volume to the breast without the use of a foreign implant. The fat is harvested using liposuction and prepared and thereafter injected into the breast with minimal scarring and effective addition of volume. If you are happy with the volume of your breasts then you probably would need a lift if you feel that your breasts are too large then you need a breast reduction which includes a lift type procedure. Go ahead and transfer the fat to buttocks...
