Is uneven dropping normal at the 6 month mark?
Uneven
Nov 14, 2023
At 6 months, most of the "settling" is done, but it's hard to say if this is "normal" or not without seeing what you looked like before, and knowing more about the surgery and implants. My best advice is to go to your surgeon and tell him/her that you are not happy because you feel they are uneven. Then he/she can evaluate your healing and tell you if it is normal or not, and if it is possible to fix. Hope that helps. Best of luck!
5'3, 138lbs, 22, does 375cc mod profile Natrelle give me a similar result?
Implant size and profile
Nov 14, 2023
There isn't one implant size that is the only size that would work for you. The result with each different size and profile has a different look, and I think that you would do well with a high profile implant. Go to a plastic surgeon consultation where you can try on the different sizes and profiles. Then you can see what each one will look like on your body and frame. Then you can make an informed decision about which one looks best to you. Best of luck!
Can an areola reduction be performed without a breast lift?
Areola reduction
Nov 14, 2023
Yes, you can have areola reduction without the vertical scar that is typically added when a breast lift is done simultaneously. Without the lollipop scar, your breasts won't be lifted as much, but it sounds like that is ok with you. Best of luck!

Too big
Nov 12, 2023

Scar has moved from my breast fold
Bottoming out
Nov 9, 2023
Hi. Your breast implants have something called bottoming out. That means that there isn't enough support to hold the implants higher. You should talk to your surgeon ASAP so that he/she can fix it before it gets worse. You will need surgery to give more support at the bottom of your breast to keep the implants up. This can be done with stitches or mesh. Your surgeon can discuss the options with you. Best of luck!
