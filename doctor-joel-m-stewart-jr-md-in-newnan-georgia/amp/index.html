I have saline implants that are 15 years old. Both appear to need a lift. Is this common?
Breast lift
Jul 27, 2020
As you age, it is very common for your breasts to age as well. The larger and heavier the implants are, the higher the likelihood that they will pull your breasts down necessitating the need for a breast lift.
It is not uncommon to feel occasional pain, but having an exam with a plastic surgeon is the most prudent thing to do if you are concerned.
Best of luck.
