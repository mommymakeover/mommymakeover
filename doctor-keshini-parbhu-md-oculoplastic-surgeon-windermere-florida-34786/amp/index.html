61, male, realistic expectations & remedies available for blepharoplasty overcorrection?
Options for lower lid rejuvenation
Sep 29, 2023
It's difficult to assess lower eyelids just from photos but it appears like you have excess/overactive orbicularis muscle and skin in your lower eyelids. Age 32, what procedure would correct the puffiness and shadows under my eyes?
Puffiness after Filler
Sep 29, 2023
Fillers can sometimes affect the lymphatic drainage of the eye and multiple rounds of hyaluronidase can be needed to dissolve it. I would recommend seeing oculoplastics/ facial plastics to ensure you don't need more dissolving and to assess if you are a candidate for a surgical lower blepharoplasty.

Recommendations for my uneven eyes? Is this an eyelid/ptosis issue?
Eye Asymmetry
Jan 31, 2023
There is asymmetry to the eyes with one canthus slightly higher than the other. It is difficult to assess from the photos if one eye protrudes more than the other but this can be determined in person by your surgeon. Asymmetry is normal and can be due to differences in soft tissue but also underlying bony structure. Eyelid surgery addresses the soft tissue. To help you with the nuances of your eyes I would recommend that you see an oculoplastic surgeon.
Is there more Restylane to dissolve or is this a malar bag?
Under Eye Puffiness
Jan 31, 2023
Under eye puffiness following filler can be due to the filler hydrating or the filler can block lymphatic channels.
Will dissolving under eye filler also remove cheek filler?
Dissolving Filler
Jan 31, 2023
Under eye filler is close to the cheek and so dissolving under eye filler can possibly dissolve cheek filler because the hyaluronidase enzyme can spread.
7 year old under eye filler removal, can this be dissolved/surgically removed?
Dissolving Under Eye Filler
Jan 31, 2023
Juvederm is composed of hyaluronic acid so hyaluronidase can be used to dissolve it. It can sometimes take more than one session to completely dissolve the filler.
