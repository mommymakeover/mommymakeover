Is it better behind the muscle tissue or in front for a woman who is flat chested, when getting brea
Implant position
Feb 3, 2015
For the vast majority of women, implant placement behind the muscle is the best position. This provides the maximum amount of tissue above the implant, and has been shown to have the highest durability and lowest complication rate. There are some exceptions though where placement behind the breast tissue can be advantageous.
