Botox or forehead surgery following ptosis surgery
Nov 19, 2017
I agree with others. It would be fine to perform either neurotoxin injections or forehead surgery after ptosis surgery.
Check with your ptosis surgeon, but in general I recommend at least 6 weeks before undergoing Botox injections; especially in the glabellar area. Surgery in the eyelids can create potential spaces where the Botox can spread where it shouldn’t. Would wait 2-3 months before forehead surgery.

Blepharoplasty Recovery
Nov 19, 2017
In short, the appearance of your eyelids is within expectations for 4 days after blepharoplasty. Everyone heals differently. Some have more bruising than swelling, others have a lot of swelling with minimal bruising. I tell my patients to expect a certain amount of both bruising and swelling that is likely to be the worst in appearance on postoperative day 2 and 3.
