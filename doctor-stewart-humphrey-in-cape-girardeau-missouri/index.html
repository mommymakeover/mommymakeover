Local vs general anesthesia for large lipoma?
lipoma removal anesthesia
Apr 20, 2018
Small lipomas in favorable areas are often removed under straight local in the clinic. Moderate lipomas or those in more challenging locations are typicaly removed under IV sedation and local in the outpatient surgery center. Large lipomas are also sometimes removed under IV sedation and local, but as the size increases most surgeons are more likely to recommend general anesthesia. I personally would not want a 8 x 10 cm lipoma removed from my body under local anesthesia alone!
