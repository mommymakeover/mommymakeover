Ptosis of the eyelid. What can be done?
you seem to have mild ptosis.
Dec 23, 2016
Lazy eye, in medical terms, means poor vision of an eye. From the photos you’ve sent me you seem to have mild ptosis. There are several ways to correct ptosis and in your case non-incisional muller-levator complex tucking seems to be the way. This procedure uses a thread to shorten and strengthen your muscles involved in eye opening and takes about 30 minutes. Thank you.
