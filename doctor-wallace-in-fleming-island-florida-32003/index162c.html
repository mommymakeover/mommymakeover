Jan 18, 2017
The FDA states saline implants are approved for augmentation in women aged 19 years or older, while silicone implants are approved for women aged 22 years or older. To select a silicone implant for a woman younger than 22 years old is considered "off label" use and up to the discretion of the surgeon based on what they feel would provide the best results and whether or not the surgeon feels the patient is mature enough for silicone implants.
