Rhinoplasty with thin skin?
thin skin nose
Nov 10, 2023
Hello.
Yes, it is true thin-skinned noses have a higher difficulty for rhinoplasties because underneath structures can show sharp edges, and there is an increased risk for skin necrosis. Avoiding the creation of sharp surfaces, making them curved and soft, and adding tissue as fascia in between structures and skin is what is used to reduce this risk. Hyperbaric oxygen therapy after surgery for 5 consecutive days can be used to prevent skin necrosis.
