Can I get moles removed during breast augmentation?
Questions
Aug 22, 2019
Hello!
Usually this would be determined by your board certified plastic surgeon. Due to protecting the sterility and safety of the patient and use of implants (foreign body), we usually would not perform the mole excision at the time same time.
Excisions under local anesthetic are quite simple, safe and painless in the office.
I recommend checking with your plastic surgeon if they are comfortable with the combined procedures.

Good luck!
