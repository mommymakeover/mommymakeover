Regret after laser beard removal. Will it grow back?
regrowth after beard removal
Sep 27, 2018
Hi. Thanks for the question. Typically, it takes 6-8 sessions or more to remove the majority of the hair in any area, and only hair in the active growth phase (anagen) is affected by the laser. If you have only had one session so far, it is very likely that the majority of the hair will return, though it may grow back more slowly than usual.
