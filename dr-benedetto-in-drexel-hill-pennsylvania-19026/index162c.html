Can botox lift a droopy nasal tip?
Yes
Nov 6, 2020
Yes. This can be done with fillers and botox. The botulinum toxin will relax the muscle underneath and will elevate the tip of the nose. The fillers will constitute a support and will elevate the tip too.
Do I need botox or fillers at this point in my life?
50-year-old good looker
Feb 17, 2016
At your age you still look very young with a minimal amount of wrinkles according to the picture I see.  You are just starting to develop frown lines which are easily treated with BOTOX.  In time you might need some soft tissue filling.  This should be done when there is obvious loss of fullness and changes to the center of your face that are obvious when you compare pictures of yourself from years ago.  Until then, just a little BOTOX will do, if you are in the mood.
Will botox help this??
Deep wrinkles
Feb 17, 2016
Your forehead wrinkles do not look that deeply embedded in your forehead.  I have treated others with deeper wrinkles with BOTOX and they eventually all disappeared.  It may take a month or more and even a second treatment, but you should not give up on having BOTOX treatments for your forehead.  However, you should see a doctor who treats a lot of people with BOTOX and is comfortable with different types of patients and their particular problem.
Can you get red eyes from Botox?
Botox
Feb 8, 2016
It was coincidental and should not happen if injections are done correctly. Botox is not know to cause red eye from injections. That is what may have happened ten years ago.
I have two-fold under eye bags after Voluma treatment. What's the best way to fix it?
Dear Eyebags
Oct 26, 2015
Since Voluma is a hyaluronic acid filler, hyaluronidase will be able to reduce some of the excessive puffiness of your eyelids. Please return to your dermatologist to reevaluate and treat your situation.
How can a bad eye injection be fixed?
Dear Puffy Eyes,
Oct 26, 2015
It depends on what was injected. If it was a hyaluronic acid filler, then Hyaluronidase can be used to reduce the size of the fullness. Return to the physician who treated you for him or her to reevaluate your situation. Restylane is a hyaluronic acid filler which should reduce with injections of hyaluronidase.
Cheek fillers, cheek implants or mini facelift ? Other suggestions?
Dear Young and Beautiful,

You are young and beautiful and you do not need anything at this time. But, if you insist, only a little filler is all that you should have done. No implants and no mini facelift.
