I’m 324 pounds and 5’4″. Can I get a BBL?
BBL weighing 324 pounds
Oct 5, 2020
Your BMI is calculated to be almost 56. You absolutely are not a candidate for liposuction and BBL. You must lose well over 100 pounds to be considered an acceptable BMI for body contouring, and once this much weight is lost the skin elasticity changes to such a degree that stand alone liposuction would not produce favorable aesthetic outcome. You require a medically supervised weight loss program before considering cosmetic surgery.
