Had Full Face Phenol Peel Oct. 15. No Results?
Phenol Peel Results
Jan 28, 2014
I would recommend discussing concerns with your physician. Phenol peels can be applied at many different concentrations and with different techniques. Sometimes patients may require a second peel for treatment of difficult areas. Also review your pre and post treatment photos with your doctor. It is not uncommon to forget the amount of aging and wrinkles you experiences prior to your procedure until you review your old photos.
