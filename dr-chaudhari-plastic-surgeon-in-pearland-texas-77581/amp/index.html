Is breast augmentation safe for a person with hyperthyroid?
Breast implants are safe, your thyroid condition is the issue
Mar 21, 2018
Your thyroid nodule and hyperthyroid condition are a risk for ANY surgery, so that's the bigger issue. Getting breast implants is generally safe for most healthy patients, but your thyroid condition may affect your blood pressure during surgery and give the anesthesiologist a challenge, so it's important to have that thoroughly addressed and treatment before having your breast implant surgery.
