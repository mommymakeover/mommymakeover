Can a minor donut lift revision be done if there is a permanent suture inside?
Periareolar Revision
Aug 9, 2023
Yes, I am sure it could be revised. Just know, however, that when the permanent stitch is removed, this will allow that nipple to stretch out more. The purpose of the permanent suture is to prevent widening of the nipple areola complex. I recommend you pursue an in person evaluation by a board-certified plastic surgeon who has experience in revisionary breast procedures. Best of luck.
Is this areola skin in my vertical incision? Is this fixable?
Retained areolar tissue
Oct 14, 2022
Greetings. Yes, this is areolar skin which has been moved along the vertical incision line during your surgery. This happens sometimes when the areolas are very large. This is a easily fixable situation. Re-excising along the vertical incision line can remove that tissue. This can usually be done in the office at a local anesthesia. Discuss it with your surgeon.

Best regards

Dr. Deboni
