Statement: Dr. Devan Griner, MD is a board-certified plastic surgeon at Peak Plastic Surgery with offices in Cottonwood Heights and Provo, Utah. Some of the most popular plastic surgery procedures at his clinic include rhinoplasty, facial rejuvenation, tummy tuck (abdominoplasty), breast augmentation, liposuction, and mommy makeover.
