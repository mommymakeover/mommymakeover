Activity after body lift

Is a 2 hour flight 2 days after chin liposuction a DVT or PE risk?
DVT risk
Jan 21, 2019
In general, the greatest risk for DVT comes from longer surgeries with general anesthesia. In your situation where you are just having local anesthesia and a short procedure then the risk should be minimal. Getting up and walking during the flight and staying well hydrated would be best. I would recommend discussing this with your surgeon as well. The flight itself should have no effect on the overall outcome of the surgery or interfere with the healing process.
Had an FDL nearly a year ago, I still have excess skin, quite a lot of it. Do I need another full FDL
Revision
Jan 1, 2019
Congratulations on the weight loss. It would be optimal to have a consultation with a Plastic Surgeon experienced in procedures after weight loss. With most people that have lost as much weight that you have it is necessary to perform a circumferential procedure (such as a belt lipectomy or body lift) to be able to remove enough tissue to make the remaining skin tight. Sometimes, simply revising the FDL will not allow the surgeon to remove enough loose skin.
What procedure would be best for excess neck skin?
Neck Lift
Jan 1, 2019
What you should do is really dependent on what you want to go through and what you are looking for as an end result. Any non invasive treatment will mean slightly less down time but will not produce significant changes. A lower face and neck lift will help tighten the neck skin, remove the jowls and define the jaw line. From the photos, it seems that if you wanted to create the best result for the longest period of time then a surgical lower face and neck lift would be optimal.
I want to get rid on my saddlebags, is liposuction the way to go?
Saddlebag Liposuction
Jan 1, 2019
Your saddlebag region looks to be easily treated with liposuction and you should be able to have a great result. For your situation there probably will not be a difference with the type of liposuction used (traditional, ultrasonic, laser etc). I would recommend seeing an experienced Plastic Surgeon and following their advise regarding the type of liposuction that they are most comfortable with.

Fat transfer for breast cup size 32A to a 35. What is best results with fat transfer?
Breast Fat Grafting
Feb 7, 2018
Fat grafting to the breast can increase the size by about one cup. So, in your situation you may realistically be able to go from a 32a to a 32b with one procedure. If you wanted to go larger then it may require additional fat grafting procedures at a later date. The number 32 is a measurement of circumference of your chest. Fat grafting to the breast will not increase you from a 32 to a 35.
Will my skin loosen and implants soften?
Post-op Breasts
Jan 19, 2018
It is very normal for you to have swelling and for the breasts to feel firm at this point. The implants will usually sit a little higher right now and will settle with time. As the implants are in the sub-glandular position the breasts will continue to look more "natural" as time goes on.
Will new fat cells from fat transfer react the same as my own?

Nov 7, 2017

The transferred fat will gain and reduce in size as you gain and lose weight. However, to a certain extent the transferred fat "remembers where it came from".
