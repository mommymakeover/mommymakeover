What happens if you get sick with a cough or cold before surgery?
Postpone your surgery
Jul 2, 2021
Depending on what is causing your cough, your surgery may need to be postpone until you are feeling better. Having surgery when you are at your healthiest is best and can reduce risk of problems in recovery. Please let your surgeon know if you are not feeling ideal before surgery. There should never be rush when it comes to your body. The surgery will always be there when you are healthy and ready.
When Does Swelling After Breast Augmentation Begin to Subside?
Swelling for Breast Augmentations
Jul 2, 2021
We do not recommend our patients to even try buying any bras until 3 months post-op when 90% of their swelling is gone. Wearing a compression bra is ideal for healing as well with a strap to keep your implants from riding high. You should also not be wearing a wire bra for at least 1-2 months. If your implants are behind the muscle, they will definitely be high and swollen for at least 2-3 months. Doing implants massage can help them relax and keep your pocket, capsule and implants soft!
How Long Should You Wear Your Binder/Garment for After a Tummy Tuck?
Compression Garments
Jul 2, 2021
No matter which procedure you are getting, a compression garment is necessary to get your best results. I recommend using compression garments for at least 4 weeks. For any procedure, 50% of your swelling will be gone at your 4 week mark, which is why we recommend wearing a binder, bra or whichever garment. After the 4 week mark, we do not require our patients to continue wearing it, but we do recommend it until their 90 day mark.
