Will I need a donut lift with Ideal implants?
The implant will give the illusion of a lift
Dec 27, 2020
When positioned properly, the implant will fill in the skin in the lower portion of the breast and make it look like the nipple is higher. This is with a dual plane technique. A donut lift really doesn’t provide much elevation of the nipple. In your case, I would place the implant through a periareolar incision and then wait 3 months to see if you need the lift. Once it’s done, it can’t be undone – but it can always be done later! Good luck.

You have a keloid scar
Nov 4, 2020
Your scar is called a keloid. It happens unfortunately, but is more common in people with darker skin. It’s not you “fault,” but just your genetics. It happens most commonly where the hair regrows, probably from chronic irritation. I would NOT cut it out again – that often makes it worse. Instead, it should be injected with a steroid to get it to settle down. It often takes a series of injections, spaced about a month apart. Good luck!
