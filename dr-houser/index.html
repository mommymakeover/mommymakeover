Removing Permalip implants?
Very simple to remove.
Oct 5, 2020
It seems odd but one of the selling points of the PermaLip implant is that it is easy to remove. Your body does not grow in to them, you all the surgeon needs to do is numb the corner of the mouth, make a small incision, snag it and remove it. I would contact your original surgeon – they may not charge you much for removal. At a minimum, go to someone that has placed them so they don’t do something excessive. Good luck!

Sizing

What is Acell? Why is it being used for hair growth?
Not a great product for hair
Apr 25, 2020
A-Cell comes from the lining of pig bladders. Gross, right? But we have found that it works great in the realm of wound care.   In that application, it is used topically and is really effective. However, injecting it is not a good idea.   it can create a foreign body reaction and granuloma formation.   That could actually cause hair loss! PRP alone works well, but finasteride is most important in men with hair loss.  Make sure you are seeing a reputable hair surgeon.   Good luck!
