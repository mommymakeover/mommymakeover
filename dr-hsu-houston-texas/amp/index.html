Dry and flaky skin after a tummy tuck
Dry and flaky skin after a tummy tuck
Jun 13, 2018
This is very common. The skin is severely traumatized in this operation and gets very swollen and is under great tension. Peeling is a result of the skin healing….out with the old and in with the new. It will pass and moisturizer is your friend.
Post TT after infection: looks like it's going to take along time to heal.
Post TT after infection: looks like it's going to take along time to heal.
Jun 13, 2018
Wounds like this are a risk with large surgeries like a tummy tuck.The good news is that wound looks healthy and all that redness is healthy healing tissue. It will take a matter of weeks in order for that to heal, but rest assured it will get there and will look quite nice when all said and done.
I’m 5’7” and about 135 lbs. Would it be possible for me to get a BBL? Or do I️ not have enough
You are rather thin
Jun 13, 2018
You are rather thin. In order to answer this question well, I would want to know what your end goal for your buttocks would be. It definitely wouldn’t hurt to gain 15 pounds or so in order to create enough donor fat for your hips and buttocks.
Can I wear waist trainer over compression garment? 5 days post BBL.
Be careful
Jun 13, 2018
There are no rules with compressive wear after a BBL. I do think that more compression is better than less. Be careful with garments so they don’t create creasing in the abdominal skin as you sit. These creases can become permanent. I like to have my patients wear a belly board for 6-8 weeks and lymphatic massage definitely helps move out the swelling and fluid quickly.

Is it probable that I still have fluid in my stomach and back 8 months after tummy tuck and Liposuction?
Jun 13, 2018
It is unlikely there is fluid remaining this far out from surgery. It is however very common to still have tightness and pain in the abdomen after tummy tuck especially as your activity level risk.
Do you have to be a certain weight to get a waist liposuction and/or butt augmentation?
Yes
Jun 13, 2018
In general, the ideal BMI for a BBL is 26-29. This allows for enough fat to make a real difference in the buttocks and to have a noticeable change with liposuction. That being said, every female figure is different and each person’s goals for their buttocks are unique. A consultation with a surgeon can help you get a realistic idea of outcomes and what you could expect to see.

It is very early in your healing process and you have a large amount of swelling this early on. The skin will look bumpy and irregular for the first 2-4 weeks and then things will begin to settle down. Be sure to wear your compression garments and I really like both lymphatic massages and a belly board to get the best possible results especially in that location.

Does the fat transfer for BBL permanently move fat cells to your hips/butts?
Does the fat transfer for BBL permanently move fat cells to your hips/butts?
Mar 22, 2018
BBL does permanently transfer those fat cells to the hips and buttocks. They can never return from where the came. Just remember that not all fat that is grafted will survive the transfer. Your new shape will fluctuate with weight but in proportion to its new shape. In other words, your shape will not revert to where it once was.

BBL recovery and returning to "active" work life?
BBL recovery and returning to "active" work life?
Mar 21, 2018
I agree that most patients can return to work at two weeks, but your job is not like most women’s. You will experience lots of discomfort and swelling with all of the walking and moving you do on a daily basis. At one month, you should be much better at completing your daily tasks, but even then swelling and discomfort will be more than average and your final results will take longer to reveal themselves.

3 weeks post op BBL. I have hard and soft bumps all over my abdomen. Is this normal?
Mar 21, 2018
The things you describe are rather normal at this stage. Liposuction often results in lumpy/bumpy in the beginning and things begin to smooth out and soften with time. Your results will not be complete for months but the majority of what you describe should be gone in about 8weeks. Your results are looking great. Keep wearing your compression and boards as they will help things continue to progress in the right direction.

How bad is it to sleep chest down after a breast augmentation (2 years post op)?
Mar 21, 2018
I always tell my patients they are safe to sleep on their stomachs once it feels comfortable. That is usually some number of months and the internal healing is complete. Is pain medication necessary after a BBL?
Jan 21, 2018
Pain medication is prescribed in an as needed capacity. Thus, if you do not need the medication you do not have to take it. Keep in mind that the analgesics are in fact pain medication.
