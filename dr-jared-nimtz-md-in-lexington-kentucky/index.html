What is the risk of doing a mommy makeover and arm lift while smoking?
mommy makeover and smoking
Jan 30, 2019
While you may feel ok accepting the risk of having multiple larger procedures done while you still smoke, your surgeon likely does not. They have your welfare in mind - they want you to heal well, and they want you to look your best. If you're going to spend the money, you likely want the best possible outcome - this comes with stopping smoking for a month before and a month after surgery.
