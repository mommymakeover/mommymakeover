Why is the calf/ankle a touchy place to get liposuction?
Ankle liposuction
Aug 20, 2020
Very touchy area to treat. Many critical structures travel through the narrow ankle (lymphatic, blood vessels and nerves) and liposuction can create a circumferential scar around the ankle and along with lymphatic damage one can have chronic pain and swelling. Needless to say, unless your plastic surgeon has done a lot of ankle lipo with no problems, it would behoove you to hold off. Thanks for sharing.
