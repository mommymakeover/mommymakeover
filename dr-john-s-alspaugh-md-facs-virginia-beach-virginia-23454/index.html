Before and after blepharoplasty 1 year later. Is a revision possible?
bleph
Jul 10, 2019
In blepharoplasty surgery, attention has to be paid to avoiding removal of so much eyelid skin such that eyelid closure is incomplete. Further eyelid closure with or without a longer scar may not be appropriate.  Brow lifting would probably be helpful and possible even fillers. Make an appointment with a board certified Plastic or Oculoplastic Surgeon to discuss options.
