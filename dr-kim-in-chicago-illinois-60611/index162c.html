Will I Need a Breast Lift with my Augmentation?
Breast Lift with Implants
Jan 15, 2017
The choice of a lift is up to you and your expectations.  Certainly an implant can help improve shape and size by re-inflating the deflated skin envelope.  A lift will give you extra enhancement in shape and reduce the areola size.  The issue of scarring is something to consider.  You can also delay the lift if you really aren’t sure.  Doing a labiaplasty at the same time should be possible.
Do I need a just a lift, or a lift with augmentation?
Breast Lift with or without implants
Jan 15, 2017
A lift alone will improve the shape of your breasts especially if you want to maintain approximately the same size.  There are ways to do an “auto-augmentation” that can also give you a little more projection and improved shape.  A small implant can also help enhance shape without dramatically impacting volume.  Your PS can go over these options with you.
