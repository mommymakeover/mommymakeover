Should I Still Wear a Girdle with 3 Months After Vaser Liposuction?
Time for compression garment
Nov 27, 2018
In my VASER Liposuction patients, I have them wear the compression garment for 3 months post-operatively.  It is best to check with your surgeon as every one has a different approach.  The garment is important to help minimize edema but it does not make the liposuction better or worse.  I also recommend post-operative massages starting the next day following the operation.
