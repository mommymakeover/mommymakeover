What exactly does under-the-muscle mean? How does the placement work?
under the muscle is a form of dual plane
Aug 5, 2020
When placed under the muscle, the upper part of the implant is covered by muscle but the lower part is covered by the breast gland. A complete submuscular approach is not used as is not conducive to a full/natural lower breast shape. The muscle and overlying breast soft tissues and skin conform to the shape of the underlying implant, a process that takes a few months for full "settling".
