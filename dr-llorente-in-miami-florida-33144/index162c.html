Florida 1 liter VS DR liters of fat?
Florida liposuction volume limit
Apr 12, 2016
In the state of Florida, the limit of fat that can be removed using liposuction when combined with another procedure is one liter. This rule aims to limit the amount of blood loss during combined procedures. If you don’t live in DR, it might be risky. I recommend you perform your procedure with a board certified plastic surgeon in your community.
