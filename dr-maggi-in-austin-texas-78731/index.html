Statement: Dr. Sergio Maggi, MD, FACS is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Austin, Texas. He specializes in a variety of cosmetic surgery procedures, ranging from rhinoplasty to breast enhancement surgery for both women and men. In fact, he’s often referred to as Austin’s “Breast Doctor.”

Dr. Maggi completed his undergrad at UCLA, then headed to medical school at the University of Texas Medical Branch at Galveston. He chose to do his surgical training at four different medical centers and universities, including a plastic surgery residency at St. Louis University in St. Louis, Missouri, then went on to complete his plastic surgery fellowship at Washington University/Barnes-Jewish Hospital (also in St. Louis).

His approach to cosmetic plastic surgery is to start small, for the sake of both safety and the most natural-looking results. “We can offer a spectrum very much tailored to what the patient is interested in receiving and keep it as minimal as possible,” he says. “Rather than throwing a lot at somebody, I prefer to start in small increments. I always prioritize safety and always maintain a natural look.”

It’s this philosophy that makes him one the best-known plastic surgeons in Austin, with a reputation for giving patients the results they envision for themselves. “The primary focus of our practice has always been on patient safety and obtaining predictable results,” he says. He focuses on patient satisfaction through delivering on the surgical experience and results they expect.

Board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, Dr. Maggi is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and a Fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).
