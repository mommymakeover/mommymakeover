Feedback on breast explant surgery?
Implant removal
Feb 11, 2019
This is a very good question. Once you have implants placed, there are changes in the breast, as well as the chest wall that make going without an implant difficult. The breast tissue thins, and in some patients the rib cage forms a dish-shaped depression in response to the pressure of the implants. The result is a very flat breast after the implant is removed. A breast lift will help, but most patients in my experience are not happy with the appearance of the breast after implant removal.

Why is my left so much different in size and profile than my right?
Size difference after surgery
Dec 11, 2018
Hello!The nature of the peri-prosthetic capsule can effect the projection and shape of the breast due to its interaction with the implant. This can make one breast project more than the other, which leads to the appearance of a size difference when viewed from above. When capsular contracture occurs, the shape of the breast changes dramatically. This likely is the source of the apparent size/shape difference. Go and see a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon for help. Best wishes!Matthew C Camp
