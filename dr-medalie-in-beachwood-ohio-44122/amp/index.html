What would you do? Is this fixable?
How to fix a double bubble deformity
Jan 30, 2020
HI,You have what is called a "double bubble" deformity. The implant has dropped below the level of the original inframammary fold. There is not a perfect solution for this, but during revision surgery the fold can be released to allow the overlying tissue to stretch and the implant capsule can be tightened to move the implant higher in the pocket. This should result in a significant improvement, but relapse and recurrent dropping of the implant can take  place. All my best,Daniel A. Medalie, MD
