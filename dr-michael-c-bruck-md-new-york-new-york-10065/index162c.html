What happens if you get sick with a cough or cold before surgery?
What Happens if I Get Sick Before Surgery?
Dec 31, 2020
This is an important question NewYearsNewBoobs. Patient safety and welfare are the first priority. It is always best for symptoms to completely resolve before proceeding with surgery. Recovery from anesthesia, and recovery in general, may be more difficult if you are ill. Coughing can increase blood pressure and cause bleeding. Please see your primary care physician for a full evaluation before proceeding with surgery. Remember--Safety First!
