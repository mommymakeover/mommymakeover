After such a great amount of weight loss, skin resection in the form of a tummy tuck or body lift would be more appropriate.  Liposuction can still be used to touch up areas but the former two procedures would likely be more appropriate.

How much can I expect my swelling to go down after labiaplasty?
3 days postop
Apr 9, 2018
It  is completely normal to be very swollen after labiaplasty surgery as this area is highly vascular (ie. has a robust blood supply).  Usually 4-6 weeks will bring down the majority of the swelling but it will continue to subside each month up to a year.

Apr 9, 2018

What are the odds of permanent nerve damage, or even temporary nerve damage post facelift?
Nerve damage
Apr 9, 2018
This is a very rare complication of facelift surgery.   Be sure to choose a surgeon who is very comfortable with the surgical anatomy involved in facelift surgery. Most ‘nerve injuries’ that become manifest after facelifts are also temporary.

areola reduction

Is it true that the more fat you remove the more the swollen you become?
More lipo, more swollen?
Apr 9, 2018
Yes, to some degree that is true.  The more fat that was taken, the more swollen you will become.  The swelling has to do with fluid that your body will ‘swell’ the surgical sites with to fill the ‘void’ that was left by removing your fat.   It generally takes 9-12 mos to see final results, although you should be seeing an improvement from your preop self much earlier than this (as long as youve kept a good diet etc.)hope this makes sense.
