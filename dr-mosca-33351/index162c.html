Does laying on your side at night after a breast augmentation cause soreness?
Side Sleepers and Breast Augmentation
Jul 20, 2018
Thank you for this question. Typically after your breast procedure you should not be lying on your side for a timeframe usually determined by your plastic surgeon. The ideal position for the first 3 months after the procedure is on your back with a sports bra on at all times. You do not want your capsule/pocket to form to far laterally (armpit) or medially (causing symmastia) which can occur when placing pressure on one side by lying on your side.

Large implant alone or lift and implant?
Necessary Lift
Jan 22, 2018
This a a question we face every day. However, the answer is typically the same. We cannot place an implant under the muscle and not do a lift in ptotic breasts. It can cause a deformity called “double bubble”. If you wish to be perky and have youthful cleavage like the wish pictures, you will need a lift. Simply placing large implants subglandular in ptotic breasts will just make them sag more along with your cleavage. I hope this helps your decision making.
