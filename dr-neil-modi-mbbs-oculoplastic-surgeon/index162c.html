Ensure ocular protection and review

Protecting the surface of the eye is the priority as if the ocular surface dries, it can become damaged and compromise vision. If your surgeon was an Oculoplastic/Ophthalmic specialist then they should be able to advise about this, other wise it would be worth consulting one regarding the ocular surface. Lubricants would be worth taking and perhaps try taping the eyelids shut at night to protect the eyes. The eyelid surgery looks to have been done recently given the bruising….
Do I have ptosis?
No – there is a difference in pretarsal show.
Jan 23, 2019
Hi John, Your eyelid height appears symmetrical. The upper lid show is slightly asymmetrical and this is to do with the amount of excess upper lid skin. The difference is minimal but ptosis surgery would not correct this. A small blepharoplasty may work on one side to balance the pretarsal show. All the best. Neil
I am considering blepharoplasty to upper eyelids. What is the average recovery time?
Very little downtime.
Jan 11, 2019
The recovery time is around a week to return to normal activities and around 2 for most bruising to settle. You do have excess upper eyelid skin and would be a good candidate for blepharoplasty. Prices vary considerably but you need to be happy with whoever you choose and certainly being local is important as aftercare is an important part of surgery.

How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
3-5 days onset with peak activity at 2 weeks. Duration 3-4 months.
Sep 10, 2018
Usually, patients see the results of Botox within 3-5 days. A similar product, Dysport typically works 1-2 days earlier. The full effect is seen within 2 weeks.
There is no limit to how long one can keep having it – the medical evidence goes back 20 years. It does wear off over 3-4 months and the exact duration for repeat injection can be varied depending on how much function you are happy to see return.
