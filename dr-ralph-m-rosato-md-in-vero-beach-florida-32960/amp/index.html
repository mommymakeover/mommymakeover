Previous abdominal float - do I need a revision? If so, what?
Abdominoplasty/ umbilical float
Jul 31, 2020
An abdominoplasty/umbilical float operation will benefit a very select patient. The criteria for this operation is a patient has minimal loose skin, thin, and has a high umbilicus to start with.

A full abdominoplasty is required to produce a flat abdomen in your case. THe umbilicus might need to be reconstructed if it was "floated". You need to find a very experienced surgeon that has done this surgery before, good luck.

Follow-up question: Do my chest measurements match my Mentor implant well?
correct implants
Jul 31, 2020
Based on your photo and measurements it appears that your breast bone (sternum) is wide (muscles are far apart)so there will be a gap between the breasts when you are not wearing a bra. A larger implant will not help this very much. As long as the breasts have a soft capsule they should look not look stuck on.
Do I have tuberous breasts? Because the shape look weird
Not tuberous
Jul 31, 2020
AN examination is required to be sure but it appears that you do not have a tuberous breast. The bottom part of the breast is under developed. If you wanted this corrected an implant could help. An experienced board certified plastic surgeon would be able to give you an ideal of what is possible. Good luck.
Is there any procedure that can reduce the appearance of my high hips?
Hips
Jul 15, 2020
You appear to have excess fat in the hip area. You are an excellent candidate for liposuction. This is an out patient surgery that has a few days to a week of down time. Non surgical methods of fat reduction are not very effective i n this area. Good Luck.

Tummy tuck

Will a tummy tuck get rid of my pooch and help me get more defined abs?
Tummy tuck
Jun 30, 2020
An abdominoplasty will tighten the resting tone of your abdomen and reduce the excess skin. Some of the excess fat can be removed at the time of the tummy tuck. Exercise and diet control as your part is very important to achieve your best results. Good Luck
Looking for advice for correction of my mild tubular breasts?
Breast augmentation , tubular?
Jun 30, 2020
An examination is required to access the breast in order to determine the best plan for you. If you want smaller areola then a lift is required with an implant. If  you are ok with larger areola then a breast augmentation  alone may work. Without an examination I cannot make a specific recommendation. Your best option is to find a surgeon you like and go with their plan.
My natural breasts are a D cup already. Which size of implant do I choose to fill up the saggy skin
Breast augmentation
Jun 30, 2020
Implants can be used for sagging skin. It all depends on the location of the Nipple /areola on the breast. If the NAC is lower than the fold (use a pencil to test) then a lift is required. A large implant will look better for a short while then just look big and saggy. Talk to your plastic surgeon they will be in the best position to advise you  on the best plan for you.

nipple piercing

A donut lift and fat grafting will not correct your shape. A lift with an implant would. Alternately a lift and the use of mesh like Galaflex might avoid the use of an implant. Do not have a donut lift and fat grafting you will not be happy.
