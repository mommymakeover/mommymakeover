Wide breasts. Better results with augmentation alone or lift plus augmentation with my mommy make
Breast Augmentation and Lift Often Go Together
Jul 19, 2018
A breast augmentation with a lift will give you the volume and shape you desire.  With widely spaced breasts, I have found that implants placed over the muscle (and under the breasts) will allow the breasts and their cleavage to move closer together.  If under the muscle, implants will have a tendency to be pushed out to the sides by the inner edge of the muscles which is attached to the breast bone.   After an examination, your plastic surgeon can tell you what is best for you.

Facelift Incisions take time to heal
Apr 13, 2018
Incisions heal at their own rate!  Most will be acceptable (or easily hidden with hair or makeup) within 3 weeks or so.  Depending upon the exact procedure done, there could be some wrinkling or puckering for many more weeks.  Yes, one side could be more swollen (or bruised) than the other.  Final scar maturation occurs over at least 12 months.  Discuss your concerns with your plastic surgeon, as he is the one who is best able to put it all together and help you through the healing period.
