Would this work? Butt implants and breast augmentation via fat transfer?
Best option for breast and buttock enhancement
Sep 21, 2019
Unfortunately, you do not seem to have enough fat to increase your breast size 2 cup sizes via fat transfer. The resorption of fat in the breast after fat transfer is more significant than in the buttock, so in general, a 2 cup size enhancement is rarely possible. Your best option would seem to be breast augmentation with implants. Not only will that accomplish your goals, but it will be much more simple to execute. Hope this helps - Sam Jejurikar, MD

Sep 16, 2019

Is 50 years old safe to do a tummy tuck?
50 years old and wanting a tummy tuck
Jul 8, 2019
Age, in of itself, is not a contraindication for tummy tuck. The existence of health problems, which is associated with higher ages, does make tummy tuck more risky, can be a contraindication for surgery. These can include issues including, but not limited to, diabetes, heart disease, kidney disease, etc.In regards to your BMI, although your chances of wound healing complications are slightly higher, it should not prevent you from having the procedure performed.Hope this helps! Sam Jejurikar, MD

Lower abdominal liposuction
