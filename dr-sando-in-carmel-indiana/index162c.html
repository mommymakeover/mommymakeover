General Anesthesia Vs. Iv Sedation for Breast Augmentation?
Preferred Anesthesia for Breast Augmentation
Sep 28, 2016
General anesthesia has been preferred if the breast implant is inserted beneath the pectoralis major muscle. Either anesthetic approach generally is fine if the implant is placed atop the muscle beneath the gland.
What Are the Effects if I Keep Wearing my Sports Bra After Breast Augmentation?
good bra support
Sep 28, 2016
Good bra support helps to counteract the downward deleterious effects of gravity. Ordinarily a sports bra is fine.
Loss of Sensation After Breast Augmentation?
risk of nipples/breast numbness
Sep 28, 2016
Yes, there is a risk of nipple or breast numbness from breast augmentation surgery, although the vast majority of patients do not experience this complication. The larger the implant, the greater the risk of a long-term bruise-or-stretch-related numbness.
What happens if you get sick with a cough or cold before surgery?
Possible cold before surgery?
Sep 28, 2016
If a cough or a cold is felt to be from an infection, (e.g. rather than allergic symptoms), then it is important to distinguish between a viral or bacterial type. If there is reason to believe it is bacterial, or there is uncertainty about its being one or the other, then it is prudent to postpone surgery. One wants to avoid any contamination and infection of an implant by a blood-borne spread of bacteria.
How soon can a person return to normal activity after a traditional Facelift?
Normal activity after a traditional facelift
Aug 12, 2016
One should avoid straining, head lowering, salty foods the first week after surgery. One may return to more aggressive exercise two weeks after surgery, but should not be surprised in seeing transient swelling after such vigorous activities.
What is a traditional facelift?
traditional facelift
Aug 12, 2016
A traditional facelift has described surgical rejuvenation of neck, cheeks, and temples. A lot of surgeons now use the terms lower facelift for treatment of neck and cheeks, and browlift for treatment of the temples.
Breast Lift versus Breast Augmentation
​Criteria for mastopexy (breast lift):
Aug 8, 2016
Criteria for mastopexy (breast lift): Position of nipple….low-lying nipple, along with gland resting below level of breast fold, suggest need for mastopexy. Specific measurements need to be taken to individualize discussion/recommendations.
Advice for Exercise After Breast Augmentation?
Exercise after Breast Augmentation surgery
Aug 8, 2016
If under the muscle pocket for implant, I suggest running, cycling, elliptical, lower body, core, toning reps for biceps and triceps as early as desired with good sports bra support,, but avoidance of vigorous pectoral activities (e.g. push-ups, pull-ups, pec-fly, bench press) for 2 weeks.
How Do You Massage Breasts After Breast Augmentation?
Massage?
Aug 8, 2016
With under the muscle pocket placement of implants, external massage not needed as occurs internally with muscle (arm) usage.
At What Age Do Your Breast Stop Developing?
​Age for breasts to stop developing:
Aug 8, 2016
It is highly variable, varying from mid-teenage to early twenties, affected by some medications like corticosteroids or female hormones. Lifetime changes also occur through pregnancies, weight changes, aging.
Breast Augmentation Recovery: What Can I Expect?
Basic Recovery questions
Aug 8, 2016
Typically patients take off one week from work, although heavy machinery use or heavy lifting greater than 40 pounds may still be uncomfortable. Swelling lasts 6-8 weeks. Scars require up to 12-18 months to fade.
Is there any need to have some one for helping the person after tummy tuck at nights?
Help after Tummy Tuck surgery
Dec 2, 2013
Tummy tuck challenges are greatest the first 24 hours while learning how to navigate up out of and back into bed... so a helper is recommended. Afterward, help with a child includes judgments which can be a bit clouded by medications, and are very individualized. With wall repair (pregnancies stretch the wall) heavy lifting should be restricted for several weeks. After any surgery, especially tummy tuck, getting up and moving around are crucial for the safest and fastest recovery.
