Wide nose. Would it be possible to have rhinoplasty even if I have thick skin?
Thick skin rhinoplasty
Apr 27, 2020
First of all, you're gorgeous. Based off your pictures I can appreciate some of you concerns about you shape. Quick answer. Yes you can improve the shape. The skin thickness is more relevant to the appearance of your tip after rhinoplasty. Which you mention you like. Taking down the top of your nose (the dorsum) and narrowing the bones (osteotomies) would achieve the results you're looking for. I would typically approach this through a closed approach and your tip would be unaltered. 
What procedures would I be able to do to correct my profile? Specifically, nose & chin
Rhinoplasty
Apr 27, 2020
Very good question. Yes. A rhinoplasty would help. Your nose fine but lacks a couple of things: dorsal aesthetic lines, poor tip projection and lateral contour. To correct it would likely require supporting the tip and dorsum (top of nose) with cartilage.Regarding your chin, an implant would be the easiest to contour and improve your profile. Some surgeons may consider moving your chin forward also (genioplasty). 
Is it best for me to have a tummy tuck or a Brazilian butt lift done?
BBL or tummy tuck
Apr 27, 2020
Very common question. I agree with the other surgeon's that you're going to find the best result the closer you are to your ideal weight. Regarding which is best, I would believe a tummy tuck would be a more powerful reshaping procedure for you than a BBL.
I am looking for recommendations for the best approach to help smooth these cheek lines?
Face lines
Apr 27, 2020
Based on your pictures, a combination of volume (fillers) and skin therapy would best. Lasers/skin rejuvenation would be your best bet if you're budgeting. Fillers are temporary and really only mask the issue at hand which is loss of skin elasticity. Make sure you choose a provider that offers a range of treatments: filler, resurfacing, etc. These offices  usually give the most appropriate advice.
