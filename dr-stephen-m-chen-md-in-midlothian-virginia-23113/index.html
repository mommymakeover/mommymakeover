En Bloc Breast Implant Removal?
Is en bloc removal of capsule and implants the best????
Sep 27, 2017
Thanks for this question.  The "en bloc" removal of implants refers to the removal of the capsule and implants in one piece without violating the capsule.  The factors that will determine if this is possible are the amount of remaining breast tissue as well as the size of the revision incision.  The most important factor is meticulous removal but not at the expense of sacrificing too much breast tissue.

Will Ultrashape work on "visceral fat"?
What works on Visceral Fat
Feb 16, 2017
The only thing that works for visceral fat is weight loss.  This layer of fat surrrounds organs and is not affected by surgery.  If you have a large amount of visceral fat, this will preclude and abdominoplasty as the shape will resemble more of a barrel rather than a sculpted abdomen.
