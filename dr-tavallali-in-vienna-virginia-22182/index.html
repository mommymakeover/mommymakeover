I really want to know which type well good for my body type (360 tummy tuck or fleur de lis)?
Tummy tuck
May 12, 2021
In my opinion an extended tummy tuck will get you the best result. A fleur -de -lis will remove more skin from the central abdomen but the nasty scar is not worth it when you can get a great result with a horizontal scar and still be able to wear a bikini! If it is just in clothes that you care about you will get a tighter belly with the vertical scar but I don't think its worth it.
Is a Donut lift my best option?
Donut lift
May 12, 2021
A donut lift is in my opinion one of the worst procedures ever invented. The scars are unsightly and often spread and the breast does not achieve a long lasting lift. You are better off with a vertical mastopexy.
How much fat is viable to used per liter with liposuction?
BBL fat
May 12, 2021
For every liter of fat that is suctioned with tumescent liposuction in my practice, only 800 cc is of a good enough quality for injection. Of that we know that only 80% will survive after a week or so inside the buttocks. The idea is to put as much viable fat as possible with a little extra for the anticipated loss. Different surgeons will treat the fat in different ways and get different volumes- Ask you surgeon what they do to that fat and why.
Should I do drainless TT or utilize a provider that always uses drains?
Drain-less tummy tuck
May 12, 2021
There is nothing wrong with using drains with a tummy tuck- it's just that a drain-less tummy tuck is in my opinion better! Less trouble , less pain, quicker showering, less risk of seroma formation, and a better scar as the tension of the wound is spread over a larger surface than just the incision. Used drains for 15 years- Drain-less for 15 and never going back! No difference with the breast surgery.

facelift?

Would I be a successful candidate for a lower facelift and neck lift?
Lower Facelift

Yes- you are a good candidate and the two procedures are actually part of a lower facelift / mini facelift and are done together. Tour jowls are your main problem and lifting them will also add volume to your face and cheek.

Is Baxter Glue recommended for a face and neck lift?
Glue with facelift
Nov 2, 2020
Applying glues to the undersurface of the skin after a facelift is associated with less bleeding but facelifts have been performed for 100 years without the glue, and with no particular increased risk of bleeding. It may be a nice thing to have but I don't believe it is particularly necessary.
Which order is best If you are using two different surgeons, facelift then fat transfer or vi
Fat or face lift?
Nov 2, 2020
Usually most surgeons will perform fat transfer at the same time as a facelift as an ancillary procedure. Remember the fat is a temporary solution at best lasting at most 1 - 2 years. Having said that if you must go to two different surgeons, (which frankly is unusual), do the facelift first and wait at least a year before doing any fact injections as you will be swollen in any case.

What procedures would you recommend for my face?
Facelift or ?
Nov 2, 2020
From your photos it looks like you have too much skin laxity in your neck and jowls. Age is not important in deciding when to do surgery- what is important is your skin elasticity and amount of fat. You could be a candidate for just a liposuction of the neck or more likely a lower face lift ( neck and jowls ). See a board certified plastic surgeon to get a recommendation after your skin elasticity has been felt.
Which is the permanent technique for mid-facelift?
Permanent facelift??
Nov 2, 2020
Beware of anyone telling you a facelift is permanent or lasting longer than 7-10 years. Though sub-periosteal facelifts which are typically performed by endoscopic technique move you facial muscles , they do not address the aging of the skin which will occur in time irrespective of technique used.
