Fat removed in drain-less tummy tuck

The amount of fat removed in a drain-less vs drain tummy tuck is the same in my practice .It has no effect on the volume that can be removed. As an outpatient surgery though you can only remove 5 litres safely.

BBL without tummy tuck

So, if you want fat removed from the front of the belly do a tummy tuck...but I think you have enough fat for a BBL by suction of the fat of your hips and back which will not need a tummy tuck and still give...
Are drainless tummy tucks safe?
Drain-less tummy tucks
Aug 18, 2020
I performed tummy tucks with drains for 20 years and for the past tens have been performing them Drain-less. Why? , less risk of infection as the drain that carries fluid out can also allow bacteria in, less chance of seroma, less pain for patients, ability to shower in 2 days, and a better scar with less tension on it. Clearly a better technique in my opinion.
Would a mini tummy tuck work to to get rid of my disproportionate loose skin and tighten my a
Tummy tuck
Aug 17, 2020
A full tummy tuck would definitely give you the best result. The scar for a mini and full tummy tuck will be the same, but you will get a lessor result as the muscles will not be tightened to the same degree.
What do you recommend for tummy tuck and lipo?
Tummy tuck options
Jun 6, 2020
You have the typical symptoms that would be best treated with a full tummy tuck and lipo of the flanks. The incision for a full and mini tuck are the same. The full tuck will also allow your muscles to be tightened from your chest to your pubis. A mini tuck will allow muscles above the belly button to be tightened. Finally the looseness of your skin means that removing just an inch or two of skin will leave you wishing you had more removed! 
Should I get a TT or BBL 1st?
Tummy tuck or BBL
May 22, 2020
It is a great pity that you can't do both at the same time as that would give you the best result.  I include liposuction of the flanks in my tummy tuck so if you were to get only the BBL that would be a second surgery. If you must do one or the other, do the BBL first. You do have enough fat for injection if you do the BBL first with fat from the love handles. Later when you do the tummy tuck you could get more fat injected
