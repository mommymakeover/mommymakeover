Is it possible to get 4 procedures done at one time?

Aug 6, 2017
In your case, I would advise doing the bbl first for several reasons:(1). You can maximize the amount of fat that can be obtained from abdomen,flanks,and thighs etc..(2).The pressure exerted on you buttocks after a tummy tuck may harm the newly transferred fat.
