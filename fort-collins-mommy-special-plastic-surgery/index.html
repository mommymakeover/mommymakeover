How much does a mommy makeover cost?

Individually, the average surgeon’s fee are reported each year by ASPS (The American Society of Plastic Surgeons). Operating room fees and anesthesia fees are added on to this cost.

If you look at the average cost per procedure without any discounts for multiple procedures, here are the 2013 national averages for the surgeon’s fee:

  • breast augmentation: $3678
  • breast lift: $4207
  • abdominoplasty: $5217
  • liposuction: $2866

Yes, combination for Lipo and Tummy Tuck is OK

It is typical to perform liposuction at the same time as tummy tuck. The only limitation has to do with the central area of the abdomen, as the other doctors have described.

I can do gentle liposuction of the upper or lower abdomen but would not recommend aggressive liposuction to this area at the same time as the tummy tuck.

There is no problem having liposuction to other areas such as hips, under the bra strap area of the back, inner or outer thighs. (Robert B. Pollack, MD, La Jolla Plastic Surgeon)

Liposuction and tummy tucks are routinely performed at the same time.

Dr Andrew Wolfe Mom Plastic Surgery Package

Combining these two procedures is becoming quite common for patients who are in good health.

When performed together, the liposuction typically focuses on sculpting the hip and flank regions (love handles), which complements the aesthetic effects achieved by the tummy tuck, resulting in a more defined waist and a smoother, toned torso.

It’s a bit more risky to perform liposuction on the abdomen during a tummy tuck, requiring caution and a specialized technique known as lipoabdominoplasty.

A consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon who has a documented history of combining these procedures can help you decide what will best suit your needs. (Frank Campanile, MD, Denver Plastic Surgeon)

Marriage of liposuction and abdominoplasty. they can live happily after but…

Like any good marriage, both procedures can be together while respecting the boundaries of each other. There are studies demonstrating that the blood supply is preserved even after a simultaneous abdominoplasty. However, caution is needed on preventive aggressive behavior. No excessive pulling, nor excessive lipo will keep the combination safe and happily ever after. Just like marriage. (Julio Clavijo-Alvarez, MD, Pittsburgh Plastic Surgeon)

Same Time Liposuction and Tummy Tuck

In most cases and in an expert board certified plastic surgeon’s hands it is Safe to do Liposuction and Tummy Tuck at the Same Time. Care must be taken to not compromise the blood supply to the lower tummy skin but I have never found this to be a problem in the more than 25 years performing both procedures together. Most of my TT procedures involve liposuction.

Frequently, additional enhancing surgeries such as liposuction to improve the waistline or other regions, pubis lift or breast surgery can be performed at the same time without delaying your recovery period. Please discuss with your doctor if this applies to you. (Larry S. Nichter, MD, MS, FACS, Orange County Plastic Surgeon)

Liposuction and a tummy tuck

You can certainly do liposuction at the same time as a tummy tuck. Some modifications to technique may be required to obtain the best results in a safe manner. The best result of any cosmetic procedure is to customize it to the patient’s individual needs. if they have a roll that needs to be suctioned to go from a good result to an amazing result then I would typically do so. (Rodger Shortt, FRCSC, Toronto Plastic Surgeon)

Liposuction and tummy tuck at same time

The tummy tuck can be done along with liposuction as long as the tummy tuck flap blood vessels are not compromised which would lead to healing issues and possible tissue loss. The hips and flanks are often liposuctioned at the same time as a tummy tuck but care must be taken in the tummy tuck tissues themselves. Most surgeons will conservatively treat the abdomen at the same time or stage liposuction of the abdomen to prevent healing problems. (Donald Nunn, MD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)
