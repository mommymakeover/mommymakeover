‘Mommy Makeover’ in GA is a term used to describe the surgical procedures that are chosen to address the many body changes that a woman may experience following pregnancy.

The procedures involved in a ‘Mommy Makeover’ in Atlanta, GA can include a breast augmentation (to address breast volume loss), a breast lift (to address the breast sagging), a tummy tuck (to address the loose skin and stretching of the abdominal muscles) and possibly liposuction.
