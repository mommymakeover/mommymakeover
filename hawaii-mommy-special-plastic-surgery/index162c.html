Hawaii Mommy Special Plastic Surgery

Mommy makeover usually best in one stage

The mommy makeover in Hawaii consists of some combination of a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty), and breast surgery which could be a lift (mastopexy), augmentation with implants, or both.

Your recovery time will not be extended by combining them and if you did the abdomen and breast procedures at different times the overall time for recovery would be much longer.

Either way you will need help taking care of the kids and have to avoid lifting for a few weeks. Combining them of course presumes that your surgeon can get both procedures done in a reasonable amount of time so the anesthesia isn’t too long.

The results depend on the surgeon performing the procedure. You can have results that are equally as good if performed together or at different times.

I would recommend that you find a surgeon who is board certified in plastic surgery and an expert in the procedures who can perform the procedures at the same time to reduce the recovery time to one period versus two. Performed together this would be considered a mommy makeover in HI.

Does grade 3 ptosis make for a more difficult lift and augmentation?

The degree of ptosis does not necessarily make the augmentation/mastopexy more complex or risky. As not photos have been provided, no opinion on your specific case is possible.

The aug/pexy procedure deserves an in depth conversation with your surgeon regarding the option of staging into two separate procedures to decrease the risk of complications versus combining the procedures to avoid the cost and recovery of two procedures.

Is a tummy tuck, breast augmentation, and extensive lipo too much for 1 mommy surgery?

If you are healthy, It is generally safe to undergo a breast augmentation and tummy tuck at the same time. This is generally what we refer to as a mommy makeover in Hawaii. As a rule however, I send all patients with surgery to multiple body areas for one night of care at a recovery facility such as the Serenity aftercare in Santa Monica.

This is very important for your safety to avoid significant risks in the first night after mommy tuck. What is alarming in your question is ” … and significant liposuction…”. Limited liposction with a full tummy tuck is generally o.k., but one should be careful with too much liposuction in this context.

Do I need a tummy tuck and can it be done with breast surgery?

You would appear to be a perfect candidate for a tummy tuck and this certainly be performed with a breast lift and/or a breast augmentation. You would also appear to be a good candidate for using a Model 410 tear drop style implant if you prefer this. I have been using this style of implant for several years in addition to the traditional round implants.

The tear drop styled implants are the true gummy bear , cohesive style that is commonly discussed as giving such a natural look to the breasts. You can discuss this with your chosen Plastic Surgeon.

You seem to be a good candidate for a mommy makeover. This combination of procedures includes tummy tuck, liposuction and breast augmentation which can usually be done at the same time.

Good candidate for a tummy tuck with Liposuction of the flanks

You do appear to be a good candidate for a full tummy tuck with rectus plication (muscle tightening) liposuction of the flanks. There would be no other way to rid yourself of the loose abdominal skin.

As you probably already know as you are in great shape, no amount of exercise will tighten your skin. However, the mommy tuck is a trade-off to some degree; loose skin for a lower abdominal and periumbilical scar with a flatter, tighter tummy. How long should you wait to go swimming after mommy makeover

I would wait at least four weeks after a tummy tuck and breast surgery before going into a pool, jacuzzi or the ocean. There are a lot of bacteria in these bodies of water (especially a jacuzzi) which could contaminate the incision and lead to an infection.
