Illinois Mommy Makeover Surgery

Mommy makeovers in Illinois may or may not include breast implants

Mommy makeovers don’t necessarily have to include breast implants. I have done many breast lifts as part of the mommy makeover and by using the patient’s own tissue, the breasts can get a fuller look and more cleavage.

It does appear as though by just doing a lift your will get nice improvement in the shape and upper volume of your breasts.

If you want even more cleavage and fullness then you would need implants. During a consultation, I could give you an estimate of how your breasts would look with and without implants.

There are lumpers and there are splitters in this world of plastic surgery. Obviously, an in person physical exam with a detailed history, lab work, EKGs, and knowing the quality of the support group that will be assisting you after momy lift in Illinois will help determine whether to put the two surgeries together or do them separately.

The interesting thing about anesthesia is that it is very much like flying a plane. The only real potential problems in anesthesia occur at the beginning and end, and therefore one anesthesia of 6 hours should be potentially less dangerous than two anesthesias of 3 hours each because you have doubled the number of takeoffs and landings.

The concept of the Mommy Makeover in Illinois is that multiple procedures can be done safely at the same time in a certified surgery center by a highly trained Board Certified plastic surgeon assisted by a Board Certified anesthesiologist and an outstanding surgical team.

Determining Candidacy For A Mommy Makeover

My only concern in doing these two procedures together would be the amount of tissue present on your chest and abdomen because a well done breast lift reduction tightens the upper part of the abdomen, and a well done abdominoplasty tightens the lower part of the abdomen.

If these two procedures are done at the same time, it may in certain circumstances compromise how low your surgeon can place your abdominoplasty incision. In today’s world, with abdominoplasty incisions, the lower the better.

I recommend you do a full tummy tuck with muscle repair. I think you would be disappointed with a mini-tummy tuck. The scar would not be much longer.

You will get great results with a Mommy Makeover in IL! If you are happy with the volume of your breasts, do just the lift. If you are looking for additional volume, you could also consider fat grafting to the breast to give a slight volume increase without implants. Talk to your surgeon about grafting if you want to avoid implants.

You’ll still be able to maintain your same volume, only with a perkier, more youthful appearance. Know that a breast lift won’t be able to increase your volume, though, so as long as that is in line with your goals for your final results, you should do well with a breast lift alone.

Full muscle repair with mini tummy tuck and umbilical float

In general, a mini tummy tuck only improves the skin tone in the lower abdomen. If there is a muscle separation, it can be repaired all the way up using a variation called umbilical float.

While an in person exam would be necessary to determine what type of tummy tuck is best for you; only certain candidates are ideal for a mini tuck (those with high belly button, and those with little skin excess above the belly button).

Mommy Lift Most Often Treats Separated Abdominal Muscles

If there needs to be a lot of muscle tightening I prefer a traditional tummy tuck, and the scar is not much long than a mini tuck. Regarding the breasts, I would have a plastic surgeon make sure the shape of your breasts will still be good after only an implant, if there is a log of excess skin or the nipples are low and very asymmetric than a lift might help correct these.

Tummy tuck and breast lift

I routinely perform these two procedures at the same time for women who are looking to return their bodies to the pre-pregnancy state. Each surgeon’s opinion on the matter varies though, and you want to be sure that you have chosen a surgeon who is board certified in plastic surgery and is comfortable performing these procedures together.

Mommy Surgeries May Be Performed Together, Or The Treatment Plan May Be Split Up Into Several Different Phases

It is quite routine to perform a breast reduction and tummy tuck in the same operation – they go well together. Inevitably, the overall operative time is longer but in a healthy patient that should not be an issue. The potential benefit in terms of avoiding two separate operations and therefore two separate recovery times if considerable.

Combined procedures

I do agree that you have a slightly increased risk of complications with combined procedures. All surgeons are going to have a different comfort level, but if you are healthy and not significantly overweight you should be a candidate for both. I think you should talk to someone with more than ten years experience. I also think that six hours is longer than average.

Mommy Makeover costs and scar location

In performing a tummy tuck as part of a Mommy Makeover, I will place the incision as low as possible and in most cases this is in or at the top of the public hair line (7-8 cm above the vagina).

The length of the scar will be as short as possible, while still removing the excess/loose skin over the abdomen and/or hips.
