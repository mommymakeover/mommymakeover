What is the best injectable filler for undereye hollows?
Best filler for undereye
Jul 22, 2019
Thank you for your question!
I would recommend hyaluronic acid injectable fillers for the undereye area as these fillers are reversible if necessary. Filler selection is important in this delicate area and should be based on the results you are trying to achieve as well as individual patient anatomy. In general, I prefer to use Belotero in this area although this is dependent on individual patient goals.
