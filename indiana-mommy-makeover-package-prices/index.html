Indiana Mommy Makeover Package Prices

A mommy makeover in Indiana usually includes a breast lift with/without implants and a tummy tuck. You may want to get several opinions as to whether or not you’re a candidate for everything. Then, you can decide if you want to do both together or apart.

Combining a tummy tuck and a breast lift is certainly reasonable to be done under the same anesthesia. The mommy makeover procedure will typically take between 4 and 6 hours. From your photo, I would recommend a breast lift.

You do not need additional volume that an implant would provide, but rather, repositioning the nipple on the breast will give your a more youthful look. It is always a good idea to get a number of opinions from different surgeons and different perspectives.

Combined procedures for “mommy makeover”

There are plusses and minuses for combining procedures. There is the benefit of shared recovery time, but there is the added risk of a longer anesthetic.

Generally a “mommy makeover” combines abdominoplaty with bresat lift with or without implants. This is a safe combination of proceduresif done expiditiously.

How to stage a mommy makeover?

The surguries can be done either in combination or in stages. There is simply no right answer on whether to combine them or, if not, how to order them. Factors to consider are: which bothers you more? Will you be having more children? Would you wait until the children are old enough to not be lifted before doing the tummy tuck?

The answers to these questions have to be measured against the increased overall cost and two separate recovery periods for separating the surgeries. My advice is that unless you are interested in having a whole lot of surgery right now, you are on the right track by planning to stage them.

Mommy makeover

While typically thought of as breast and abdominal surgery to repair some of the changes caused by pregnancy (i.e. deflation and sagging of the breasts, and laxity of the abdominal skin and muscle), it is not a specific procedure as one would think, like an appendectomy.

You can, and should, decide on having whatever cosmetic or reconstructive procedure you think will benefit you. Don’t worry what you call it.

Mommy Makeover and the Gym

The short answer is to wait for approximately six weeks. You will want everything to heal strong enough to handle the increased stresses.

Walking each day after the procedure is very important and will provide some “exercise” as you become more comfortable and can go for longer distances.

6 weeks is the average recovery time before going back to the gym. Light cardio is ok after 4 weeks. But bending, pushing and lifting weights is not recommended.

Jacuzzi after Mommy Makeover

In general, I ask my patients to wait until all incision lines have completely healed prior to going into the pool, jacuzzi or ocean. On average it can take 4-6 weeks.

Swimming after breast lift and tummy tuck

It is expected that you will have some thin healed tissue for probably several months after the procedures you describe. You definitely want to wait until all of the incisions are completely healed before submerging your incisions, since there are some strange organisms that live in pool, lake, and ocean water which can cause slow infections that are difficult to diagnose and treat.

Who Is A Good Candidate For A Mommy Makeover in Indiana?

You appear to be an excellent candidate for a Mommy Makeover based on the photographs you submitted. However, an in person exam and review of your history would determine if, in fact, you are medically ready for both the surgery and the recovery.

The Mommy Makeover in Indiana usually refers to four basic surgeries:

1. Tummy tuck with repair of separated abdominal muscles
2. Breast surgery, usually a breast lift with implants
3. Liposuction
4. Labiaplasty

The above four surgeries, or any combination of two or more, is usually referred to as a Mommy Makeover. From your photographs, I believe you would do well with a breast lift with small implants, a tummy tuck with muscle repair, and liposuction of multiple areas.
