Curtis William Fisher II, DO

Name: Curtis William Fisher II, DO
Position: Practice Manager
Biography: Dr. Fisher is a well known and respected Family Practice physician in Pekin Illinois, where he has practiced for 25 plus years. Seeing many of his patients suffer from illness due to weight, he searched for a program he could offer them that actually worked, and he found it in the Robard New Directions system. Having seriously considered a specialty in plastic surgery, Dr. Fisher was excited to learn all the good he could do in the cosmetic medical field, and added aethestics to his many services, including injectables, lasers, etc. Finally, at a routine medical conference, he was introduced to the growing field of study in bio identical hormones, and embarked in a training course that taught him the amazing benefits of hormone replacement therapy and optimization. Dr. Fisher was born and raised in Illinois, served in the military during Desert Storm, and returned to Illinois after his service. He stands ready to offer his patients and clients many options to meet their goals.
