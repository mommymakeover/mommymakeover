Ireland Mom Tummy Tuck

Mommy makeover in Ireland is tummy tuck plus breast surgery. Typically the abdominal area and the breast suffer the most after pregnancy, so the mommy makeover rejuvenates those with a tummy tuck and breast surgery. That would include a lift, implants, or both, and the cost would depend on the specific plan

You can have a tummy tuck with mesh.

A combination of a tummy tuck and breast lift procedure is performed routinely for patients as a “mommy makeover” operation. Based on the anticipated surgical time, and assuming you to be a good candidate, these two procedures can be safely performed

You can always stage the procedures if you would like but the tummy tuck will likely be your greatest source of recovery time needed.

Mommy makeover- combined procedures

This is a very common combination of procedures to do together. As long as you are determined to be healthy enough for mommy surgery, it’s likely you could combine the procedures. Longer procedures can have more risk of developing blood clots, so please discuss this with your surgeon and learn about what can be done to prevent this complication

Regarding the recovery, I often find that adding breast surgery to a tummy tuck does not significantly increase recovery time. The recovery is mostly the abdomen, and it’s likely that doing your breasts at the same time would not make your tummy tuck recovery any longer

This is one of the advantages of combining procedures – similar recovery, but having both done at once.

Mini tummy tuck/breast lift

It can definitely be done it’s very common to perform this procedures at once, please consult a board certified PS

Both combined surgery and staged surgery can be safe and effective. Four hours of surgery is not a lot of time and the combination of the two surgeries is well tolerated and safe for most people

In the end it will be your personal choice. The benefit of doing two surgeries is that the second surgery can be used to fine tune the results of the first surgery if needed.

It’s actually quite common to combine surgical procedures without any additional complications in recovery time. Whether or not you choose to have the procedures together or separate, most surgeons will agree that it will depend on how comfortable you, the patient, feels

It may be best to discuss your thoughts and concerns with your surgeon to come up with a surgical plan that makes the most sense to you.

Most patients in good health can have a combined breast lift and mini tummy tuck with very low complication rates. It’s important to walk early and often to minimize the risk of DVT (deep vein thrombosis) which is a blood clot in the legs

Sleeping on your back is best!

After breast augmentation, breast lift and tummy tuck it is very important to initially limit your sleeping to on your back and little elevated (like in a recliner chair) the reason for this is to avoid excess stress on the incision lines that could pop stiches, and to prevent gravity from pooling too much swelling in one area that could delay healing or again, stress the incisions and make stiches pop

This is most important for the first couple of weeks, and becomes less important the further that you get form surgery and the more healing that has occurred. The exact timing for lifting that restriction can vary greatly depending on exactly how your procedure was done and how quickly you are healing

In my practice I usually start to let patients sleep partially tipped on their side at about 2 weeks, but you want to change position frequently and not sleep all the way on one side or stay there all night long until about a month post op

Cosmetic vs. reconstructive surgery

Augmentation mammaplasty is a cosmetic procedure and not covered by insurance, unless it is part of an overall plan for reconstruction after mastectomy for breast cancer. A tummy tuck is also cosmetic, although if severe skin breakdown occurs as a result of overhanging skin, a panniculectomy, which is a more limited operation, would be covered

Swelling after tummy tuck

Swelling is absolutely normal for months after a tummy tuck. It will begin to subside slowly – At 6 weeks, it will be much better. But final swelling takes 6 – 12 months to go away

Mommy Tuck Is A Cosmetic Procedure That Can Gift Your Younger Self Back

This is something we can’t change – it is the nature of wound healing. A garment will give you support if you are not wearing one. The swelling is in the lower abdomen because gravity pulls the swelling down

Cosmetic surgery and Medicaid

Medicaid does not cover cosmetic surgery. You may qualify for a panniculectomy of skin/fat apron removal, but not a tummy tuck

If these orocedures are done for cosmetic enhancement of a normal body then they are cosmetic in nature and not covered by insurance.

Breast augmentation and tummy tuck are both cosmetic and not medical in nature. Since these procedures are not treating or correcting a medical condition, they are not covered by insurance.

Insurance coverage of breast augmentation and abdominoplasty

If the mommy makeup is done as part of a treatment plan for cancer. Otherwise they are cosmetic procedures and there is no reason why taxpayers should cover the bill through medicaid.
