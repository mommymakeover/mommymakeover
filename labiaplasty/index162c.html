Mommy Surgery And Labiaplasty

A labia plasty can be done at the same time as a mommy makeover.

The labia plasty can easily be done at the same time as a mommy makeover. It certainly has a bit of discomfort but doesn’t have much physiologically to the procedure. (Vincent N. Zubowicz, MD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)

There is not set procedure with a mommy makeover though it most often covers tummy tuck and breast enhancement, lift or augmentation. There is no reason labiaplasty cannot be combined into your ideal makeover with a single recovery if you wish. (Peter E. Johnson, MD, Chicago Plastic Surgeon)

There are many methods to perform a good labiaplasty. Done well with preservation of the normal anatomy, it will add another hour to hour and a half to the procedure. So that would depend on the expertise of your surgeon. (Samir Shureih, MD, Baltimore Plastic Surgeon)

Without an examination, it is impossible to say what you may or may not be a candidate for all in one setting. It is nice to combine everything into one surgical setting so that the recovery time is all in one stage. None of those surgeries interfere with recovery from the other, so they can all work well together. However, there are some patients who need so much work that the time under anesthesia would be longer than is deemed safe for an elective procedure. Some surgeons will put patients under anesthesia for 6-7 hours for an elective surgery, which really increases the surgical and anesthetic risks. The best thing to do is to go for multiple consultations with board certified plastic surgeons and see what is recommended. I do a lot of these combinations, but it has to be in the right patient. (Christopher V. Pelletiere, MD, Barrington Plastic Surgeon)

Combine Mommy Makeover Procedure With Labiaplasty

You should of course discuss this with your surgeon, but speaking in general a mommy makeover can be combined with a labiaplasty, without particular problems. (Ciro Adamo, PhD, MD, Italy Plastic Surgeon)

While getting a mommy makeover, Is it possible to also have a labiaplasty at the same time?

Talk to your surgeon about the requisite time needed in the OR to do all of your procedures, but yes, all of these could be done in a single setting if you are an otherwise healthy individual. (Nelson Castillo, MD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)

Mommy Makeover with labiaplasty

Ask your plastic surgeon the time estimate for all of the procedures. It is possible to perform them at the same time. If the total time is over seven hours, consider a 23 hour admission. (Michelle Hardaway, MD, Farmington Plastic Surgeon)

Having a labiaplasty at the same time as mommy make over.

In general, yes, this surgery can be added to a typical mommy makeover. However, your surgeon will have to determine how much time will be needed for the entire operation to make sure that this can be done safely.especially if doing it as an outpatient procedure. (Jeffrey G. Lind II, MD, The Woodlands General Surgeon)

Mommy Makeover and Labiaplasty

If you are in excellent health and a good candidate for mommy makeover surgery, most likely it is possible to have a labiaplasty at the same time. The major consideration is how much anesthesia time you would require to achieve each of your goals. Ideally you would not have longer than six hours of surgery as beyond this you are more likely to have post operative complications. Additionally, if you need an extensive abdominal recontouring with significant repositioning of your muscles, then it may be more risky to add an additional surgery. Consult a board certified plastic surgeon who following a review of your medical history and a physical exam will be able to tell you if you are a candidate. (Carlin Vickery, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)

While getting a mommy makeover, Is it possible to also have a labiaplasty at the same time?

It is certainly possible to do those procedures together, but it will be at the discretion of your surgeon after a complete evaluation. (Sofia K. Kirk, MD, Jacksonville Plastic Surgeon)

Tummy tuck, breast surgery and labiaplasty combination surgery

Depending on the estimated length of your surgery, the answer is ‘yes.’ I have frequently done labiaplasties with tummy tucks and mommy make-overs. However if you are having a tummy tuck, breast lift and implants and surgery is already going to take 6 hours, it may be wiser to have separate procedure. So discuss this with your surgeon – a lot depends on your weight and the exact surgery that you have. Always see a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon. (Elizabeth Morgan, MD, PhD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)
