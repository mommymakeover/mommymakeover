What is a Mommy Makeover?

A mommy makeover refers to any combination of procedures designed to restore a woman’s body to its prepregnancy state. A woman’s body undergoes a lot of changes as a result of pregnancy. These changes primarily affect the breasts and abdomen. The breast skin stretches causing the nipples to appear lower with an overall loss of breast shape and volume. This gives the appearance of “saggy” breasts. There are a number of procedures that can be performed including breast augmentation with implants, breast lift or a combination of the two to improve the overall aesthetic of the breasts.

Mommy Makeover Corrects Skin Excess From Pregnancy

A Mommy Makeover is a combination of either breast augmentation or breast lift with an abdominoplasty, both of which address the excess skin of the breasts and abdomen that can result from pregnancy and childbirth.

Breasts are affected from pregnancy often with a loss of breast volume, enlarged droopy nipple areolar complexes, both of which make the breasts appear seemingly unattractive and less youthful. The abdomen is often affected by pregnancy by the production of stretched, excess skin, and laxity of the muscles below the skin that produce the “pouch” that is often seen below the belly-button.

An abdominoplasty removes excess skin and simultaneously tightens the muscular wall, providing a youthful foundation for the tight abdomen that is produced. By performing a breast augmentation, or breast lift with or without breast augmentation, the breasts are given a more youthful appearance to compliment the abdomen completing the “mommy makover. The best way to find out if you are a good candidate for these procedures is to obtain a detailed consultation with possible options by a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon. For research purposes, look at before and after photographs of these procedures separately or in combination to get a feeling for what you can expect. (Daniel Shapiro, MD, Miami Plastic Surgeon)
