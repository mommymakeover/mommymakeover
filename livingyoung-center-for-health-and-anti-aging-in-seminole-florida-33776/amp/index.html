Katie Kaffai, CME, LE

Name: Katie Kaffai, CME, LE
Position: Business Owner
Biography: As Founder and owner of LivingYoung, Katie’s focus is on anti-aging and wellness. Her business addresses the key issues associated with aging: weight gain, wrinkles, diminished energy and loss of passion … all of which are entwined with the pursuit of wellness. As a member of the American Academy of Anti-Aging, Katie makes it her job to stay abreast of every new development that promises to provide thinness, youthfulness and healthiness. She does her research diligently and only those treatments that produce desirable and effective results ever make it onto LivingYoung’s menu. She sets LivingYoung’s standards high.
She specializes in nutrition, is a licensed Medical Esthetician and Electrologist (License EO 182), and has her CME in Advanced Laser.

On a personal note, Katie says she has a vested interest in anti-aging. As the mother of a 7-year daughter and 6-year old twins, she needs all the energy and youthfulness she can get when it comes to keeping up with the speed of children.
