Mom Plastic Surgery Los Angeles Cost

A mommy makeover procedure in Los Angeles CA is an excellent way to help counteract all of the changes that happen in a women’s body with pregnancy.

Loose skin and stretch marks like you talk about are part of the problem for most women. Loose skin is easily managed with “Resectional” surgeries like tummy tucks and body lifts to tighten up the loose sagging areas.

The trade-off is having a scar which most people, like you, would rather have. Stretch marks are a little harder problem.

There is no way to repair the strength and elasticity of the skin which is weakened in skin with stretch marks. Where the marks are comes from a kind of tearing or thinning of the deeper layer of the skin (the dermis).

As pressure is exerted on the skin from inside when your body expands from the pregnancy.Even when the pressure is back to normal after delivery and weight loss that damage in the skin remains (and looks ugly).

The only way to remove a stretch mark is to cut out the skin that it is on. This is great when they are located on skin that will be removed with a tummy tuck or breast lift, so it really depends on where those stretch marks are.

If they are outside of those areas, it doesn’t make sense to just cut out the stretch marks, because the scars that are left in those places usually are worse than the stretch marks would be. It is hard to tell on your photo, but it looks like your stretch marks extend above the area we would excise with a body lift or tummy tuck extension.

Love handles stretchmarks and extended abdominoplasty

When I see patients for a tummy tuck I make sure the sideward extension of the incision at least reaches the extent of the skin fold if one is present. You do not have sufficient excess abdominal skin to have such a fold. Therefore you are not really a good candidate for the extended abdominoplasty (extended towards the back) or a belt lipectomy type of procedure.

Of course this is not an absolute without a face to face examination to verify just how much extra skin is present. You also need to keep in mind that if you cut out the skin higher up in the love handles area you are by definition pulling on and stretching the lower skin.
