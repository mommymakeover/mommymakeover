Mommy Make Over – Tummy Tuck and Breast Augment

It appears that you have typical post-partum breast changes (atrophy and looseness) and relaxation/stretching of the abdominal wall skin and rectus muscles. The combined surgery to correct both problems constitutes a mommy-make-over. This surgery can certainly be performed safely together.

However, your initial recuperation will be challenging because of abdominal wall and chest pain. Normally after a tummy tuck, we have patients use their arm and chest muscles to help stand up from a sitting or lying position. This would be difficult right after a breast augment.

You would certainly need some help caring for your young children, and even yourself in the first few days. Unless you can arrange for help at home, I would suggest you consider separating the procedures into 2 stages.

From your photos it appears that placement of breast implants will both enlarge your breasts and lift them nicely. Also, your c-section scar can be removed at the time of the tummy tuck, and the incisional scar placed very low, hidden within a bikini line.

The separation of the rectus muscles/hernia would also be corrected creating a nice, firmer and flatter abdominal contour.

How soon can I get back in the gym for a mild workout after a mommy makeover?

In my practice, patients are able to return to light workouts about 4-6 weeks after a mommy makeover. Be sure to follow the instructions provided by your surgeon.

Typically, most surgeons would want you to wait at least 6 weeks prior to lifting any weights or engaging in any strenuous cardio exercise. However, best to check in with your operating surgeon since every surgeon may have a slightly different postop protocol.

Depending on the procedures you are having performed as part of your mommy makeover you can resume walking on a treadmill at 2 weeks. If, however, you are looking to perform weight lifting exercises you may need to wait 6 weeks or more. Your surgeon will be the best source of advice for you.

Looking For A Mommy Makeover In Louisiana
