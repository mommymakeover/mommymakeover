Mommy Makeover! What is it?

A Mommy MakeOver in Lubbock, TX is whatever it takes to give you back your Pre-Pregnancy Body!

Most commonly, this includes Breast Enhancement procedures such as Breast Implants or a Breast Lift. It also includes correction of the changes which have occurred to the abdomen and torso such as Liposuction, Tummy Tuck or Mini Tummy Tuck procedures

Some women have also included labiaplasty and Vaginal Tightening to their desires. Having kids is a great experience! B ut it does take its’ toll on a woman’s body. My goal is to help you get your old body back!

Mommy Makeover with possibility for more children

Yes, you can have a mommy makeover at the age of 22. However, if you plan on more children down the road, I recommend that you delay the muscle tightening portion of the tummy tuck until you have had all of your children

Age is not necessarily a factor in whether a patient can have a Mommy Makeover. It depends on the specific patient and their situation. I generally advise patients to make sure they are done having children before having the tummy tuck surgery

If you are planning on having additional children, a Mommy Makeover would be best delayed until after you have completed having children. A pregnancy could easily undo the result of a Mommy Makeover in Lubbock, TX. Just remember, when you are ready, find an experienced Board Certified Plastic Surgeon that you trust to transform into your new body

Breast Implants With Dr Robert P. Schmid

The main question is: are you 100% sure that you are finished having children? If you can answer that with certainty – then most plastic surgeons will probably be willing to help you with the mommy makeover.

There is no set age limit for this type of procedure per se. I recommend that patients be done with their families before undergoing a tummy tuck procedure.

That being said, if a young patient like yourself is not sure if they are done or think it may be a long period of time before they get pregnant again, then we discuss the pros and cons of proceeding with mommy tuck

It is not uncommon for a young woman to want to look and feel better after delivering a child. The biggest concern is that if you do get pregnant after a tummy tuck, your results will be permanently changed and further surgery may/will be needed
