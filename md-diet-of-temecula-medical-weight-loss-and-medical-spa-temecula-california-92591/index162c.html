Are there any tips to help maintain my filler and make it last a little longer?
How long will filler last?
Sep 12, 2018
There is no evidence to my knowledge that doing more filler will help it to last longer. Filler does not last longer the second or third time you do it, but if you are consistently getting injected every couple months the amount of product in your lips will continue to build. I find that Juvederm in the lips typically lasts 6-9 months.
