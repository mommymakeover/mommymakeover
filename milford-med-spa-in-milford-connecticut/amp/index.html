Umbilical Hernia - Could I still do Emsculpt on my abdomen area?
Sorry, not a good idea to treat
Mar 4, 2022
I would advise against this for your safety. Hernia of any kind in the treatment area is a reason to not treat with EmSculpt or EmSculpt Neo. I'd also be concerned about the effect on the fat within the hernia. The treatment results in burst fat cells, which in this case would be within the herniated area that may not be removed optimally by your body through lymphatics and circulation. I think the risk of injury would be too high unfortunately.
