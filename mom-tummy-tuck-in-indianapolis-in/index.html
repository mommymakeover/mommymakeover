Indianapolis Mommy Makeover: Is it Advisable to Stay n Hospital Overnight?

We routinely do mommy makeovers as out patient procedures. We see patients in our office the morning after surgery and the following week. You will need someone to not only take over your responsibilities at home but also to look after you.

Do as much in advance at home to prepare (shopping, laundry, meal preparation etc) and give yourself a chance to recover. Staying overnight is always a possibility, but it is expensive, and not medically necessary.

What’s included with the mommy makeover in Indianapolis

Various options of surgical combinations in a series of surgical sessions. Ie: TT, Lifts, Lipo, BBLs, FLs, etc.

Most commonly, a mommy makeover includes a breast lift with implants, a tummy tuck, and liposuction of the flanks. However, in some patients only a tummy tuck and breast implants are needed.

Indianapolis Mommy makeover and breast implants

A breast lift tightens the skin envelope of the breast as it lifts the breast. Implants add volume. You can certainly have a lift without implants as part of your mommy makeover.

A consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon can help you decide which procedure is most appropriate for you.

You appear to be a great candidate, judging by your photos, for a tummy tuck as well as breast lift +/- breast implants.

How Long Between Smoking and MOM?

Of course we would like you to quit smoking and not resume, in our practice we ask you to quit smoking thirty days prior to mommy lift.

This has been answered by many doctors already but I wanted to stress how important it is to quit smoking before any major procedure, especially a cosmetic one! It sounds like you have worked really hard to lose weight- congratulations!

Now, you HAVE to quit smoking at LEAST 6 weeks before the mommy makeover. You’ve come this far-you deserve a beautiful result and this can only happen if you quit.
