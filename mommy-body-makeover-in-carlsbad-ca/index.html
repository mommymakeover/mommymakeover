Here’s a quick summary of terms as I use them (which are not necessarily standardized): Mommy Makeover = Breast enhancement and tummy contouring Tummy Tuck = abdominal area rejuvenation in the front combined with liposuction of the hips and lower back (the power of a Tummy Tuck or any lift is through re-supporting what I call the “body suit” layer).

Body Suit = the layer of tissue responsible for providing support of the skin and connecting it to our muscles and skeleton Body Lift = Lower Body Lift = Tummy Tuck + Lateral Thigh, Posterior Thigh and Buttock Lift = a Tummy Tuck “all the way around.”

Body Lifts have the most power to restore lifting forces all-the-way-around the body, and therefore provide significant benefits in lifting the thighs and buttocks (that cannot be achieved with a Tummy Tuck alone)
