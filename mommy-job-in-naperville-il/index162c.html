Mommy Job in Naperville, IL

A mommy makeover in Naperville, IL combines breast enhancement with body contouring

A ‘mommy makeover’ isn’t one procedure, but is instead a combination of cosmetic surgeries (and sometimes nonsurgical procedures) designed to target the primary problem spots women notice after having children.

Most often, this means performing some type of breast enhancement along with body contouring like a tummy tuck or liposuction. However, each mommy makeover is tailored to the needs of the individual patient, so no two are exactly alike.

The question of whether you need a mommy makeover rather than a tummy tuck really depends on your personal goals for surgery. (Frank Campanile, MD, Denver Plastic Surgeon)

What is a Mommy Makeover?

This question is a very common one for most plastic surgeons. A mommy makeover is a combination of procedures that helps restore a woman’s body to a pre-baby state.

The mommy make over in Naperville, IL includes 2 or more of the following procedures – tummy tuck, breast augmentation, breast lift, breast lift with augmentation, liposuction, and labiaplasty. Depending on the procedures required, the cost of a mommy makeover can vary considerably.

Cost will also vary from surgeon to surgeon. Do your homework and consult with 2 or more board certified plastic surgeons to learn what options are best for you and to find a surgeon with whom you are comfortable. (Nicholas Tarola, MD, Nashville Plastic Surgeon)

What is a mommy makeover image

If the physical effects of pregnancy have left your body misshapen with sagging breasts, stretched stomach or bulging hips and thighs, “Mommy Makeover” surgery, which may include breast lift, breast augmentation, breast reduction, liposuction, tummy tuck or other body sculpting procedures, can help you take back your figure.

Because combining procedures increases the complexity of the surgery, it’s especially important to choose a board-certified plastic surgeon who is highly qualified and experienced in performing Naperville mommy makeover procedures. (Camille Cash, MD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)

Getting rid of loose skin after pregnancy

Dr Niki Christopoulos Recovery From Mommy Makeover

Loose, saggy skin can not be treated with lipo, or even laser-lipo. It can only be removed surgically. There are several options available to treat this problem.

Always remember that safety is the first concern! If the only problem is sagging skin with some a bit of excess fat, the simplest procedure is a panniculectomy, which is the removal of the skin/fat apron.

A panniculectomy is a good procedure when the abdominal wall (muscles) are not stretched or weakened, or perhaps the patient is not in the best of health.

Panniculectomy is also preferred in cases where the patient may want to have more children, or in obese patients where the panniculus is an impediment to exercise and weight loss.

In general, the cosmetic result is not as great as an abdominoplasty, but it is a worthwhile procedure when indicated. Another option is the “mini-tummy tuck”, or “mini-abdominoplasty”

The name of this procedure is somewhat confusing as it bears no relationship to the length of the scar. Rather, it refers to the amount of word performed on the muscular strength layer beneath the skin and fat layers.

Specifically, after the loose and sagging skin & fat are removed, the weakened muscle layer is repaired only between the pubic bone and the umbilicus (navel). This procedure in my opinion, is ideal for only a limited number of patients-specifically those people with a “pooch” that is isolated to the lower abdomen below the navel.

I have been in private practice since 1993 and have performed a very large number of tummy tucks, but very few patients had abdominal wall weakness limited to the lower abdomen. Full tummy tuck. This tummy tuck is the mainstay of abdominal contouring. What differentiates this from the mini version is the degree of abdominal wall repair. With the full tummy tuck, the repair extends from the pubic bone to the breast bone. The skin incision may or may not be longer than the one seen in the mini tummy tuck.

The term “mommy makeover” in Naperville is a nonspecific term as it refers to the breast. It could include a breast augmentation, a breast lift or both at the time of a tummy tuck. The decision to perform these procedures must be made carefully, knowing that multiple procedures, although more convenient, does add to the risk of complications. (Scott E. Mommy Makeover can Get Rid of Loose Sagging Skin after Pregnancy

The majority of loose sagging skin after pregnancy can be corrected on the breasts and tummy with a mommy makeover. 

The mommy makeover entails a tummy tuck which removes the excess skin of the tummy and tightens the muscles that were pulled apart by the baby.

The breasts are lifted and usually augmented at the same time to give a better shape and size to the breasts. 

Additionally, liposuction of the muffin top and flanks is performed to decrease the fat deposits in these areas. (Kent V. Hasen, MD, Naples Plastic Surgeon)
