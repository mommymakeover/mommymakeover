How safe is it to have tummy tuck and breast augmentation with lift at doctor surgical center?

If you have a surgeon certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery with privileges to operate at a major hospital, that doctor is required to operate only at a highly certified facility whether it be an actual hospital, or more commonly a licensed surgical center.

If you are seeing such a surgeon, the surgicenter will be quite safe. I have personally not done these particular surgeries in a hospital for over 20 years. The benefit is no hospital bacteria and a high degree of patient privacy as well as a large saving of your money.

Breast lift alone or Breast lift with implants to get upper pole fullness with a Mommy Makeover?

It does help to know that you are happy with the volume of your breasts. In most situations, this would mean that a breast lift alone can tighten up the breasts and proportionally reduce your areolae. However, you want upper pole fullness.

There are only two ways to get this: breast implants or a really good push-up bra after breast lift alone. That is the decision you need to make. A breast lift alone means a higher, tighter breast, but no full-time upper pole fullness without a good bra.

A breast lift with implants will get you that upper pole fullness (even without a bra), but you add the long-term extra variable of breast implants and your breasts will be larger than they are now (unless you put in a tiny implant!).

An honest discussion with your PS can help you understand the pros and cons of adding the breast implants.
