Mommy Lift Plastic Surgery In Reno, NV

Mommy Makeover in Reno, NV refers to some combination of breast and abdominal surgery to reverse the effects of pregnancy in these two areas.

Usually it means having a tummy tuck and then some combination of either breast implants, or a breast lift, or both, depending upon how much your breasts have changed with pregnancy and your individual goals and preferences.

Implants for the most part do not lift the breast. So you may actually be a better candidate for a breast lift rather than an implant.

Cost of a mommy makeover in Reno, NV

Costs will vary but for breast implant surgery and a tummy tuck I would suspect $12-15K depending on where you live.

These procedures are commonly combined and performed safely in outpatient surgery centers. Several factors are important to ensure safety. The center should be accredited. The surgeon should be certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery.

You should not have any major health issues or prior postoperative problems. You should be aware of possible complications including blood clots (DVT). Your doctor should have admitting privileges at a local hospital in case you need to be admitted overnight after mom plastic surgery or if serious complications arise.

Most patients do well after these surgeries if they are good surgical candidates.
