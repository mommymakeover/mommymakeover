Mother Makeover Cincinnati Cost

Costs for a mommy makeover in Cincinnati will certainly vary depending amongst surgeons as well as on your geographic location.

Things to factor in from the surgical standpoint will be the facility fee, anesthesia fee, surgeon fee, other fees (e.g., liposuction, implants, surgical garment, addition of other surgical procedures, etc.).

A range in price ~$9000-17000 is not unreasonable. $10-12K would be a reasonable cost on average. A consultation with a plastic surgeon will help you in determining which procedure(s) would be the right one for you.

Cost also depends on the complexity of your case and how much work would need to be done to achieve an aesthetically-pleasing result for you.

“Mommy Makeover” Down Time

Dr Kurtis Martin Breast Augmentation With Tummy Tuck

A “Mommy Makeover” in Cincinnati (abdominoplasty / breast augmentation / mastopexy) is a significant surgical undertaking. As such, patients should anticipate the need for several week of recovery.

Generally patients can expect at least 6 weeks off of aggressive activity (strenuous exercise, heavy lifting).

I encourage patients to anticipate the need for help around the house (especially with small children). All patients will have some level physical discomfort which is usually manageable with oral pain medication (or pain pump). This level will differ from patient to patient.

A conservative schedule for patients undergoing this sort of procedure is 7-10 days for return to normal activity. After 2 wks, activity can slowly be increased.

Most patients are doing light exercise by 1 month post op. Full workouts are generally delayed until 6 weeks.

As always, consult with your board certified plastic surgeon in preparation for your surgery. (Donovan Rosas, MD, Nyack Plastic Surgeon)

Mommy makeover Recovery will vary

The recovery from your combination of surgeries will be dictated primarily by the type of tummy tuck. You will have some discomfort from the breast surgery, but most of your recovery will relate to your abdomen.Particularly, if there’s a lot of muscle plication involved.

Cincinnati mommy makeover procedure for mothers may include a breast lift and/or breast augmentation and a tummy tuck. Majority of the recovery, however, will come from the tummy tuck. How postoperative pain is managed has a direct effect of drastically reducing the discomfort from an abdominoplasty.

Pain management is key; and, patients will do so much better in the long-term if they start off relatively pain-free.Different options for pain management include a local anesthetic pump, TAP block, or a one-stick spinal anesthetic which provides excellent pain control for about 18 hours. These three mentioned are all done when a patient is under anesthesia. Do discuss these pain management options with your surgeon. Likewise, the type of tummy tuck needed is also another indicator.

If a patient only needs skin removed without plication of the muscles, the recovery will be dramatically easier. On the other hand, if the procedure involves more muscle repair, the recovery will be a bit more of a challenge, and require good pain management for optimal comfort and healing.

As for the breast lift or breast augmentation, recovery is well tolerated. (Glynn Bolitho, PhD, MD, FACS, San Diego Plastic Surgeon)

What is recovery time for mommy makeovers

I will tell my mommy makeover patients that they can expect a recovery time of 4-6 weeks. Most patients will require an oral narcotic for the first week. Every patient is different when they take a narcotic for pain, but I would not anticipate being “comatose” while on the medication.

You should be able to interact with your children normally despite not being able to pick them up for a couple of weeks. After the first week, most patients are able to tolerate their discomfort with Tylenol or Advil with the occasional narcotic at bedtime. (Robert Najera, MD, Frisco Plastic Surgeon)
