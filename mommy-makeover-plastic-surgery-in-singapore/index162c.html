Mommy Makeover Plastic Surgery In Singapore

Mommy make-over in Singapore is merely a term that describes multiple cosmetic procedures performed either on the same date, or within several days of each other.

It was given this name simply because most of the patients interested in multiple procedures have had an event in their life that causes many areas of their body to be changed, most often pregnancy

For instance, birth of a child or multiple children. Pregnancy causes changes in a woman’s body that can be quite dramatic and involve the breasts, tummy, thighs, buttocks etc. Age is not necessarily a factor in whether or not a patient can undergo ‘mommy make-over’

The major factors to consider are things like: is your weight now stable, do you anticipate having children (or having more children) in the future.

If you are not at a stable weight or if you plan on having additional children, undergoing plastic surgery might be best delayed until after you have completed having children or when you are at a stable weight

Results are more predictable at that time.

I really prefer to do TT in patients that are for sure, done with kids. Accidents do happen but if a woman tells me she is planning on more down the road, I usually would steer her toward the breast procedures and away from the TT

And even then, I prefer to avoid breast lifts in women also planning on more children…

Safety of combining plastic surgical procedures

In general, it is safe to have more than one surgery done at the same time, including abdominoplasty, breast augmentation, and arm lift.

The safety does however depend on the extent of each surgery, and your general health condition

Depending on the specific patient, these surgeries may be able to be safely done at once, or have them done in 2 separate procedures. This decision is best made during your evaluation with a board-certified plastic surgeon
