Drains after tummy tuck surgery

Drains after tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) surgery are placed to ensure proper drainage of the fluid that accumulates in the dead space created between the internal abdominal tissues and the skin the is separated from it during plastic surgery after birth in Irvine.

If the fluid accumulates with out drainage a fluid collection called a seroma may form and will need to be drained with a needle or in worse cases with complete mommy makeover.

So drains are left to drain the fluid until the body recovers and is able to absorb the fluid and attach to the soft tissue and skin layer. I wait till the drains output is less the 30cc over a 24 hour period that is on average about a 7-10 days give or take.

Scar treatment after mommy makeover

Scar quality can be predicted most easily by past healing and your prior breast surgery will give you an indication of how you will do. There are so many scar treatment programs on the market and this gives the indication that there is no ‘best’ program or silver bullet.

Scar massage and pressure with any lotion that you skin tolerates well is a proven winner and easily done by all. OTC scar creams offer hope though cost more, and silicone sheeting works though is hard to keep up with in a busy life.
