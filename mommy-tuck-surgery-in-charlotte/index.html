Mommy Tuck Surgery In Charlotte

What’s included with the mommy makeover?

A mommy makeover usually refers to correction of the breasts and abdomen. Depending on the patient, different combinations of procedures will be needed. Typically for the breasts, this could be a lift, implants, or a lift with implants and for the abdomen, it could be lipo, a mini TT, a full TT or a combination of these.

What is the total cost of a mommy makeover w/ breast implants and a tummy tuck?

Costs for a mommy makeover will certainly vary depending amongst surgeons as well as on your geographic location. Things to factor in from the surgical standpoint will be the facility fee, anesthesia fee, surgeon fee, other fees (e.g., liposuction, surgical garment, cost of implants, addition of other surgical procedures, etc.) A range in price ~$12000-20000 is not unreasonable. $13-16K would be a reasonable cost on average. A consultation with a plastic surgeon will help you in determining which procedure(s) would be the right one for you. Cost also depends on the complexity of your case and how much work would need to be done to achieve an aesthetically-pleasing result for you.

It is very common to combine tummy tuck with breast surgery; this combination of procedures is known as a “mommy makeover”.

A couple of things:

1) Downsizing your breast implants sounds like a very reasonable thing to do. The sheer size of your current implants has likely stretched out the breast skin envelope and weakened the supporting ligaments. When you downsize to such a smaller implant, a significant amount of revision will be required of the internal breast pocket.

Day 5 After Mommy Makeover Photo

In addition, at the very minimum, you will need an incision around the areola and a vertical incision extending to the inframammary fold (lollipop incision) to deal with the extra skin of the breast. You may even need a scar in the fold at the base of the breast, depending on the elasticity of the skin. This doesn’t qualify as “minimum scarring”.

2) You’re an excellent candidate for a tummy tuck. Although you have very little fat, you clearly have separation of the abdominal wall muscles and skin laxity. A tummy tuck will make a dramatic difference for you.

Breast lift or breast aug

In the best case scenario, a breast augmentation alone will just give you bigger saggy breasts. This is not what you are saying your goal is. A breast lift will drastically improve the shape of your breasts but may fall short on the “upper pole fullness” you are after. It really looks like you have plenty of breast tissue.

Dr Bill Kortesis Tummy Tuck Operation Patient Images Result

Although adding an implant at the same time as the lift is tempting to add volume to the upper pole, this will make your breasts larger, which over time will cause further sagging. This discussion needs to be had in person at the time of your consultation. Be sure to express your goals clearly. This will increase your chances of a good outcome.

Tummy Tuck and Breast Lift

If you are healthy, this is a safe combination. This is a common combination for dual procedure. See a board certified plastic surgeon using an accredited surgery center.

Based on your photograph, you are a great candidate for a tummy tuck with liposuction. This can be done through a low and short incision, all completely below the bikini line. The belly button can be designed to look natural and have no scars on the exterior. Your muscles can also be plicated resulting in a flat abdomen.

Dr. Gaurav Bharti Before And After Mommy Makeover Photos

A breast lift can be performed at the same time for improved shape and contour of your breasts. Make sure you specifically look at before and after pictures of real patients who have had this surgery performed by your surgeon and evaluate their results.

If you have lost all the weight you intend to lose, an abdominoplasty (tummy tuck) and breast lift with implants looks to be the best procedure. This would provide you with a slimmer waistline and improve the appearance of you breasts.

The most important aspect is to find a surgeon you are comfortable with. I recommend that you seek consultation with a qualified board-certified plastic surgeon who can evaluate you in person.
