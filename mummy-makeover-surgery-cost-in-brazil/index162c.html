Sleeping Following Tummy Tuck

This is a frequently asked question following abdominoplasty surgery and the answer depends on a variety of factors. Your surgeon is the only one who can answer this question because he’s the only one who knows exactly what was done in the operating room

In most cases, I prefer that patients sleep in a flexed position in a recliner chair. This keeps tension off the wound closure. The return to side sleeping depends on multiple variables including the tension on the wound closure, the lateral extent of the incision and muscle plication to name a few

It’s important that you discuss this issue with your plastic surgeon. Your surgeon can then make an appropriate recommendation.

Tummy Tuck and Sleep

After a tummy tuck it is recommended that you wait at least 2 weeks before sleeping on your side. Consult your physician for their recommended post operative instructions.

Side sleeping

You should check in with your surgeon and follow his or her recommendations. I allow my patients to sleep on their side at 10 days.
