If you have no problems waiting, then definitely wait until after your last pregnancy. Otherwise it the delivery can stretch out your repair and often requires a revision. Always best to avoid this.

I recommend you to perform your mommy makeover after pregnancy! Usually I tell my patients to wait at least six months after delivery to perform any kind of surgery.

Mommy makeover postoperative care

Most of our “Mommy Makeovers” are done on an outpatient basis. If the surgeon is relatively efficient and has a well-designed surgical plan, it should be relatively straightforward to recover you for going home the same day, assuming you are a low risk patient in terms of medical conditions.

Some surgeons prefer an overnight stay, but I personally don’t think there is enough direct observation that happens or is needed the first night that would really change the outcome if the patient is in a hospital, assuming the patient does not have substantial life-threatening bleeding or a sudden cardiac event.

Weight stability is an important feature of ensuring the longevity of your result. You don’t want to have substantial weight loss after a tummy tuck as you may end up with loose skin again and be disappointed.
