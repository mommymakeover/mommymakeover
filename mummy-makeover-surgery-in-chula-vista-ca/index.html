Chula Vista, CA Mommy makeovers are designed to return proportionality and balance to a woman whose figure has changed after childbirth: breasts are lifted, filled, or both and tummys are tightened. Breasts are composed of only two el;ements: skin and volume.

A few things to note and ask about:

  1. Is your surgeon a Board-Certified Plastic Surgeon?
  2. Is the facility accredited? Meaning, does it carry a license by a national accreditation body, which will require the surgeon to have and maintain certain safety criteria.
  3. Is the accrediting body a ‘real’ one that requires the surgeon to have the same privileges for the same procedures he/she is performing on you?

This is important, because there are some major differences. Ie: AAAASF is a REAL accrediting facility, AAAHC is NOT. AAAASF requires the surgeon to be able to perform the same procedures at the referral hospital, AAAHC only requires that the staff know how to dial 911!
