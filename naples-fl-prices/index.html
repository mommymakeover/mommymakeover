The Mommy Makeover In Naples, FL

A mommy makeover in Naples intends to rejuvate the parts of the body that have been stretched, loosened, and weakened by the processes of preganancy and nursing. Breasts tend to become deflated, flattened and droopy. Bellies become weakened, loosened and protuberant. Mommy makeovers restore youthful, pre-preganancy appearances (often enhanced that is) to breasts and abdomens through breast augmentations, with or without lifts, and tummy tucks. I often combine mommy makeovers with liposuction, labiaplasty, and/or other procedures. Your consultation will clarify all details.

Mommy Makeover and Brazilian Butt Lift in one surgery in Naples

If one is in good health, a Brazilian Butt Lift (fat injection into the buttock) can be combined with a Mommy Makeover (tummy tuck and breast augmentation/lift). Although you are slim at 5’6″ tall and 125 lbs, I have performed Brazilian Butt Lifts on patients your size with good results. One patient noted a very nice improvement with as little as 200 cc of fat injected into each buttock (400 cc total). Check with a Board Certified plastic surgeon in your area to see if this would be possible in your case.

Butt lift and tummy tuck; you can’t have it both ways…..

You are in a bit of a dilemma. I normally will not combine a tummy tuch with a butt lift. I do not like my patients to sit on their buttocks for a week post fat injection. After a tummy tuck you will have a hard time not sitting alot. You however are fairly thin; it certainly seems to be a waste of good fat to perfrom a tummy tuck and to discard the fat, then to perform the butt lift later. You could do the butt lift first, and use the fat from the lower abdomen to perform the butt lift, the have a planned tummy tuck 3-4 weeks later.

Weight Loss for Tummy Tuck and liposuction

Many clients come to my office in search of a easy way to get back the figure they had years ago. The topic of weight loss is discussed in detail in the initial consultation. If you are over weight and need to loose weight then I recommend you search for a healthy diet to reach a safe weight before your surgery.

If you are just seeking to loose more pounds but have extra skin then it is not helpful as you are just adding to your already loose skin and fat. A board certified plastic surgeon should be able to examine you and tell you the best plan of action. Many of my clients also loose weight after tummy tucks and look much better. Inner thighs tend not to change with weight change so this really would not affect your mommy lift in Naples.

Smoking cessation

Every plastic surgeon has their own protocol for smoking cessation. I generally recommend no tobacco/cigarettes for 4-6 weeks before surgery and definitely no smoking after your procedure. This also includes 2nd hand smoke as well. Please visit with a board certified plastic surgeon in your area to learn more about your MOM options!

If you only need a breast lift you do not need implants

Implants are for adding volume. If the only concern you have with your breasts is droop, then avoid the implants and go straight to a lift. There are different patterns of lifts including vertical lifts and anchor type lifts. My philosophy is to try to avoid more scarring if at all possible. I believe the vertical lift accomplishes this for most people while giving a beautiful shape and minimizing the scar.

Overnight stay after mommy makeover

There is ot any one clear answer on this. I do feel as though this is a lot of surgery and I offer everyone having breat surgery and tummy tuck an overnight stay. This does add to the cost of the procedure, so if you are otherwise healthy, I would not require the overnight stay.

The stay does make it easier for you, but I am not sure there is a medical benefit. Compression stocking are readily available in the hospital, but I think people are more likely to get up and walk around at home, so I am not convinced there is a benefit in regard to DVT prophylaxis.

If you have any underlying medical issues, I may require an overnight stay for observation, but this is individualized decision.
