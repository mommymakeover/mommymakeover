Nebraska Mom Makeover Prices

A mommy makeover in Nebraska is usually a breast lift, breast augmentation, and tummy tuck. Of course you can have only part of this! Just talk to your plastic surgeon about what you want.

Mommy Makeover before or after pregnancy ?

I would recommend waiting until after your pregnancy for a mommy makeover. It would be a shame to have a pregnancy after your mommy lift undo the nice results.

The body is smarter than the doctor, so even after a tummy tuck you could get pregnant, deliver and have another tummy tuck. Even so, most patients would rather go through it just once.

Generally if child bearing is in the near future I advise my patients to wait. I would use this opportunity and time to be as healthy and fit as you can be and get to the weight you want to be before your wedding and the same after child bearing. It will make your tummy tuck safer and more gratifying as well.

Yes it is better to wait; the pregnancy would again make your muscles lax and stretch the skin. Wait until you are done having children then the procedure will be much more worth while.

Before or after children

It is always best to finish having children before investing in abdominal contour correction. This prevents the possibility of requiring a significant revision down the road as a result of the abdominal expansion from pregnancy. Abdominal contour correction can be a substantial investment and you only want to have to commit this investment once if possible.

Am I a candidate for plastic surgery mommy tuck at current weight ?

That is a difficult question to answer via pictures. Although lots of docs have made recommendations of weight loss it depends more on your health , additional medical diseases and current exercise regimine.

Another words not everyone can be 5’9″ 150 lbs , some pts bodies are naturally heavier …. I tell pts to consider PS at a weight they have maintained for years, they must also be healthy ( no diabetes or high blood pressure that can be alleviated with weight loss)I call it a pt s natural weight .

Combined procedures

You worked hard to loose the weight, but unfortunately that extra skin will not go away without surgery. The good thing about doing them together is the recovery from the breast aug is relatively simple.

I do tell my patients the average time off work for a tummy tuck is two weeks. I also tell them that if they can work from home, or have the potential to do part time work at first they can do that. Regardless 5 days recovery is really pushing it. 10 days or so can happen but still a little unrealistic.

You appear to be a great candidate for a full tummy tuck and breast augmentation. If you have your surgery on a Friday and can take the next 5-day work week off, that should be enough time to recover sufficiently before returning to work. Discuss your recovery expectations with your surgeon.

Breast lift vs implants with mommy makeover

If you lose 40 pounds, it is likely that you will lose volume from your breasts which will then likely leave them with a deflated and more droopy appearance. Therefore, I anticipate that you will need an implant to restore the volume and a lift to restore the shape/perky appearance. The scars from the lollipop lift usually heal quite well and are usually worth it to get the desired result. All that being said, only the plastic surgeon you see in person after you have lost the weight will be able to advise you appropriately. Choose a board certified real plastic surgeon experienced in cosmetic breast surgery.

Plastic Surgery After Weight Loss

I would absolutely consider an extended Tummy Tuck or perhaps a Lower Body Lift operation. The breast lift can certainly be combined with the tummy tuck, but it might be too much to do at one sitting with a lower body lift operation.

Personally, I would wait until the breast lift is done to decide whether you want more volume with an implant. Breasts like these can be surprising when lifted – sometimes you have more volume than you think, and sometimes you still want more fullness above the level of the nipple (which really can’t be achieved without the implants). It really depends on what you want.
