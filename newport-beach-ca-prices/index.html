Mommy Makeover Recovery: What to Expect Following this Combination Procedure

Mommy makeover is a term applied to a combination of procedures that are intended to correct some of the problems caused by pregnancy. Normally, some type of breast enhancement combined with some type of tummy tuck and liposuction are included. In your case, a breast augmentation with a breast lift and a tummy tuck will be performed. Of the two procedures, the tummy tuck will require more recovery.

Normally, there will be some restriction with lifting and moving your arms associated with the augmentation and breast lift. The tummy tuck is a bigger portion of the mommy makeover, and by itself, will take more recovery time. Normally, patients do not have “unbearable” pain. Our patients are normally well-controlled with the narcotic medication we provide in the first several days following surgery.

As the patient tolerates it, this is usually reduced down to other types of pain medication that are not narcotic-based. Normally, the patients are very coherent during this time and their actual ability to think and perform mentally are not changed.

A C-section is normally more painful than an abdominoplasty (tummy tuck). During the course of a C-section, the inner lining of the abdominal wall is cut. For that reason, one would expect the pain associated with a C-section to be considerably different than that associated with a “tummy tuck.” (John J. Edney, MD, Omaha Plastic Surgeon)
