New York State Postpartum Plastic Surgery Cost

A “Mommy Makeover” in New York reverses the changes that occur as a result of your pregnancies. In most cases this will include an abdominoplasty which removes the excess skin and stretch marks, tightens the muscle to give you a flat “six pack” look, creates a beautiful belly button and liposculpture of the “muffin top”.

In addition the breast changes will be addressed by breast implants, a lift or a combination of these procedures. All women love how they feel about their rejuvenated bodies. Many comment that they look even better than they did before they had their pregnancies.

Multiple Plastic Surgery Procedures

It’s is a lot in one setting and there are increased risks. If you came to me, I would break it up into 2 separate procedures with lipo first then tummy tuck and breast Aug.

You could also transfer the fat to either your breasts or buttock during the lipo session. This could enhance your overall makeover.

Am I a good candidate for a tummy tuck?

Simply put, yes you are. To get optimal results you will need a full tummy tuck with liposuction as needed. Do not get talked into a mini tummy tuck which is a poor substitute 99% of the time.

Is a TT, breast aug, and extensive lipo too much for 1 mommy surgery?

My only concern would be the total time under general anesthesia. Inquire with your surgeon how long it would take him/her to accomplish everything on your list. If it totals more than 6 hrs., I would probably do the lipo at another time.

Jacuzzi After Mommy Makeover

Jacuzzis are notorious for having the possibility of harboringsignificant bacteria including pseudomonas. Additionally, the heat from theJacuzzi can create significant dilation of the blood vessels and capillariesand can lead to swelling or worst case scenario burns of the tissues where surgery was performed and where blood supply is still recovering.

I generallywould not recommend patients use a Jacuzzi until at least six weeks aftersurgery and additionally they need to ensure that all their wounds are fully healed with no open areas that could be at risk for infection.

Showers aregenerally okay 48 hours after surgery or after drains are removed and warm baths are probably fine after a month or so.

Sometimes a tighter, flatter abdomen makes smallish breasts look just fine and sometimes fuller breasts match up very nicely with a roundish tummy. Remember, it’s all about balance and proportion.

Breast ptosis

The grades of ptosis are determined by how low the nipple and/or breast tissue is relative to the breast fold. Grade 3 requires a more extensive lift and more surgical time for correction but does not necessarily increase difficulty or recovery in terms of pain or time.

The grade of ptosis (3 in this case) makes the ensuing lift more significant but the mommy makeup in New York for a lift is itself simple. The issue is the extent of the scars and the discussing the implant use, size, and choice.

Many of us will do single stage augmentation mastopexy but there are some risks to this that your board certified plastic surgeon will go over with you.

Grade 3 skin laxity simply means that your nipple position is at least 3 cm below your infra-mammary folds. This will require, normally, a full mastopexy with skin removed both vertically and along the infra-mammary folds. An anchor scar is the resulting incisions.

Multiple Procedures at once, can it be done safely?

Usually patients combine procedures at the same time without any issue. My safety limit is about 6 hours of general anesthesia time. As far a lift, it just depends on what you expect to see.

A better photograph may help to assess if you need or WANT a lift. It is always advisable to see more than one board certified physician before making a decision so that at least you get two opinions!
