Upper and lower eye bag surgery. How much would this procedure cost?
Visual Field Testing
Mar 6, 2017
Thanks for your question. An Oculoplastic Surgeon ( Eye Surgeon that specializes plastic surgery around the eyes and face) can determine if your upper eyelids cause a visual field defect that qualifies for insurance coverage. Your profile picture suggests that you have blockage of your peripheral field of vision. Lower eyelid cosmetic surgery is not covered by insurance. Thanks and I hope this helps.
Vikram Durairaj, MD FACS
