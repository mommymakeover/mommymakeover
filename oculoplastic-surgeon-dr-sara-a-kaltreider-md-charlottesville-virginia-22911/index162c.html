How long does swelling last after a lower blepharoplasty?
Swelling after lower blepharoplasty - way to early to worry!
Feb 21, 2019
It is not unusual to have swelling at 2 weeks. There are many factors that contribute to post-operative swelling - even if you have none of these factors, you would have some swelling. See your surgeon for follow up so that he/she can track your progress. In the meantime, keep your head elevated, avoid high salt intake, keep medical problems under control, minimize aerobics - but exercise with your head up. Best wishes.

You will notice improvement in symmetry over the next few weeks to months. Followup with your surgeon is important so that he/she can track your progress. Best wishes.
