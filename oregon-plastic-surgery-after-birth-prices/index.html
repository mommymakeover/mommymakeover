Oregon Plastic Surgery After Birth Prices

The term “Mommy Makeover” in Oregon came into popular use during the era of the Extreme Makeover TV show. It is not a formal plastic sugery term.

It does refer to restoring any part of the body that was changed by pregnancy. For most that involves a tummy tuck and whatever breast surgery will give the best outcome.

That might mean a breast lift or reduction, or a breast augmentation. You should fix what bothers you and certainly do not have to have everything at once. However having the change come with a single recovery period can be a real benefit.

You should have an abdominoplasty after you are done with pregnancies. If you are planning in having more babies, you should do that and plan for your surgery in the future.

Any ethical plastic surgeon will and should tell you the same. A pregnancy after a tummy tuck has the potential of destroying all the work performed and a secondary TT is more difficult to perform.

Be sure to seek the help of a plastic surgeon member of The American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Aurgery

Have the baby first!

Have your kids first. Is you’re lucky, you may not have much change with pregnancy after mommy tuck in OR. But MOST women will need something done after children.

That’s why it’s a “mommy” makeover in Oregon and not a “I’ve never had children but am planning to someday” makeover. Have the baby, wait 3-6 months after giving birth and go for it!

Breast Plastic Surgery With Doctor Kiya Movassaghi

How soon after surgery can I try for a baby?

If you are planing on getting pregnant in the near future, I would suggest waiting on having your mommy lift until after you have completed your family.

A pregnancy can certainly compromise the results of the tummy tuck and breast feeding can result in sagging which may require a breast lift.

Can I get the mommy makeover without having a breast lift?

A mommy makeover in Oregon is a combination of tummy and breast procedures. If you are looking to just increase the size of your breasts than an augmentation willbe fine.

Be sure to consult with a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon who performs numerous mommy makeover procedures peryear to be assessed. Without a physicalexam, it is difficult to evaluate your specific needs.

You can discuss the results you are looking to achieve and your surgeonwill advise you of your options and make his or her recommendations. At my clinic in Oregon, I can perform a tummy tuck withbreast augmentation in approximately 3 hours.

You are definitely a candidate for a mommy make-over. One needs to be realistic about expectations. Everyone gets their best results if they are a normal weight. At your current weight, you will not have a flat stomach because you have excess fat internally, not just under the skin.

The hour glass shape depends not just on fat but on a persons anatomy – A long waist (long distance between ribs and hips) is necessary.

Breast reduction is an excellent operation if you want smaller breasts – it does not create upper breast fullness which is actually not a natural look and requires an implant to achieve.

If you have a consultation, you can get a better understanding of what is best for you. Always see a BoardCertified Plastic Surgeon.
