Breast Augmentation and Tummy Tuck with Insurance

Unfortunately, insurance never covers a straight forward breast augmentation or tummy tuck procedure. These are considered “elective, cosmetic” procedures and are not covered by insurance. Breast surgery may be covered if it’s reconstruction after cancer or a deformity

Returning to work after mommy tuck

Most patients who have an occupation where they do not have a lot of physical activity and are mostly seated return to work after 2 weeks. Your surgeon will be the best person to advise you on when you can expect to return to work based on the details of your mommy surgery in Ottawa

I advise my patients that they should be able to return to work in 10 to 14 days if they have a desk job. There are drains in place for 7 to 10 days, and usually after a week or so you will be able to stand and walk normally
