Safe to combine lipo and tummy tuck?

Presuming you are an appropriate candidate, lipo can be combined with tummy tuck, however, the location of liposuction is very important.

For example, liposuction of the non-abdominal area is generally considered safe. However, liposuction of the abdominal area where the tummy tuck is being performed is considered less safe and great care and specialized techniques must be used in these cases.

The concern is causing injury to the abdominal tissue which can result in loss of skin. I recommend obtaining a formal consultation with a plastic surgeon to get your questions answered more fully. (Bryan Correa, MD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)
