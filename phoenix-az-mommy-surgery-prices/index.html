Mederma and massage for surgical scars is great to maximize healing and to good results. However, everyone is unique and it’s important to allow your plastic surgeon to monitor your scar healing and to provide you with specific advice.

Recovery Period After Mommy Makeover in Phoenix

Every patient’s recovery period is individual, but most patients are able to get back to basic housework within 2 weeks of their surgery. By the 3 week mark, almost all of our patients are back to normal housekeeping work like you described.
