How Long Should You Wear Your Binder/garment for After a Tummytuck?
How Long to Wear a Compression Garment After a Tummy Tuck
Jul 14, 2018
A standard guideline for our Tummy Tuck post-op compression support is 6 weeks. Some patients with persistent swelling may benefit from prolonged compression garment support. It is imperative that the support fits smoothly to avoid any ridges or dents after a Tummy Tuck.

Tummy Tuck Recovery
Tummy Tuck Recovery
Jul 14, 2018
Recovery can vary depending on the type of Tummy Tuck performed and patient comfort. The first couple of days can be challenging and help at home is needed. Drinking lots of fluids prevents dehydration. Most patients will be able to resume driving within 10 days. No athletic activities for 3 weeks. Most people can resume normal activities within 6 weeks.
How Can a Physician Tell the Difference Between Fat and Loose Skin for a Tummy Tuck?
Difference Between Fat and Loose Skin
Jul 14, 2018
It is easy for an experienced Board Ceritifed Plastic Surgeon to determine the difference between fat and loose skin. On examination of the area(s) the amount of localized fat coupled with skin laxity helps determine which procedures to recommend.
Length of Time Between Laser Hair Treatments
Laser hair removal: Time between treatments
Jul 10, 2018
Laser hair removal only treats actively growing hair. The length of time between treatments will vary depending on the individual and the area treated. Facial hair generally grows more quickly than hair on the body. Based on this you can expect the time between treatments to be approximately 4-10 weeks. Make sure to consult with a certified technician.
How Much Hair Should Fall Out After Session of Laser Hair Removal (Bikini)?
Laser Hair Removal
Jul 10, 2018
Laser hair removal targets actively growing hair follicles. Hair grows in cycles and we recommend 4-6 treatments to effectively treat hair follicles in the right cycle.
What Can I Do to Get Rid of Swelling After Tummy Tuck Surgery?
Swelling after Tummy Tuck
Jul 10, 2018
Swelling after a Tummy Tuck depends upon many factors; incisions, amount of tissue removed, areas treated, and amount of liposuction included. Some swelling may continue for 2-3 months. The two factors that help reduce swelling are wearing post-op support garments and massaging of all liposuctioned areas.

Low Belly Button After TT. What Can I Do?
Umbilicoplasty may be another option.
Jul 8, 2012
After an umbilical float abdominoplasty, the belly button only get its blood supply from the surrounding skin, not from underneath as after a standard abdonimoplasty. Therefore, moving the belly button up could very well could cause umbilical necrosis. One alternative could be excising the current belly button and reconstructing a new at a more desireable level.
