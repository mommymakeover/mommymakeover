Muscle spasms after TT. Is this normal?
Muscle Spasms after Tummy Tuck
Apr 30, 2018
Muscle spasms and pain are not rare after this type of surgery. Generally we expect this sort of thing to settle over 2-3 months. Our first line treatment is non-steroidal anti-inflammatory medication like ibuprofen. If that’s not cutting it your doctor may want to try a muscle relaxant but most of us are hesitant about using that kind of medication for any prolonged period.
