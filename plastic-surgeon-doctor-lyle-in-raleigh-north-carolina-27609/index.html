Progressive tension sutures
May 5, 2018
One of the potential problems of drainless tummy tucks is the progressive tension sutures which fix the elevated abdominal wall back down to the abdominal fascia. If overly aggressive- they can scar down the skin to the midline and create a line . Such tethering may need to be surgically released if it doesn’t relax with massage.

What’s the earliest I can go back to work, at my desk job, after breast reduction surgery?
Time off
Apr 19, 2015
I am reluctant to recommend anything less than a week off for a breast reduction for patients with desk jobs. Everyone is different and every situation varies but as a general rule- I have found a week to 10 days is desired. Many patients are still taking pain medication for up to week. I think most physicians are not willing to have patients disappointed and unhappy if they say you can go back earlier and then you have to miss work and leave your employer…
