Massage for teardrop implants?
Massaging implants
May 28, 2019
This is a question that gets asked a lot by patients and the answer varies from surgeon to surgeon. Some surgeons advise all of their augmentation patients to massage the implants, others specifically advise against it. There is not necessarily one "right" way to do it. I recommend you reach out to your surgeons office and see his or her preference.

Can I get a lift with existing implants?
breast lift with old implants
May 22, 2019
It may be possible to get a breast lift without exchanging your current implants for new ones. There is, however, a risk of injury to the implant during the surgery. Another point to mention is that your implants are already 10 years old. Since you will be under general anesthesia already, it may be wise to exchange the implants for new ones while you have the opportunity.
Is it possible to just raise the nipples without a breast lift?
Changing nipple position
May 22, 2019
Without photos it is very difficult to determine the best option for your breasts. In general, most women with low set nipples do best with a full breast lift. There may be a possibility of removing a small section of skin above the areola which would lift the nipple only, but you would need to be evaluated in person to determine if this is an appropriate option for you. I recommend you schedule a consultation with a plastic surgeon for more information.

Concerns about necrosis
Mar 30, 2019
Based on your photos, it does not appear you have any skin necrosis after your surgery. If you were to have necrosis, this would cause skin breakdown and ultimately open wounds. If you note either of these, you should contact your surgeon.
