Is it okay to take Advil for a major headache after BA surgery?
Advil and Massage after BA
Jul 12, 2017
Your surgeon would be the best one to tell you when it is safe to take Advil. I tend to advise all my patients to wait 2 weeks after surgery. Massage is a very important part of getting the best outcome from your surgery. Massage allows the implants to move nicely in the pocket and can actually cause the pain to lessen once your breast get used to the initial discomfort. I would suggest talking to your Doctor about the best way to do this at minimal discomfort. Best of wishes.
