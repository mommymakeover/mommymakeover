Is fat removed during gynecomastia surgery?
Gynecomastia
Oct 19, 2023
Typically yes. Most surgeons will combine fat removal with gland removal to achieve a harmonious result. This can be either direct excision or with liposuction. A smooth contour throughout the entire breast is always the overall goal.
Am I a good candidate for a crescent lift with implants?
Breast Lift
Oct 19, 2023
I typically don’t recommend crescent lifts since the risk/reward ratio isn’t favorable. The lifting benefit is minimal and the possibility of distortion is greater. If anything, I recommend a circulareola lift more which adds a little more scar but much more lifting ability.
Nerve damage and nipple sensitivity - are some people more prone to nerve damage?
Nerve issues
Aug 17, 2022
Breasts are a different story than the rest of your anatomy. The nerve anatomy is different on every person. Although you have a dominant nerve for nipple sensation, usually there are multiple that innervate to help reduce the chances of complete nipple sensory loss.
Breast Implant size - lift or not?
Breast contouring
Mar 12, 2022
This depends on your goals. If you want to be functional, most patients go with a more conservative size. If that means a lift, then patients tend to choose that. I have 3D Vectra imaging software in my office to facilitate these discussions so you may want to ask if they have something like that to help.
How many stitches are used to fix the muscle repair?
Stitches for muscle repair
Mar 10, 2022
There are a lot of actual stitches but the actual number doesn’t matter. What matters is the type of stitches. I use 2 long permanent stitches and then reinforce it with alot of interrupted absorbable sutures. This is a very common and long-standing technique.
Does dual plane implant placement always require cut to the muscle?
Dual Plane
Jan 16, 2022
Technically dual plane means you dissect under the gland and the muscle. Therefore it is does require release of the muscle slip onto the rib periosteum. It is a great technique especially for patients with ptosis.
Should I have fat transfer done at same time as breast implant removal and lift or wait?
Fat Transfer with Implant Removal
Jan 28, 2019
Thank you for your question. Fat Transfer is normally performed under general anesthesia due to the liposuction procedure needed in order to harvest the fat for the transfer. Fat Transfer can be performed at the same time as an implant removal, however, it may be recommended to stage your surgeries. Consult with a board certified plastic surgeon who has experience with fat transfer to the breasts, and communicate your post-op expectations post implant removal. Good luck on your journey!
Surgeon says I need to wait 1 yr to correct my breasts. Is that true?
Breast Revision
Jan 28, 2019
Thank you for your question. Providing photos would give a better visual of your situation, but based on your concerns it would be recommended to follow your surgeon's guidelines and wait until the implants are settled to determine your next steps. Patience is key through your recovery as well as communication with your surgeon. Good luck to you!
How long until scar is white?
Scar care after surgery
Nov 12, 2018
Thank you for your question, and congratulations on your recent surgery. Do know, the healing of your scar will take more time, and redness this early into your recovery is normal. It would be best advised to consult with your surgeon to discuss scar massage techniques or the usage of Biocorneum or Vitamin E, which may be recommended but not always necessary this early on. Patience is key through your recovery. Well wishes!
How soon before surgery should I stop drinking?
When to stop drinking before your surgery
Nov 12, 2018
Thank you for your question. It would be recommended to stop drinking 1-2 weeks before surgery, however, it is best to confirm with your surgeon as they may have more specific pre-operative guidelines for you. Good luck with your surgical journey!
Will I be a candidate for a tummy tuck?
Body contiuring
Dec 3, 2017
Thank you for your question. It is difficult to answer this without an examination or photos. However, typically if you have excess skin and/or lax muscles, you can be a candidate for an abdominoplasty. Of course, you have to be fairly healthy and at a stable and low weight. Most plastic surgeons prefer patients BMI’s to be lower than 35. I hope this helps and remember to seek a board certified plastic surgeon.
Exparel pain shot for Tummy Tuck. Is it similar to what the dentist gives? Does it have epinep
Anesthetic
Nov 14, 2017
Exparel is a long lasting anesthetic that is typically injected when you are asleep during surgery. I inject into the muscle fascia during abdominoplasties and the incision sites. It usually doesn’t contain epinephrine. It has definitely made a difference postoperatively for my patients.
I'm almost 4 week post op with 300 Vic silicone unders. What's the best way to minimize scarri
Scar
Nov 14, 2017
I have my patients begin scar massage at 3 weeks postop daily for about a year. Scar creams such as Biocorneum or Vitamin E is recommended but not always necessary. Be sure to use sun screen in the scars even when clothed in intense sunlight.
Does a tummy tuck actually remove fat cells, yielding the same result as lipo?
Abdominoplasty
Nov 14, 2017
An abdominoplasty removes fat cells and skin while liposuction just removes fat cells. Be sure to know that not all fat cells are removed so those left behind can hypertrophy (get bigger) if the patient gains weight after surgery. Thanks for your question.
Dog ears. When is the best time to go back to your PS for repair?
Dog ears
Nov 2, 2017
Thank you for the question. I usually recommend waiting at least a year before revision. Most of them will resolve with patience. Most can also usually be done under local in the office.
