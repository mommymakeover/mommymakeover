Is it possible to make eyebrows an even height with Botox?
Botox and eyebrow position
Oct 13, 2015
Botox can be used to alter the position of the eyebrows. If the Botox is placed above the tail of the eyebrow that is too high, it will cause that eyebrow to drop down. If Botox is placed below the tail of the lower eyebrow, it can help with lifting the lower eyebrow. This is where the experience of injector can play a large role to determine which option is best for you.

Will a 10 pound weight gain after full tummy tuck stretch my muscle repair?
Tummy tuck and weight gain
Oct 1, 2015
A ten pound weight gain will not add a significant amount visceral fat (fat around the organs and inside the rectus muscles) which is the fat that can cause separation of the rectus muscles. The fat will be distributed across the entire body. It usually requires a pregnancy or large weight gain (greater than 50 pounds) to significantly affect the rectus muscles.
