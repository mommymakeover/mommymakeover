Would you recommend I have a breast lift or can I have just a breast augmentation?
To lift or not to lift?
Jan 13, 2020
The answer of course is “it depends”. For a smaller augmentation, the skin won’t fill out enough to avoid sagging of the breasts off the implants. However, larger implants certainly may fill out the breasts and avoid a lift. Measurements, skin elasticity, and density of the breast tissue all affect the nature of an augmentation and require in person evaluation. 
What would be the best option for Brest enhancement?
Larger breasts from implants or fat
Jan 13, 2020
Initially, you will need to get closer to your goal weight of 150lbs. This will affect the size of your breasts and may also affect your goals from the surgery. Fat grafting works well in small volumes, but is usually unrealistic for a large size increase. Implants will reliably give you larger breasts, but you will definitely require a breastlift to position the breast tissue and the nipples in the correct place. 
How can I improve the shape of my breasts after initial breast augmentation?
Smaller breasts and implants.
Jan 13, 2020
The breast tissue is being pushed forward by the implant, however there are restrictive tissue bands inside the breast which are contributing to the distortion. These can be released surgically to allow the breast tissue to properly redrape over the implant. 
What’s the deal with my asymmetrical and oddly shaped breasts? Are they tuberous? What can be done for
Constricted breast deformity
Jan 13, 2020
Tuberous breasts are a variant of the common “constricted breast” deformity. This is a congenital deformity where the inframammary folds are elevated and the breasts hang low. Rounder breasts require a mastopexy and sometimes benefit from implants. The fold underneath the breast may need to be lowered. In your case, I don’t believe you have a traditional “tuberous” breast, but you certainly appear to have a constricted breast deformity.
Is there a way to fix my uniboob?
Symmastia
Jan 13, 2020
The uniboob deformity arises from communication of the implant spaces or violation of the sternal skin. Correction is a difficult procedure and requires a new pocket, repair of the existing communication, and sometimes dermal matrix for reinforcement. This is essentially a reconstruction despite being considered cosmetic by insurance companies. A particularly useful approach is the neo-subpectoral pocket approach we designed at Georgetown many years ago.
Should I go for 365cc, 385cc, or 415cc? Currently 5’5 105lbs.
Implant sizing
Jan 13, 2020
The most important factor in implant sizing is the base width of the implant. The implant should be narrower than your breast width, otherwise the implants will push to the side giving you a heavy, even look. 415cc is a large implant, and will look very big on your smaller frame. Your goals in regards to fullness, “natural vs. fake”, and projection all play into the implant choice. If you are confused, second opinion consults are a good way to sort out the issues.

Why would a donut lift not be ideal for large breasts?
Donut mastopexy limitations
Dec 10, 2019
Donut mastopexies involve a lot of tension around the areola. When large implants are placed, they can exhibit a lot of additional pressure on the skin. This combination is not always ideal and can lead to very wide areolas and poor results.
