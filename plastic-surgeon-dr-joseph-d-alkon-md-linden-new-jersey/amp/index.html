What is the average recovery time of a tummy tuck?
recovery after a tummy tuck
Feb 23, 2018
In my practice, patients are usually back to work in two weeks - depending upon how strenuous their job is, light exercise at 4 weeks, and generally without any restrictions by 6 weeks.  i have patients wear compression garments - including when they return to work.  some patients recover faster - but this is the typical time frame.  discuss this with your plastic surgeon based upon their experience, their technique, and typical postoperative instructions and regimen.
