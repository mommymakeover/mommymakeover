Is it common to have an air bubble in stomach after BA?
Air under the skin is a clinical condition described as subcutaneous emphysema
May 5, 2014
It appears that what you are describing is subcutaneous emphysema. During a breast augmentation surgery, a pocket is created. Air will get trapped in the pocket after the implant is placed. This air can travel under your skin and move into your abdomen, back or neck area. This condition usually resolves withing 3-5 days.If it persists beyond this period, you should see your surgeon.
