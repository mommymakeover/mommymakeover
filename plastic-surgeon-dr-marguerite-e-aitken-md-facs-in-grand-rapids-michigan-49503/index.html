What is the best treatment to help with loose skin?
Weight loss and loose skin
Jul 12, 2018
After weight loss and stretching of the skin from pregnancy, my patients have had their best outcomes with skin removal surgeries vs. liposuction or cool sculpting. Once your skin has had a chance to prove if it is going to tighten up on its own or not, then seek the consultation of a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss the options of abdominoplasty, lower body lift, circumferential abdominoplasty, etc.
What happens when the capsule gets left inside after saline explant?
post explant seroma?
Jul 12, 2018
When an implant is removed, the capsule will scar down to the other side unless there is fluid in the way. If a drain was used, it’s less likely to have fluid fill up the space. If not, your body will eventually absorb it. It can be quite hard and sometimes uncomfortable, but removing the capsule does not necessarily prevent this from happening.

Does BMI have to be under 30 to get a mommy makeover?
Its about results
Jun 1, 2017
Plastic surgery literature shows that if your BMI is 35 or greater you are at increased risk for complications for wound healing, infection, seroma formation, etc. A BMI of 30 or below will help your procedure have a lower risk and your outcome be more attractive and more worthwhile. Do the best you can, this is a big surgery and worth the extra effort to get your most ideal result! Good luck! Dr. Aitken
