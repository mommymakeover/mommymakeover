Are Facials Safe for Our Skin?
Facials
Sep 11, 2019
We offer different types of facials but the most popular is our HydraFacial.   The HydraFacial can be done on all skin types and is gentle but effective.  It exfoliates, does extractions with a gentle suction and infuses antioxidants in the skin and hydrates and plumps the skin.  We also use LED with the HydraFacial.  Its a great once a moth facial that gets great results and can be done on your lunch break.
What's the best treatment to remove brown, age spots?
Brown spots
Sep 10, 2019
IPL and Alexandrite Laser are great options for brown spots.  We typically recommend 3 treatments over the course of 3 months.  Also, use a retinol every night to help increase the cell turnover but discontinue 5 days prior to a laser treatment.

Jun 21, 2019

IPL Hair Removal Effectiveness
IPL
Jun 21, 2019
IPL is not the best choice for hair removal.  We recommend an Alexandrite and a Yag for Laser Hair removal.  The Yag is used for darker skin and Alexandrite for lighter skin. 
Does IPL or a photofacial effectively treat rosacea?
IPL -Rosacea
Jun 21, 2019
Yes, an IPL is a great treatment for Rosacea.  We usually recommend a series of 3 treatments 1 month apart.  The IPL helps with the vascular and redness of the skin.  I also recommend people with rosacea to get a maintenance treatment at least once a quarter or every other month if possible to help manage the rosacea.
After IPL how long does hyperpigmentation last?
IPL
Jun 21, 2019
We usually recommend a series of 3 IPL treatments for hyperpigmentation removal.  The dark spots usually get darker right after the treatment and will flake off in 7-10 days.  Most people will need 2 additional treatments.
Can an IPL home laser for hair removal injure your eyes?
IPL
Jun 21, 2019
Yes, an IPL cam injure your eyes.  You must wear protective goggles whenever you are in a room with an IPL being used.  The light can reflect off of a mirror or anything shiny and injure your eyes.
What is the best way to get rid of random age spots on the legs and arms?
Age Spots
Jun 20, 2019
IPL,  Intense Pulsed Light is a great treatment for removing age spots (hyperpigmentation).  You might need multiple sessions but sometimes one treatment will work.  You must stay out of the sun 4 weeks prior to treatment and you can not be on antibiotics for 3 full days before a treatment.
Can I use Tretinoin with AHAs (glycolic and lactic acid)?
Tretinnoin
Jun 20, 2019
Yes, you can use Tretinoin with AHA's.  Always start slow when beginning a Tretinoin product.  Start 2 times a week for 2-3 weeks then 3 times a week for 3-4 weeks and add on nights as your skin acclimates to the product.
