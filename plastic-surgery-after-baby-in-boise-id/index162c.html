Implants and Mommy Make-over

The use of implants during a mommy make-over is only done if the patient desires to have larger breasts. Unfortunately, the very nature of mommy make-over generally indicates that breast volume and shape have dramatically changed.

Therefore, it is common for mommy make-overs to include breast augmentation and/or breast lifting as well as some sort of body contouring, such as a tummy tuck and/or liposuction.

As you consider where you would like to have your mommy lift, you should ask your plastic surgeon a few questions. Ask him or her if their office-based surgical facility is accredited. Ask who will be performing your anesthesia.

Doctor Dean E. Sorensen Mummy Makeover

Ask if your surgeon has privileges at a hospital to perform the same procedures. Accredited office-based surgery centers have to go through an accreditation process similar to hospitals to verify that they meet patient safety standards.

If the facility is accredited, your surgeon has hospital privileges, and all of your questions are answered to your satisfaction, you should be fine. Many of us prefer to operate out of our office-based surgical facilities, and these are safe; but as a patient, it is important that you do your homework and feel comfortable.
