What is a Mommy Makeover

The Mommy Makeover in Dallas TX is a group of procedures designed to address the aesthetic changes in your body following pregnancy.

Most commonly, a breast procedure (breast augmentation / breast lift) and a tummy tuck are performed to help you restore your pre-pregnancy figure. Some breast changes following pregnancy include loss of volume (often treated with a breast augmentation) and sagging of the breast tissue (usually treated with a breast lift).

A combination breast lift/augmentation is most commonly performed to address both of these changes. A tummy tuck can address the loose abdominal skin and bulging abdominal region resulting from pregnancy.

While pregnancy is a beautiful experience, the changes you see in your figure can negatively impact your body image. A Mommy Makeover by a board-certified plastic surgeon is a wonderful body contouring approach to rejuvenate your body image. (Louis DeLuca, MD, Palm Beach Plastic Surgeon)

Removing Stretch Marks

Dr Fadi C. Constantine, M.D., P.A. Mommy Surgery Images

Stretch marks are one of the more difficult problems faced by plastic surgeons. Although a variety of treatment options exist, excision offers a reliable solution. In patients undergoing abdominoplasty who have flank stretch marks, extension of the incision laterally is a viable alternative when skin redundancy is present.

Although your pictures suggest that this would be possible, it’s hard to know without a physical exam. In some cases, this incision can be extended circumferentially and used to remove excess back and flank skin.

If you’re considering this type of procedure, consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon is appropriate. This surgeon should be able to help you formulate an appropriate treatment plan.
