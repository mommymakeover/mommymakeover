Post Baby Plastic Surgery In Montana

A mommy makeover in Montana typically entails breast surgery (implants, a lift or both), a tummy tuck and liposuction (this can be your hips, flanks, back, thighs…).

The cost is going to in part be determined by what exact procedure you decide upon. The other factor is who does your mommy surgery. You will find that there is a wide range of pricing for these procedures.

Looking at your photos, you are a great candidate for a tummy tuck, some areas of liposuction, and breast augmentation with or without a lift (depending on the findings at your exam and your goals).

Your budget will allow for this with some practice, but not with others. My suggestion is that you start looking around several months before you think you want to to the mommy lift.

Many offices like mine are happy to provide ballpark pricing – just accept that we can’t get specific until you have been examined and we know what your specific goals are.

Mommy makeover, excellent candidate for full TT and breast augmentation

You can get half of what you want in that all your breasts need is an augmentation without a lift based on your photos. However, with 5 kids and the degree of full abdominal muscle laxity and skin excess, you would be so very disappointed with a mini TT because it would do nothing above the belly button and the upper ab muscles would stick out more.

A full TT isn’t that much more recovery or risk so it is always best to do the right mommy package to get the best result. Go to the top plastic surgeon in this arena for best results and safety.

Breast Lift In Billings MT

If you are looking to fill out the breast volume an augmentation will do that nicely without a lift. A Mini Tummy Tuck will not be the right procedure for you. It only tightens the lower abdominal skin and your best result will be a full Tummy Tuck.

See a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon who can show you lots of photos of patients just like yourself so you can see the results you can expect.

Mini- or regular tummy tuck and implants with/without lift?

The amount of redundant skin that is demonstrated in your photographs suggests to me that you will benefit from a traditional tummy tuck: it will give you the greatest assurance of a great result.

It will involve maximum tightening of the slack tummy muscles, tightening the loose skin and removal of some of the adjacent fatty tissue. The appearance of the breasts suggests that implants alone should suffice to enhance the size while filling out the desired volume.

Tummy tuck, breast augmentation, breast lift

The best result will likely come from the combination of a tummy tuck, breast augmentation, and breast lift. These procedures can be performed separately or at the same time.

The cost of all three will vary depending on the surgeon and area of the country but all three together will likely be higher than 9500. You may want to consider prioritizing the areas that are most important to you and starting there.

I recommend seeing several well trained board certified plastic surgeons and getting their opinion as well as looking at their before and after photos.

Medical Tourism

In the event that you have an issue or complication from mommy makeover, and are unable to travel to Montana, you will need to find a plastic surgeon in your area willing to manage any issues that may arise. I would definitely try to have this arranged before your mommy lift.

What will you do if you have a problem and you are not near Montana? If you live in New York, you ought to consider having the procedure in New York. I assume you are considering traveling to save money, but you are really rolling the dice.

This is not fixing your car, this is your body and your life. And if you had a serious problem, what is your recourse? You should find a good surgeon in your area.

Dr. Sarah Nargi Plastic Surgery After Birth Candidate Images Results

Best to see if you can find a plastic surgeon in Montana who is willing to follow you post op before your planned surgery. Where you have your procedure may have no influence on your outcome but should you experience and issues post op, your availability of competent follow maybe critical.

Remaining nearby your surgeon for several weeks post op would be an important consideration. Have you considered and discussed with your Colombian surgeon, who pays for possible complications post op? Every surgeon experiences post op problems. Expect the best but have plans for the worst.

Out of town plastic surgery mommy lift

Although your surgeon in Montana may be excellent, you’ll want to have someone available locally in New York to follow up your progress postoperatively. I’ve cared for patients both in my office and in emergency rooms for problems that arose after mommy maker performed outside the country and it is not an ideal situation for two main reasons:

Tummy Tuck In Kalispell

1) A local surgeon in New York will not necessarily know the details of your surgery abroad which could make medical decision-making much trickier. Furthermore, when patients don’t have a local surgeon readily available to address any postoperative issues immediately, they often wait until a problem gets really bad, oftentimes making the treatment that much more complicated (and potentially with a much worse cosmetic outcome).

2) Looking for care after your mommy lifting will be costly, whether it’s in an office or an emergency room, and oftentimes insurance carriers will not pay for aftercare from cosmetic procedures. A mommy makeover in in Montana is a big procedure, and requires proper postoperative follow up. Plan ahead, both financially and medically, for how you will be followed once you arrive home.
