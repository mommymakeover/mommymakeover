A mommy makeover in Nashville, TN is usually a combination of a tummy tuck, breast surgery (breast lift, breast augmentation sometimes a breast reduction) and usually some liposuction.

The term mommy makeover has been coined because it is usually this combination that women who have had children need to get their pre-pregnancy figure back.

Of course you can opt for any part of this combination if that is all you want or need. Keep in mind though that a full tummy tuck should not be performed until you have finished having all the children that you want.
