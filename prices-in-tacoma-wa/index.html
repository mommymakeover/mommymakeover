What is included in a mommy make-over in Tacoma, WA

The term ‘mommy makeover’ in Tacoma, WA is a generalized term that refers to multiple procedures (generally a breast procedure and a body procedure) done at a single surgery setting.

It is named mommy makeover since traditionally, women who have had children have seen dramatic changes in their breasts and body after childbirth.

The breast procedure can be any of the following: breast augmentation, breast lift, breast reduction or a combination of breast augmentation with lift. The body procedure can be any of the following: liposuction, tummy tuck, tummy tuck with liposuction.

Generally, these procedures are done at a single surgical setting. However, in a few cases, staging the procedure is the safest way for the patient. Be sure to discuss this with your board certified plastic surgeon.
