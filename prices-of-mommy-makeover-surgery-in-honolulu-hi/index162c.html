Prices Of Mommy Makeover Surgery In Honolulu, HI

A Mommy Makeover In Honolulu may be different for each patient. Although the surgery most often involves the breasts and abdomen, it may include other procedures as well. Pregnancy affects the body differently in each person.

Some gain or lose weight in the thighs, hips and buttocks so for them the mommy lift may include liposuction but perhaps not something on the abdomen or breasts if these are still in good condition. For the breasts there is often loss of volume after breast feeding and the woman may only need breast implants.

For others there is significant sagging of the breasts as well and that person may need some type of breast lift. It is certainly possible that you may only need a breast augmentation and not a lift or mastopexy at the same time.

It is best to consult with your plastic surgeon to determine what procedures will be best for you. The total number of procedures is often limited by time of operation.

I try not to operate for over six hours on any patient for cosmetic surgery mommy makeover since this increases the risk of serious complications.

A mommy makeover includes a tummy tuck, I advise my patients to finish having children before having a tummy tuck that includes tightening of the abdominal muscle, however, it is safe to have just excess skin removed.
