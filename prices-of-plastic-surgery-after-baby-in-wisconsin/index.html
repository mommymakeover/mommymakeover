Prices Of Plastic Surgery After Baby In Wisconsin

“Mommy Makeover” in Wisconsin is just the popular name for a combination of procedures that are common to women after having finished with their pregnancies.

It usually includes breast and abdominal surgery but may also include additional liposuction the thighs or even procedure to improve the appearance of the butt.

You can certainly have these done separately so there’s no need to feel pressured.

The only advantage to doing them all together is that you only have one recovery and often you can save some money by having multiple procedures done at the same time.

Depending upon how much is being done, the type of facility and Any other health issue it may be safer to separate them.

The basic components of a mommy makeover are a tummy tuck plus minus liposuction and a breast augemntation or lift or both.

Certainly you can spearate these out and do just what you want without losing anything if you do the rest later. It just requires multiple recoveries.

Tummy Tuck and Breast Augmentation Together

Both Tummy Tuck and Breast Augmentation can be performed together. When doing these operations together, it limits your recovery and anesthesia exposure to one time.

One of my patients had these procedures performed at separate times, and felt that the recovery from the tummy tuck was easier than her breast augmentation.

She is however, a size 0 and had little skin to remove. If you need to do the procedures at separate times, the outcome of one, should not affect the other. Do the one that bothers you the most.

The classic ptoiss classificationis as follows:

  1. Grade 0 – nipple above the inframammary fold (IMF) or breast crease,
  2. Grade I – nipple ias at the breast crease,
  3. Grade II – nipple is below the breast crease but not pointing down, and
  4. Grade III – the nipple is well below the crease and pointing down.
