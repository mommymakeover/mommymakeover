Sonal Patel, Ph.D.

Name: Sonal Patel, Ph.D.
Position: Practice Manager
Years experience: 12
Biography: Dr. Sonal Patel, received her Ph.D. in Molecular Biology from Loyola University Chicago Stritch School of Medicine in 1999. She then went on to do a post-doctoral fellowship at the University of Illinois at Chicago. Her medical research career was focused on analyzing the changes that occur at the cellular and DNA level during oncogenesis or tumor formation. Her love for science and desire to make an immediate positive impact in the lives of others eventually led her to establish Radiance Skincare and Laser Medspa in 2006. She now uses her doctorate in Molecular Biology to stay abreast of the latest aesthetic rejuvenation and anti-aging sciences. Her mission is to research and offer only services that are scientifically sound, clinically-proven and results-oriented. Dr. Patel is passionate about helping her clients look and feel confident at any age. For the last 12 years, she has taken great pride in customizing treatment plans for new clients that give them amazing outcomes.
