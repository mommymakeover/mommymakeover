Mommy Makeover help loose skin on the breasts and abdomen

A mommy makeover addresses loose skin that has resulted from pregnancy. Typically a mommy makeover involves a tummy tuck and breast augmentation.

The tummy tuck removes the excess loose skin and stretch marks from the lower abdomen and tightens the abdominal muscles.

A breast augmentation will help fill in the loose skin and lift your breast to where they once were. Sometimes a breast lift is performed in combination with a breast enlargement or instead of it.

You should consult with a board certified plastic surgeon to learn more. (Ankit Desai, MD, Jacksonville Plastic Surgeon)

Mommy makeover perfect for sagging skin

A mommy makeover is a combination of procedures that includes recontouring of the breast and abdomen. The abdomen can be addressed with a tummy tuck, a mini tummy tuck, or liposuction.

If you have sagging skin, you would be an excellent candidate for a mini tummy tuck or a regular tummy tuck. Consult a plastic surgeon that as well versed in abdominal contouring so that you can receive an evaluation to see which would be best for you. (Pat Pazmino, MD, Miami Plastic Surgeon)

Mommy makeover after pregnancy

An abdominoplasty will remove the excess skin and fat from the abdomen after pregnancy. If you are also concerned about breast sagging, you may want to consider a mommy makeover that incorporates an additional breast lift or implant. (Raffy Karamanoukian, MD, FACS, Los Angeles Plastic Surgeon)

Mommy Makeover in Raleigh after pregnancy to get rid of loose skin

Mommy Makeover in Raleigh is a group of body contouring cosmetic surgery procedures that help women regain they body after a pregnancy. This usually includes a tummy tuck +/- liposuction, a breast lift +/- implants, and liposuction of thighs and other less common procedures.

For a woman who has lost most of her weight and is now left with only loose skin, these procedures can really make a differences. They can be done at once or separately. We prefer to not do more than two or three procedures at the same time. (Martin Jugenburg, MD, Toronto Plastic Surgeon)

Eliminating loose skin after pregnancy

Some of the loose skin that results from the stretching of the abdomen during pregnancy may shrink back on its own, but often there is some that needs to be corrected with surgery. There are a number of different types of abdominoplasty procedures.

Your plastic surgeon should discuss all the possibilities and as a team you should decide which option to choose. (Olivia Hutchinson, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)
