Jabal Uffelman, MD

Name: Jabal Uffelman, MD
Statement: About Uffelman
Jabal Uffelman, MD, is a board-certified gynecologist with additional certifications in anti-aging and aesthetic medicine. He’s the primary physician at Rejuvchip, an aesthetics and wellness center with locations in Fort Lauderdale and Doral, Florida, and the Brickell neighborhood of Miami, Florida, as well as a location in Fort Lee, New Jersey.

Dr. Uffelman speaks English, Spanish, and Portuguese to better serve his diverse clientele across South Florida. He attended the University of Florida College of Medicine in Gainesville, Florida, where he earned his medical degree.

Dr. Uffelman then completed his residency at the University of Miami Jackson Memorial Hospital in Miami, Florida, before completing additional coursework in the topics of anti-aging and aesthetic medicine later in his career.

Dr. Uffelman’s skills include vaginal and laparoscopic surgery. He also specializes in minimally invasive women’s sexual wellness treatments like Rejuvshot, Rejuvstitch, and FemiLift™ and provides Rejuvwave, a treatment for men’s sexual wellness.

At Rejuvchip, Dr. Uffelman also offers pellet hormone therapy using only natural hormones as well as IV vitamin therapy with Rejuvfuel. For people in need of a boost in self-confidence, he provides facial aesthetic treatments like Botox® and high-quality dermal fillers.

Dr. Uffelman enjoys educating patients about their wellness and helping them reach realistic goals related to their sexual health, hormones, and the way they look. He welcomes new and returning patients to Rejuvchip with a free, one-on-one consultation
