Jessica Ballentine

Name: Jessica Ballentine
Position: Business Owner
Years experience: 18
Biography: Jessica is a licensed medical esthetician with over 16 years in the industry. She began her path towards beauty wellness in the San Juan mountains of Telluride after struggling for the first time with her own skin issues, at the age of 21. Her goal was to create a more integrative and holistic approach to beauty and wellness; for mind, body and beauty. She is striving to change the industries “quick-fix” approach to skin care based on addressing the symptom, and neglecting to get to the root-cause of skin issues.

Jessica’s passion to incorporate nutrition, well-being and sustainability for the environment, as well as science and medical-based treatments, led her to creating what is now Revival, a boutique-style medical day spa. Wanting to break the mold of a clinical and medical setting, her goal is to create a progressive and positive vibe where clients can feel relaxed and taken care of. She hopes you enjoy your experience as much as she enjoys making noticeable and dramatic change
