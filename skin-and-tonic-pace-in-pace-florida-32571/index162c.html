Statement: Recognized as a highly trained aesthetic physician, board-certified in multiple specialties, Dr. Christopher Croley treats patients at Skin & Tonic in Pace, Florida. “At Skin & Tonic, we know how important it is to be confident in your appearance and comfortable in your skin, which is why we utilize only the safest, most effective aesthetic treatments to ensure you leave our office happier and healthier than when you came to us," says Dr. Croley. "Whether you are dealing with acne, wrinkles, hair loss, or skin texture issues, we offer best-in-class medical aesthetic services to keep you feeling and looking your best. We are experts in identifying the underlying cause of your skin condition and will work tirelessly to develop a treatment plan that works best for you and your lifestyle.”

After earning his medical degree, Dr. Croley completed specialty and subspecialty training in Chicago, Illinois. He completed his residency in Anesthesiology at Rush University Medical Center where he served as Chief Resident and subsequently completed a Critical Care Medicine Fellowship at Northwestern Memorial Hospital.

Dr. Croley is Board Certified in Anesthesiology, Critical Care Medicine, Aesthetics, and Anti-Aging Medicine. Dr. Croley is also an active member of the American Society of Anesthesiologists and has participated in numerous meetings with legislators in Washington, DC to discuss issues important to the specialty of Anesthesiology as well as medical education.

When Dr. Croley isn't at his aesthetic practice treating patients, he serves as the Director of Aesthetics through Empire Medical where he teaches other physicians and healthcare providers the latest techniques in aesthetic and anti-aging medicine.

Is it safe to get Aerolase Neo laser on a scar? Would it help or hurt the scar?
Aerolase Neo Scar Treatment
Aug 15, 2022
The Aerolase Neo would be a great option for hypertrophic scar treatment, especially scars with increased pigment (red in this photo). The Aerolase Neo is approved for treatment on all skin types and for scars such as the one noted in your photo, a series of treatments would be necessary.
Does it really matter which PRP Centrifuge you use for a P-Shot?
P Shot Centrifuge
Aug 15, 2022
I have been a provider of the P Shot procedure for many years and used several centrifuges during that time. In my experience, a double spin system provides the highest quality PRP and patients tend to have the best results when I use this system. It is important that your provider is using a FDA cleared device.
Strange bluish permanent bruise after filler - how do I get rid of it?
Dissolving Lip Filler
Aug 14, 2022
It appears from the photos that filler may still be present. Dissolving filler can be challenging at times. Options for treatment would include another session of dissolving or evaluation by an a medical provider trained in facial ultrasound to evaluate the peri oral area and assess for residual hyaluronic acid filler. With ultrasound, the dissolving agent can be placed directly into the filler rather than just placed throughout the entire area.
