Healing takes one FULL YEAR

Healing takes a year. 70% of swelling is gone by 4 months. 85 % of swelling is gone by 6 months. All swelling is gone by a year. Be patient. This all varies from person to person and some slender in shape people seem quicker but other people take longer.

I have seen cases where swelling gos amazingly quickly and I have seen swelling decrease that is amazing in length. The little lymphatics must heal and move fluid in a normal way.

Swelling After Mommy Makeover

Swelling (edema) six weeks after a mommy makeover is expected, and is normal. It can take several months for all of the edema to subside after a tummy tuck. Additionally, swelling after breast augmentation and/or breast lift is normal as well, and can take a couple months to disappear completely.

If you have concerns, definitely make an appointment with your plastic surgeon to be evaluated.

An Individualized Mommy Makeover Plan For Your Body

Although, it is not unusual to have some residual swelling 6 weeks after a mommy makeover in South Carolina, there are are some potential problems that can cause swelling to persist. These can include fluid accumulation (seroma), or blood accumulation (hematoma), or an infection. Check back with your surgeon to see if your swelling is part of the normal healing process.

Healing time and time for resolution on swelling depends on the exact procedures that were done as part of your mommy makeover.

In addition, it has to do with your activity level and your compliance to compression therapy. Its best to ask your PS about this as he/she is the most familiar with the procedures that were performed.

Full versus Mini Tummy Tuck

I think you will most likely benefit from a full tummy tuck. Normally women with multiple pregnancies will develop skin laxity of the abdomen with muscle separation which can best be addressed with a full tummy tuck.

More takes out more

In general the more or longer the incision the more skin that is able to be taken out. A mini tuck will compromise the amount of skin to be removed.
