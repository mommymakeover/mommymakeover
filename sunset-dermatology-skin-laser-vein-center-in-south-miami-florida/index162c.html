Monica Ponce

Name: Monica Ponce

Biography: Monica Ponce joined Sunset Dermatology in 2011. She graduated from Florida International University with a Bachelor of Science in Biological Science in 2001 and completed her Physician Assistant studies at Miami-Dade College in 2004.
As a dermatologic physician assistant she is trained to diagnose and treat a variety of skin conditions in adults and children including acne, warts, atopic dermatitis, skin cancer and psoriasis. She also performs surgical procedures such as removal of benign lesions (cyst, moles, etc.) and skin cancer excisions. In addition, she has received specialized training in advanced skin rejuvenation including administration of Botox and/or Dysport, dermal filler injections, skin resurfacing procedures and laser hair removal. Monica has special interests in cosmetic lip augmentation, sclerotherapy to remove spider veins and allergy patch testing.

She has a special interest in the repair of cosmetic earlobe defects such as torn or elongated pierced earring holes. She takes great pleasure in educating her patients so that they fully understand their condition and treatment plan.

Can I get lip injections (Belotero) whilst breastfeeding my 8-month-old baby?
No
Jun 15, 2021
Hello, we recommend not to get any injectables while you are pregnant or nursing because there has not been sufficient studies to know whether its safe. Also, fillers have not been approved for use during that time.
