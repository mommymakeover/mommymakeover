The Mommy Package Plastic Surgery In Washington

Mommy makeover in Washington = tummy tuck + breast lift and/or implants

Because changes from pregnancy and breast feeding typically affect the abdomen and breasts, a mommy makeover includes a tummy tuck and whatever is needed to restore the breasts to the desired size and shape. Combining them in one surgery makes it a mommy makeover.

It is possible to combine a breast lift, implant exchange, and tummy tuck. Your request for minimal scarring, however, is somewhat problematic.

The breast lift procedure will result in a significant amount of scarring, especially with a reduction in the volume of your implants. I also agree that combining mastopexy and implant exchange is risky, especially if you are a smoker.

You appear to be a good candidate for a combination surgery mommy makeover in Washington if you are in good overall health. A vertical pattern breast lift would create the best shape and a tummy tuck with liposuction of your flanks and lower back would give a nice hourglass figure. Consult with a few plastic surgeons in person for more detailed information and a good examination.

Do I need a breast lift?

From your photos and your comment that you are looking for upper pole fullness, I would strongly suggest going with both a breast lift as well as placement of a sub pectoral breast implant. Breast lifts alone do little to nothing to improve upper pole fullness and, instead focus mostly on tightening the skin envelope, raising the nipple, and reshaping the lower pole.

But when an implant is placed, this implant effectively acts as a structure over which the breast is reshaped and can often improve the upper pole of the breast and give a more refined look to the top of the breast.
