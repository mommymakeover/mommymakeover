You do not have to be a mommy to have any of the procedures included in a Mommy Makeover.

Plastic surgeons refer to it as a Mommy Makeover as a way to promote it to moms who want to restore their pre-baby bodies, but the procedures themselves can be performed on anyone who is a good candidate – procedures such as tummy tuck, breast lift and breast augmentation.

As someone who has undergone massive weight loss, you are probably a good candidate for such procedures. Be sure to meet with a board-certified plastic surgeon to discuss your options. Best wishes!

The American Board of Medical Specialties (ABMS) is the official national overseer of the most competitive, stringent medical boards that provide certification. The American Board of Medicine is one such board under the ABMS.

Dr. Avron Lipschitz Mommy Makeover Before And After Photos Candidate Pictures Result

The American Board of Plastic Surgery is another. There are 23 boards that are recognized. The American Board of Cosmetic Surgery is NOT an ABMS-recognized board.

The American Academy of Cosmetic Physicians probably requires no more than sending in a check for a certificate – that would be my guess. These associations do not have stringent qualifications that must be fulfilled for membership and certification like ABMS-recognized boards. These providers do not train for years in academic programs to learn how to safely and effectively provide cosmetic services.

On account of your significant weight loss, you are likely an excellent candidate for body contouring surgery.
