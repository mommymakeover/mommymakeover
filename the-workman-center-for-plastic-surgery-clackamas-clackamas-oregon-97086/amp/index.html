Doctors

Michael L. Workman, MD

Name: Michael L. Workman, MD
Statement: Dr. Michael L. Workman is a board-certified plastic surgeon with over 28 years in practice. The founder of the Workman Center for Plastic Surgery in Vancouver, Washington, he specializes in breast augmentation, liposuction, and tummy tucks. Dr. Workman performed more than 400 surgeries last year alone, delivering exceptional results and compassionate care. His practice also offers many nonsurgical treatment options for body contouring and facial rejuvenation.

Dr. Workman received his medical degree from the University of Minnesota School of Medicine in Minneapolis, where he also later completed a residency in surgery. He then completed a plastic surgery fellowship at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. Dr. Workman started his private practice in 1993.

He is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, Northwest Society of Plastic Surgeons, Clark County Medical Society, and Oregon Society of Plastic Surgeons.

Dr. Workman frequently guests on Portland-area radio and television shows, sharing his expertise in plastic surgery and cosmetic treatments. He has also been honored as a RealSelf Top Doctor.

Beyond his private practice, Dr. Workman is proud to volunteer in Haiti at a 3,000-square-foot Ambulatory Surgery Center that he built himself. His wife is also an MD, and they have two children and two Yorkies. He is also a nationally ranked slalom water skier, a marathon runner, and an NFL-credentialed photographer covering the Seahawks, including two Super Bowls.
