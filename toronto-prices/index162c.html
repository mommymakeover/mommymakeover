Mommy Tuck Plastic Surgery In Toronto Cost

A mommy makeover in Toronto generally involves abdominal and breast rejuvenation surgery to restore the post-pregnancy body.

This can be done as one procedure vs staged based on patient and surgeon preferences. There are advantages for each.

Single procedure – One anesthetic – One recovery period – One facility fee – Easier scheduling.

Staged procedure – Shorter operating times = lower risk of blood clots, pulmonary complications, or infection – Surgeon focus for each procedure – Easier recovery from each procedure.

In my practice the breast procedure and the abdominoplasty would be done as separate procedures within a couple of days of each other.

This serves the patient almost every advantage with the only real disadvantage being more complicated scheduling.

I have a certified in office operating suite and an additional facility fee is not passed onto the patient.

Without this, I would probably have to do a single procedure for cost considerations. Read the post on the link provided for further details.

Preparation for Mommy Makeover in Toronto

Healthy diet and exercise is the best way to prepare for a mommy makeover. It is best if you could be close to your ideal weight.

If you are in great physical shape before surgery, you will heal more quickly and have an easier recovery.

Complete Mommy Makeover At The Plastic Surgery Clinic

The best way to prepare for your mommy lift is to follow the instructions given to you by your surgeon. Eat a healthy diet, take recommended supplements and avoid all aspirin products.  After surgery continue to follow all instructions for the best possible result.

Staggering mommy makeover procedures is not necessarily the best idea.

Separating the two operations by six weeks should be no problem. However, to shorten the convalescence you might want to consider having these procedures done at the same time.

If you have a tummy tuck,you can have a breast lift with implants six weeks later. You may also combine the procedures into one, if your surgeon thinks this is a good idea for you. Always see a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon.

What you are describing is a mommy makeover and is done at the same time.  If you   Have already had surgery, been waiting about six weeks to three months is a good time to have another surgery.

Affordable Mommy Makeover With Michael Weinberg, MD

These two procedures are often combined when carrying out ‘mommy makeovers in Toronto’. If they are not done together, it is wise to leave some time to allow you to recover from the first surgery.

This can take any where between 6 to 12 weeks depending on the patient. It is a good idea to let tissues settle for 12 weeks before the second stage as if there are adjustments to made after the first surgery, this can be addressed at the second surgery if enough time has been allowed to lapse betweent the two surgeries.

It’s difficult to tell you exactly whether or not you should stage your procedures or how they should be staged without actually seeing you first. When a large implant is desired and a major lift needs to be done, separating these two procedures is very helpful for optimal healing. However, I frequently combine breast augmentations with tummy tucks and breast lifts with tummy tucks. A picture may even be helpful to give you a general idea of how to stage things.

Dr. Cory S. Goldberg Mommy Makeover Surgery

Drains after a tummy tuck are placed under the abdominal skin. The skin has been elevated off the abdominal wall and fluid has a tendency to collect in the space. The drain prevents formation of seroma. Abdominoplasty drains are typically left in place for 1 to 2 weeks.I also like to use a pain pump. This infuses local anesthetic onto the abdominal wall and can help with some early postoperative pain. This is removed within the first week.

Transient Weight Gain Normal Immediately After Surgery

Not to worry, Transient Weight Gain is Normal Immediately After Surgery of any type and especially after a tummy tuck and/or Liposuction. This is due to swelling, also called edema, which is a physiological response to surgery especially those that involve a lot of surface area. Lots of Salt is used during surgery as well in the form of saline by both the surgeon and anesthesiologist which also causes water retention. You will pee this out in the next several days to weeks as your edema resolves. By the way, the weight of your implants will only add about 2 pounds.

Dr Martin Jugenburg Plastic Surgery Mommy Makeover

Weight gain after surgery is normal. Even though tissue has been removed fluid was added during surgery and edema (swelling) occurs as well. This fluid retention can last up to one month. Also reduced activity after surgery can lead to weight gain.

As a general rule i tell my patient not to expect to be below their preop weight for the first 30 days. But at 3 month all healing is done and the goal weight should be reached based on what was done during surgery. So don’t worry about your weight at 10 days.

Is childbirth a pre-requisite for mommy makeover?

The extra weight that you carried during young adulthood stretched out your abdominal muscles and skin much like a pregnancy would do. Same deformity, different cause.

Housekeeping business, mommy makeover

Housekeeping can involve heavy lifting and repetitive tasks. In my practice I would recommend my patients avoid rigorous tasks for at least 4-6 weeks after surgery. Some lighter cleaning activities would likely be fine after 2-3 weeks, depending on your healing and your surgeon’s advice. (William Andrade, MD, Toronto Plastic Surgeon)
