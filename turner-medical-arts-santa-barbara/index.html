Will PRP treatment under the eyes help increase collagen or thicken skin?
PRP for dark circles.
Mar 17, 2019
The injection of PRP to improve the appearance is a safe and somewhat effective procedure in the right hands. It will stimulate collagen production but will need to be repeated. Current thoughts include a gradual improvement with each injection having an improvement over the previous one. Long term it could be better than fat injections but there is no definitive research of which I am aware.
