Virginia Mommy Makeover Packages

The term Mommy Makeover in Virginia state is a broad term that commonly means an abdominal and breast surgery performed at the same time, typically to correct changes that occurred with pregnancy and/or breastfeeding.

The breast procedure can be a breast lift with or without implants, breast augmentation, or a breast reduction. The abdominal procedure can be liposuciton, abdominoplasty, mini abdominoplasty, or a combination. A mommy makeover without a breast procedure is merely an abdominal procedure.
